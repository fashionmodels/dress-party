Mens Suit Shops In London Where To Find The Best Suits

Written by Oliver Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you on the lookout for the perfect suit to wear to your next event, but don't know where to start your search? Finding a suit that fits you well and is made of high-quality materials can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure where to look.

London is home to many mens suit shops, but not all are created equal. Some shops have limited options, while others can be incredibly expensive. It can be challenging to find a store that meets your needs without breaking the bank.

If you're looking for mens suit shops in London, you've come to the right place. This article will provide you with an overview of the best places to shop for suits in London, as well as tips for finding the perfect suit for your needs.

Best Mens Suit Shops in London

One of the most popular mens suit shops in London is Savile Row. This street is known for its high-end tailors and is home to some of the most prestigious menswear brands in the world. Here, you'll find bespoke suits that are tailored to fit you perfectly. However, be prepared to pay a premium price for the privilege.

If you're looking for a more affordable option, try Suit Supply. This store offers a range of suits at different price points without sacrificing quality. The store's staff is knowledgeable and will help you find a suit that fits your body type and style preferences.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Suit

When shopping for a suit, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure the suit fits you well. A suit that is too big or too small will not look good on you, no matter how expensive it is. Pay attention to the fit of the shoulders, chest, and waist.

Second, look for high-quality materials. A suit made from high-quality fabric will not only look better, but it will also last longer. Look for suits made from wool or cashmere blends.

Third, consider the occasion for which you'll be wearing the suit. If you're going to a wedding or other formal event, a traditional suit in a dark color is the best choice. If you're attending a more casual event, you may be able to get away with a lighter color or a suit made from a less formal fabric.

How to Care for Your Suit

Once you've found the perfect suit, it's important to take care of it properly. Always hang your suit on a hanger to prevent wrinkles. If your suit does get wrinkled, use a steamer or iron on a low heat setting to remove them. Dry clean your suit only when necessary, as excessive dry cleaning can damage the fabric.

Where to Find Deals on Mens Suits in London

If you're looking for a great deal on a suit, there are a few places to check out. Department stores like Selfridges and Harrods often have sales on suits, especially during the holiday season. You can also find deals at outlet stores like Bicester Village, which has a range of designer stores offering discounts on their products.

Conclusion of Mens Suit Shops in London

When it comes to finding the perfect suit in London, there are many options available. Whether you're looking for a bespoke suit or a more affordable option, there's a store in London that will meet your needs. Keep these tips in mind when shopping for a suit, and you're sure to find one that fits you perfectly and looks great.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best place to shop for a bespoke suit in London?
A: Savile Row is known for its high-end tailors and is the best place to find a bespoke suit in London.

Q: What should I look for when shopping for a suit?
A: Pay attention to the fit of the suit, the quality of the materials, and the occasion for which you'll be wearing the suit.

Q: How should I care for my suit?
A: Always hang your suit on a hanger, steam or iron out wrinkles, and dry clean only when necessary.

Q: Where can I find deals on suits in London?
