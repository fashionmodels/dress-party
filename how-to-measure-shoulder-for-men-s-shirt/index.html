What Does it Mean to Measure Shoulder for Men's Shirt?

Measuring shoulder width is an important step when it comes to choosing the right size and fit for a men's shirt. The shoulder measurement is taken from the outer edge of one shoulder to the outer edge of the other shoulder. It is important to get an accurate measurement because it will help determine the correct shirt size and ensure a comfortable fit.

When I first started shopping for shirts, I didn't pay much attention to the shoulder measurement. I would just try on different sizes until I found one that felt comfortable. However, I soon learned that this was not the best approach. By taking the time to measure my shoulders, I was able to find shirts that fit me perfectly and looked great on me.

How to Measure Shoulder for Men's Shirt: Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you understand why measuring your shoulder is important, let's go over how to do it. Here is a step-by-step guide to measuring your shoulder width for a men's shirt:

Step 1: Stand Up Straight

Stand up straight with your arms at your sides. Make sure you are standing in a relaxed position with your shoulders down and your back straight.

Step 2: Find the Bony Part of Your Shoulder

Locate the bony part of your shoulder. This is the part of your shoulder that protrudes the most and is closest to your neck. It is usually located at the end of your collarbone.

Step 3: Measure from Shoulder to Shoulder

Using a tape measure, place one end at the bony part of your left shoulder and measure across to the bony part of your right shoulder. Make sure the tape is level and straight across your back. Record this measurement in inches.

Step 4: Repeat for Accuracy

Repeat steps 2 and 3 to ensure accuracy. Take two or three measurements and calculate the average to get the most accurate measurement possible.

How to Use Your Shoulder Measurement to Find the Right Shirt Size

Now that you have your shoulder measurement, you can use it to find the right shirt size. Most shirt manufacturers provide a size chart that lists the measurements for each size. Look for a size that matches your shoulder measurement. If your shoulder measurement falls between two sizes, choose the larger size for a more comfortable fit.

It is important to note that different brands may have different size charts, so be sure to check the size chart for each brand you are considering.

How to Adjust for Different Styles

Keep in mind that different styles of shirts may have different fits. For example, a slim-fit shirt may have narrower shoulders than a regular-fit shirt. If you are shopping for a specific style, be sure to check the size chart for that style and adjust your measurement accordingly.

How to Measure Shoulder for a Custom-Made Shirt

If you are getting a shirt custom-made, be sure to provide your shoulder measurement to the tailor. This will ensure that the shirt is made to fit your unique body shape.
