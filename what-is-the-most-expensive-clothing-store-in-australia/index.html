The most expensive clothing store in Australia is the iconic Harrolds. This luxury department store has been in operation for over 30 years and has become synonymous with high-end fashion. Harrolds offers a wide range of luxurious brands, such as Gucci, Balenciaga, and Tom Ford, to name a few.
