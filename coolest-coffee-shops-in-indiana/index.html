The Coolest Coffee Shops In Indiana A Guide To The Best Brews

Written by Daniel Oct 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old coffee chain and looking for a unique coffee experience? Look no further than the coolest coffee shops in Indiana. From cozy atmospheres to artisanal brews, Indiana has no shortage of amazing coffee shops to choose from. Whether you're a local or just passing through, you won't want to miss out on these top spots.

If you're wondering where to find the best coffee in Indiana, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll explore the coolest coffee shops in Indiana, what makes them special, and why you should check them out.

Indiana's coffee scene is thriving, and there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking for a place to work or catch up with friends, you'll find a coffee shop that fits your needs. From artisanal roasts to cozy atmospheres, these coffee shops are the best of the best in Indiana.

The Thirsty Scholar

The Thirsty Scholar, located in Muncie, is a popular spot for students and locals alike. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, with plenty of seating for studying or catching up with friends. The coffee is top-notch, with a wide variety of flavors to choose from. The Thirsty Scholar also offers a unique menu of food items, including vegan and gluten-free options.

Quills Coffee

Quills Coffee, located in Indianapolis, is a must-visit for coffee enthusiasts. The atmosphere is modern and sleek, with plenty of seating and natural light. The coffee is artisanal and carefully crafted, with a wide variety of flavors and roasts to choose from. Quills also offers a selection of pastries and food items to enjoy with your coffee.

Monon Coffee Co.

Monon Coffee Co., located in Carmel, is a hidden gem in the Indianapolis area. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, with plenty of seating and a friendly staff. The coffee is artisanal and carefully crafted, with a focus on sustainability and fair trade. Monon Coffee Co. also offers a selection of baked goods and breakfast items.

Strange Brew Coffeehouse

Strange Brew Coffeehouse, located in Greenwood, is a unique spot that's not to be missed. The atmosphere is cozy and eclectic, with plenty of seating and a friendly staff. The coffee is artisanal and carefully crafted, with a wide variety of flavors and roasts to choose from. Strange Brew Coffeehouse also offers a selection of food items, including vegan and gluten-free options.
