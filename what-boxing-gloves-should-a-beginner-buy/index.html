Boxing Gloves for Beginners: A Comprehensive Guide on What to Buy

Are you new to boxing and wondering what gloves to buy? Choosing the right gloves can be a daunting task, especially for beginners who have no prior experience. It can be overwhelming to sift through the numerous options available on the market. However, it is crucial to invest in a good pair of boxing gloves as they play a significant role in protecting your hands and ensuring a safe practice session.

As a beginner, you might be facing several pain points when it comes to selecting gloves. You may not know what to look for, what size to buy, or even how much to spend. But don't worry, in this article, I will guide you on what boxing gloves to buy as a beginner.

The answer to this question depends on several factors such as your weight, hand size, and the type of training you will be doing. As a beginner, it is essential to choose gloves that provide enough protection to your hands and wrists. It is also important to choose gloves that fit your hands comfortably.

First, let's start with the size. Boxing gloves come in different sizes, ranging from 8oz to 20oz. As a beginner, it is recommended to choose gloves that are at least 12oz or higher. The reason being that they provide more padding, which in turn offers better hand protection. Moreover, heavier gloves make it easier to practice and develop the right technique without causing any serious injuries.

Types of Boxing Gloves for Beginners

When it comes to selecting gloves, you can choose from various types such as bag gloves, sparring gloves, and training gloves. Each type serves a different purpose, and as a beginner, it is essential to choose the right one.

Bag gloves are designed for punching bags, mitts, and pads. They are lighter and have less padding compared to other gloves. Sparring gloves, on the other hand, are designed for sparring with a partner. They have more padding and are heavier to provide better protection. Training gloves are multipurpose gloves that can be used for both bag work and sparring. They offer a balance between padding and weight.

Material and Quality

Another important factor to consider when buying gloves is the material and quality. Most gloves are made of synthetic leather or genuine leather. Synthetic leather is cheaper and easier to maintain, but it is less durable compared to genuine leather. Genuine leather, on the other hand, is more expensive but offers better quality and durability.

The quality of gloves is also important as it determines how long they will last. Investing in good quality gloves may seem costly, but it will be worth it in the long run. Moreover, good quality gloves provide better protection to your hands and are less likely to cause any injuries.

Price Range

Price is also an important factor to consider when buying gloves. As a beginner, it is not necessary to spend a lot of money on gloves. However, it is also important not to compromise on quality for the sake of saving money. You can find good quality gloves within the price range of $30 to $100.

How to Choose the Right Gloves?

Choosing the right gloves can be overwhelming, but the following tips can help you make the right decision:

  • Consider the type of training you will be doing.
  • Determine the size of gloves based on your weight and hand size.
  • Choose gloves made of good quality material.
  • Set a budget and choose gloves within that range.

Conclusion

Choosing the right gloves is essential for any beginner in boxing. It is important to consider factors such as size, type, material, quality, and price before making a purchase. By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can make an informed decision and choose gloves that provide enough protection and comfort during your training sessions.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use bag gloves for sparring?

A: No, bag gloves are not suitable for sparring as they offer less padding and protection. It is recommended to use sparring gloves for sparring sessions.

Q: What size gloves should I buy?

A: The size of gloves depends on your weight and hand size. As a beginner, it is recommended to choose gloves that are at least 12oz or higher.

Q: Should I choose synthetic leather or genuine leather gloves?

A: It depends on your preference and budget. Synthetic leather gloves are cheaper and easier to maintain, while genuine leather gloves are more expensive but offer better quality and durability.

Q: What is the price range for boxing gloves?

A: You can find good quality gloves within the price range of $30 to $100.

Conclusion of What Boxing Gloves Should a Beginner Buy

Choosing the right gloves is crucial for any beginner in boxing. By considering factors such as size, type, material, quality, and price, you can make an informed decision and choose gloves that provide enough protection and comfort during your training sessions. Remember to choose gloves that fit your hands comfortably and provide enough padding to protect your hands and wrists.
