Selling Shoes Online

If you're interested in selling your shoes online, there are several things to keep in mind. First, take high-quality photos of your shoes and write detailed descriptions that accurately reflect their condition. This will help you attract more buyers and get the best possible price for your shoes.

If you're interested in selling your shoes in person, there are several things to keep in mind. First, make sure your shoes are clean and in good condition. Consignment shops and buy/sell/trade stores will typically only accept shoes that are in excellent condition.
