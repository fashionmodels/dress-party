Stay Cool And Protected Best Summer Riding Jacket Australia

Written by Oliver Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer weather in Australia can be brutal, but that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice protection while riding your motorcycle. Many riders struggle to find the perfect balance between staying cool and staying safe with the right summer riding jacket.

If you're a rider who is looking for a summer riding jacket that provides both comfort and protection, then you've come to the right place. In this post, we will discuss the best summer riding jacket Australia and what makes them stand out from the rest.

When it comes to finding the right summer riding jacket, you want to make sure that you're investing in a product that will keep you safe from any potential accidents while also keeping you cool in hot weather. The best summer riding jacket should have features like good ventilation, lightweight material, and moisture-wicking technology to keep you comfortable and dry.

In summary, the best summer riding jacket Australia should be lightweight, breathable, and offer superior protection. It should be designed to keep you cool in hot weather and offer features that make riding in the summer more comfortable.

Alpinestars T-GP R Air Jacket

One of the best summer riding jackets available in Australia is the Alpinestars T-GP R Air Jacket. This jacket is designed with a 450D polyester shell that is both lightweight and durable. The T-GP R Air Jacket also comes equipped with a mesh lining that provides excellent ventilation, making it a perfect choice for hot summer rides.

I personally have been using this jacket for the past year, and I must say that it has exceeded my expectations. The jacket is not only comfortable on hot days, but it also provides great protection with its CE certified elbow and shoulder protectors.

Dainese Super Rider D-Dry Jacket

Another great option for a summer riding jacket is the Dainese Super Rider D-Dry Jacket. This jacket is designed with a high-resistance fabric that is both breathable and waterproof, making it a great choice for riders who may encounter unpredictable weather conditions.

One of the standout features of this jacket is its removable thermal liner, which can be taken out during hot weather to provide maximum ventilation. The jacket also comes with CE certified elbow and shoulder protectors, as well as a pocket for a back protector.

How to Choose the Right Summer Riding Jacket

When it comes to choosing the right summer riding jacket, there are a few key features that you should look for. Firstly, make sure the jacket has good ventilation to keep you cool in hot weather. Secondly, look for lightweight materials that won't weigh you down while riding. Finally, make sure the jacket has adequate protection with features like CE certified armor.

Tips for Caring for Your Summer Riding Jacket

To ensure that your summer riding jacket lasts as long as possible, it's important to take care of it properly. Be sure to follow the manufacturer's care instructions, which may include washing the jacket in cold water and hanging it to dry. Avoid using fabric softeners or bleach, as these can damage the material of the jacket.

Conclusion of Best Summer Riding Jacket Australia

When it comes to finding the best summer riding jacket Australia, it's important to invest in a product that will keep you both comfortable and safe on your rides. Look for jackets that have good ventilation, lightweight materials, and adequate protection to ensure that you're getting the most out of your investment.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I wear a leather jacket in the summer?

A: Leather jackets are not the best choice for summer riding as they can be heavy and hot. Look for jackets that are made with lightweight materials and have good ventilation.

Q: How do I know if a jacket is good for hot weather?

A: Look for jackets that are designed with mesh or perforated panels for ventilation. Jackets that have removable liners or are made with lightweight materials are also good options.

Q: What size should I order for my summer riding jacket?

A: Be sure to refer to the manufacturer's sizing chart to ensure that you're ordering the correct size. If you're between sizes, it's usually best to order a size up to ensure a comfortable fit.

Q: How often should I wash my summer riding jacket?

A: Follow the manufacturer's care instructions for your jacket. Generally, you should wash your jacket after every few rides or when it becomes visibly dirty.
