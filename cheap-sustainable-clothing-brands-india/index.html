Cheap Sustainable Clothing Brands India Where To Find Them

Written by Aprilia Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of fast fashion brands that are not only expensive but also harm our environment? Do you want to switch to sustainable clothing but don't know where to start? Look no further as we have compiled a list of cheap sustainable clothing brands in India that will not only make you look good but also make you feel good about your choices.

If you are someone who cares about the environment and wants to reduce their carbon footprint, sustainable clothing is the way to go. Sustainable clothing brands use eco-friendly fabrics like organic cotton, hemp, bamboo, and recycled materials to make their products. These fabrics are not only safer for the environment but also for the people who work in the factories.

The target of this article is to provide you with a list of cheap sustainable clothing brands in India that you can choose from. We will also give you an insight into what makes these brands sustainable and why you should consider them.

In summary, this article will provide you with a guide on where to find cheap sustainable clothing brands in India. We will also explain what makes these brands sustainable and why you should choose them.

Cheap Sustainable Clothing Brands India: Fabindia

I have been a loyal customer of Fabindia for many years now. Not only do they have a wide range of sustainable clothing, but they also have home decor and accessories. Fabindia works with artisans in rural areas to create sustainable products that are not only environmentally friendly but also support the local communities.

Cheap Sustainable Clothing Brands India: No Nasties

No Nasties is a brand that focuses on creating sustainable clothing using 100% organic cotton. They have a wide range of products ranging from t-shirts to dresses, all made using organic cotton. No Nasties also has a transparent supply chain and ensures that their workers are paid fair wages.

What Makes a Clothing Brand Sustainable?

A clothing brand is sustainable if it uses eco-friendly fabrics, has a transparent supply chain, and ensures that their workers are paid fair wages. Sustainable clothing brands also focus on reducing waste and minimizing their carbon footprint.

How to Choose Sustainable Clothing Brands?

When choosing sustainable clothing brands, look for brands that use eco-friendly fabrics like organic cotton, hemp, bamboo, and recycled materials. Also, check if the brand has a transparent supply chain and ensures that their workers are paid fair wages. Lastly, look for brands that focus on reducing waste and minimizing their carbon footprint.

Cheap Sustainable Clothing Brands India: Bhu:Sattva

Bhu:Sattva is a brand that creates sustainable clothing using organic fabrics like cotton, linen, and hemp. They have a wide range of products for both men and women, including dresses, tops, and pants. Bhu:Sattva also ensures that their workers are paid fair wages and focuses on reducing waste.

Cheap Sustainable Clothing Brands India: Ethicus

Ethicus is a brand that creates sustainable clothing using eco-friendly fabrics like organic cotton and silk. They have a wide range of products, including sarees and dresses. Ethicus also works with weavers in rural areas to create sustainable products and ensures that their workers are paid fair wages.

Question and Answer

Q1. Are sustainable clothing brands more expensive than fast fashion brands?

A1. Not necessarily. While some sustainable clothing brands can be expensive, there are also many affordable options available in the market.

Q2. Is sustainable clothing good for the environment?

A2. Yes, sustainable clothing is good for the environment as it uses eco-friendly fabrics and reduces waste.

Q3. How can I check if a brand is sustainable?

A3. Look for brands that use eco-friendly fabrics, have a transparent supply chain, and ensure that their workers are paid fair wages. Also, check if the brand focuses on reducing waste and minimizing their carbon footprint.

Q4. Can sustainable clothing be stylish?

A4. Yes, sustainable clothing can be stylish. Many sustainable clothing brands focus on creating trendy and fashionable clothes that are also eco-friendly.

Conclusion of Cheap Sustainable Clothing Brands India

In conclusion, there are many cheap sustainable clothing brands in India that you can choose from. These brands use eco-friendly fabrics, have a transparent supply chain, and ensure that their workers are paid fair wages. By choosing sustainable clothing, you not only make a fashion statement but also contribute to a better and safer environment.
