The Role of Pay-Per-View

One of the main reasons why boxing matches can generate so much revenue is pay-per-view. Pay-per-view allows fans to watch the fight from the comfort of their own home for a fee. This means that millions of people can watch the fight, and the revenue generated can be massive.

Pay-per-view has become an integral part of the boxing business, and it has allowed fighters to earn massive amounts of money. The highest earning boxing matches in history have all been heavily reliant on pay-per-view revenue.

Boxers get paid through a combination of methods. They receive a guaranteed purse, which is a set amount of money that they will earn regardless of the outcome of the fight. They also receive a percentage of the pay-per-view revenue, which can be a significant amount of money.
