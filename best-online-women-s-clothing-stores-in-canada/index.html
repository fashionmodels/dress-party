Best Online Women s Clothing Stores In Canada A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Fit

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of scouring through endless racks of clothes at your local department store, only to leave empty-handed? Do you struggle to find clothes that fit your body type and personal style? Shopping for women's clothing can be a daunting task, but with the rise of online shopping, it's easier than ever to find your perfect fit.

When it comes to online shopping for women's clothing in Canada, the options can seem overwhelming. With so many stores to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. That's why we've put together a guide to the best online women's clothing stores in Canada to help you find the perfect pieces for your wardrobe.

In this guide, we'll cover everything from affordable fast fashion to high-end designer brands. We'll also touch on the importance of sustainable fashion and highlight some stores that prioritize ethical and eco-friendly practices. Whether you're looking for the latest trends or timeless classics, we've got you covered.

1. Aritzia

As a Canadian-based retailer, Aritzia has become a go-to destination for women's fashion. With a focus on quality fabrics and timeless designs, Aritzia offers a range of clothing options for all body types and styles. From cozy sweaters to chic blazers, Aritzia has something for everyone. Plus, their website offers easy-to-use filters that allow you to search by size, color, style, and more.

Personally, I love shopping at Aritzia because their pieces are versatile and can be dressed up or down. I also appreciate their commitment to sustainability and ethical practices, such as their use of eco-friendly materials and their partnership with One Tree Planted to plant trees for every online order.

2. Simons

Simons is a Quebec-based retailer that has been around since 1840, making it one of the oldest fashion companies in North America. Known for their affordable prices and trendy designs, Simons offers a wide range of clothing options for women, including their own in-house brand and collaborations with other designers.

One of the things I love about shopping at Simons is their commitment to inclusivity. They offer clothing options in a range of sizes, including plus sizes, and they also have a gender-neutral collection called "Fabrique 1840". Additionally, Simons has made efforts to become more sustainable by using eco-friendly materials and reducing their carbon footprint.

3. Frank and Oak

If you're looking for sustainable and ethical fashion, Frank and Oak is a great option. This Montreal-based retailer focuses on creating timeless pieces that are made to last, using eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton and recycled polyester. They also prioritize ethical practices, such as partnering with factories that pay fair wages and provide safe working conditions.

Personally, I appreciate Frank and Oak's commitment to transparency and their efforts to reduce waste. They offer a program called "Style Plan" that allows you to receive personalized monthly outfit recommendations based on your preferences, and you only pay for what you keep. This helps reduce the amount of clothing waste that ends up in landfills and promotes a more sustainable approach to fashion.

4. Nordstrom

If you're looking for designer fashion, Nordstrom is a great option. While they are not a Canadian-based retailer, they offer free shipping to Canada and have a wide range of designer brands available. From high-end fashion houses like Gucci and Prada to more affordable luxury brands like Kate Spade and Tory Burch, Nordstrom has something for every budget.

One thing I appreciate about shopping at Nordstrom is their customer service. They offer free returns and exchanges, and their website has a feature that allows you to chat with a stylist for personalized recommendations. This makes it easier to navigate the world of designer fashion and find pieces that fit your personal style.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are these stores inclusive of all body types?

A: Yes, all of these stores offer a range of sizes and styles to fit all body types.

Q: Do any of these stores prioritize sustainable and ethical fashion?

A: Yes, both Frank and Oak and Aritzia prioritize sustainable and ethical fashion practices.

Q: Do these stores offer free shipping to Canada?

A: Yes, all of these stores offer free shipping to Canada, with the exception of Nordstrom, which offers free shipping on orders over a certain amount.

Q: What if I don't like something I ordered online?

A: All of these stores offer free returns and exchanges, making it easy to shop with confidence.

Conclusion of Best Online Women's Clothing Stores in Canada

Overall, there are plenty of great online women's clothing stores in Canada to choose from. Whether you're looking for affordable fast fashion or high-end designer brands, there's something for every budget and style. By keeping in mind the importance of sustainable and ethical fashion practices, you can shop with confidence and know that you're making a positive impact on the environment and the fashion industry as a whole.
