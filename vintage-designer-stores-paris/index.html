Vintage Designer Stores Paris A Guide To Finding The Best Deals

Written by Frank Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Paris is known for its fashion scene, and for those who love vintage designer fashion, it is a paradise. However, it can be overwhelming to navigate the many vintage designer stores Paris has to offer. In this guide, we will help you find the best deals and hidden gems.

If you're looking for vintage designer clothes, bags, shoes or accessories, you might find it difficult to choose the right store. It can be hard to know where to go, what to look for, and how to find the best deals. Moreover, many stores can be expensive, and it can be challenging to find affordable vintage designer pieces.

The target of this guide is to help you find the best vintage designer stores Paris has to offer. We will give you tips on how to navigate the stores, find the best deals, and what to look for in vintage designer fashion.

Finding Hidden Gems

One of the best things about vintage designer fashion is finding hidden gems. When you enter a vintage designer store, it can be overwhelming to see all the different items. However, if you take your time and look through the racks, you can find unique items that no one else has.

One of my favorite vintage designer stores Paris has to offer is Mamie Blue. It is located in the Marais district and has a fantastic collection of vintage designer clothes and accessories. I found a beautiful Yves Saint Laurent blouse there for a fraction of the price it would have cost me new.

Finding Affordable Prices

If you're on a budget, you might think that vintage designer stores are not for you. However, there are many stores in Paris that offer affordable vintage designer pieces. One of the best stores for affordable vintage designer fashion is Kiliwatch. They have a large selection of clothes, shoes, and accessories from different eras and at different price points.

What to Look for in Vintage Designer Fashion

When shopping for vintage designer fashion, it is essential to know what to look for. Firstly, check the quality of the item. Look for any signs of wear and tear, holes, or stains. Secondly, check the labels and logos of the item. Make sure they are authentic and not copies. Finally, try on the item to make sure it fits well.

How to Care for Vintage Designer Fashion

Once you have found your perfect vintage designer piece, it is essential to take good care of it. Firstly, read the care instructions on the label. If there are no instructions, wash the item gently by hand in cold water. Avoid using harsh chemicals, and do not tumble dry. Secondly, store the item correctly. Keep it away from direct sunlight, and do not hang it on a hanger for too long, as this can stretch the fabric.

Finding Vintage Designer Stores Online

If you cannot make it to Paris, or if you prefer to shop online, there are many vintage designer stores available. One of the best online stores for vintage designer fashion is Vestiaire Collective. They have a large selection of vintage designer clothes, bags, shoes, and accessories, and you can find great deals.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Are vintage designer stores in Paris expensive?

A: It depends on the store. Some stores can be expensive, while others offer affordable prices. It is essential to do your research and compare prices before making a purchase.

Q: How can I tell if a vintage designer item is authentic?

A: Look for labels and logos that are authentic. Do your research on the brand and the item to make sure it is genuine. If in doubt, ask the sales assistant for more information.

Q: Can I haggle the price in vintage designer stores?

A: It depends on the store. Some stores allow haggling, while others do not. It is best to ask the sales assistant if they are open to negotiation.

Q: How often do vintage designer stores in Paris restock their inventory?

A: It varies from store to store. Some stores restock their inventory weekly, while others do it monthly. It is best to ask the sales assistant for more information.

Conclusion of Vintage Designer Stores Paris

Paris is a fashion lover's paradise, and vintage designer stores are hidden gems waiting to be discovered. With the tips in this guide, you can navigate the stores and find the best deals on vintage designer clothes, bags, shoes, and accessories. Whether you're in Paris or shopping online, vintage designer fashion is an excellent way to add unique pieces to your wardrobe.
