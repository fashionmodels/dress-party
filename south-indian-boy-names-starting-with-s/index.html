Meaningful South Indian Boy Names Starting with S

Here are some unique and meaningful south Indian boy names starting with S:

1. Sanjay

The name Sanjay means "victorious" in Sanskrit. It is a popular name in South India and is often used to denote a triumph over adversity.

2. Sathvik

The name Sathvik means "pure" in Sanskrit. It is a beautiful name that signifies innocence and goodness.

3. Shreyas

The name Shreyas means "superior" or "best" in Sanskrit. It is a popular name in South India and is often used to denote excellence.

4. Sujith

The name Sujith means "bright" or "ray of light" in Sanskrit. It is a beautiful name that signifies positivity and hope.

These are just a few examples of south Indian boy names starting with S. There are many more options available, and you can choose the one that resonates with you the most.
