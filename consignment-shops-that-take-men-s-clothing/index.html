How To Find Consignment Shops That Take Men s Clothing

Written by Petter Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having a closet full of clothes you don't wear anymore? Do you want to make some extra cash by selling your gently used clothing? Consignment shops are a great option, but finding ones that take men's clothing can be a challenge.

It can be frustrating to spend time and effort sorting through your wardrobe, only to find out that the shop you selected does not accept men's clothing. This can be especially difficult if you're looking for a specific type of store, such as a high-end consignment shop or a store that specializes in vintage clothing.

Fortunately, there are many consignment shops that take men's clothing. In this article, we'll explore how to find these shops, what to look for in a consignment shop, and how to get the most out of your consignment experience.

Local Consignment Shops

One of the best ways to find consignment shops that take men's clothing is to start with local shops in your area. You can use search engines like Google or Bing to search for consignment shops in your area. You can also check your local phone book or ask friends and family for recommendations.

When you find a local consignment shop that accepts men's clothing, take the time to visit the store and see what they have to offer. This will give you an idea of the type of clothing they accept, the quality of the items they sell, and the prices they charge.

Online Consignment Shops

Another option for finding consignment shops that take men's clothing is to look for online consignment shops. These shops allow you to sell your gently used clothing without leaving your home. Some popular online consignment shops that accept men's clothing include:

  • ThredUp
  • The RealReal
  • Poshmark
  • Vestiaire Collective

These online consignment shops typically take a commission on the items you sell, but they can be a great option if you don't have a local consignment shop in your area.

What to Look for in a Consignment Shop

When choosing a consignment shop to sell your men's clothing, there are a few things to look for. First, make sure the shop accepts the type of clothing you have to sell. Some consignment shops only accept high-end clothing, while others accept a wider range of items.

Next, consider the commission rate the shop charges. This is the percentage of the sale price that the shop keeps as their fee. It's important to choose a shop with a reasonable commission rate, so you can get the most money for your clothing.

You should also look for a consignment shop with a good reputation. Check online reviews and ask for recommendations from friends and family to ensure you're working with a reputable shop.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Consignment Experience

If you want to get the most money for your men's clothing, there are a few things you can do to maximize your consignment experience. First, make sure your clothing is in good condition. Clothing that is stained, ripped or damaged will not sell well in a consignment shop.

You should also make sure your clothing is clean and pressed. This will make it more appealing to potential buyers and increase the likelihood of a sale.

Finally, be patient. Consignment shops typically sell items over a longer period of time, so it may take a few weeks or even months to sell your clothing. But with a little patience and persistence, you can make some extra cash and clear out your closet at the same time.

Conclusion of Consignment Shops That Take Men's Clothing

Finding consignment shops that take men's clothing can be a challenge, but with a little research and patience, you can find the right shop for your needs. Whether you choose a local consignment shop or an online consignment shop, make sure you choose a reputable shop with a good commission rate and a strong reputation. By following these tips, you can maximize your consignment experience and make some extra cash in the process.

Question and Answer

Q: How much money can I expect to make by selling my men's clothing at a consignment shop?
A: The amount of money you can make by selling your men's clothing at a consignment shop depends on a variety of factors, including the quality of the items, the demand for the items, and the commission rate charged by the shop.

Q: Do consignment shops only accept high-end men's clothing?
A: No, consignment shops accept a wide range of men's clothing, from high-end designer clothing to more affordable items.

Q: Can I sell vintage men's clothing at a consignment shop?
A: Yes, many consignment shops specialize in vintage clothing and accept vintage men's clothing.

Q: How long does it take for a consignment shop to sell my men's clothing?
A: Consignment shops typically sell items over a longer period of time, so it may take a few weeks or even months to sell your clothing.
