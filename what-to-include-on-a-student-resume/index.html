What To Include On A Student Resume A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a student looking to create a resume but not sure what to include? You're not alone. Crafting a resume can be a daunting task, especially if it's your first time. However, having a well-written resume can make a significant difference when it comes to landing your dream job or internship. In this guide, we'll break down everything you need to know about what to include on a student resume.

As a student, you may feel like you don't have much to add to your resume. However, this is far from the truth. Even if you don't have much work experience, there are still plenty of things you can include on your resume to make it stand out. The key is to focus on your skills, achievements, and education.

First and foremost, your resume should include your contact information, such as your name, phone number, email address, and mailing address. This information should be prominently displayed at the top of your resume. Additionally, you should include a professional summary that highlights your strengths and what you can bring to the table.

When it comes to your education, make sure to include your GPA and any relevant coursework. You can also mention any academic honors or awards you've received. If you've taken any relevant classes or workshops, be sure to include them as well.

Your skills are another important aspect to include on your student resume. This can include both hard and soft skills. Hard skills are specific skills that you've acquired through education or experience, such as proficiency in a particular software program. Soft skills, on the other hand, are more general skills, such as communication or teamwork.

When it comes to work experience, don't worry if you don't have much to add. You can still include any part-time jobs or internships you've had, as well as any volunteer work or extracurricular activities. Be sure to highlight any achievements or responsibilities you had in these roles.

Highlight Your Extracurricular Activities

Extracurricular activities are a great way to show off your skills and interests. If you've been involved in any clubs or organizations, be sure to include them on your resume. This can include anything from sports teams to debate clubs to volunteer organizations.

For example, if you were a member of your school's debate club, you could highlight your communication and critical thinking skills. If you were a member of a sports team, you could highlight your teamwork and leadership abilities.

Showcase Your Projects and Portfolio

If you've completed any projects or have a portfolio of your work, be sure to include them on your resume. This can be a great way to showcase your skills and creativity. For example, if you're a graphic design student, you could include a link to your online portfolio.

Customize Your Resume for Each Job

One of the most important things to keep in mind when creating a student resume is to customize it for each job you apply for. This means tailoring your resume to highlight the skills and experiences that are most relevant to the job you're applying for.

Proofread Your Resume

Finally, be sure to proofread your resume before submitting it. Grammatical errors and typos can make a negative impression on potential employers. You can even ask a friend or mentor to review your resume for you.

Conclusion of What to Include on a Student Resume

Creating a student resume can be a challenge, but by following these tips, you'll be well on your way to crafting a strong and effective resume. Remember to focus on your skills, achievements, and education, and customize your resume for each job you apply for. With a little bit of effort and attention to detail, you'll be able to create a resume that stands out from the crowd.

Question and Answer

Q: What if I don't have any work experience?

A: That's okay. You can still include any volunteer work or extracurricular activities you've been involved in. Focus on highlighting your skills and achievements in these roles.

Q: Should I include my high school on my resume?

A: If you're currently in college, you can leave off your high school information. However, if you're a recent high school graduate, you can include your high school information and any relevant coursework or achievements.

Q: Is it okay to use a template for my resume?

A: Yes, using a template can be a helpful way to get started. Just make sure to customize it to highlight your unique skills and experiences.

Q: How long should my resume be?

A: As a student, your resume should ideally be one page. However, if you have a lot of relevant experience, it's okay to go onto a second page.
