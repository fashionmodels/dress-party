What Is The Salary Of An Interior Designer In India

Written by Petter Oct 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an interior designer in India? Do you want to know how much you can earn as an interior designer? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the salary of an interior designer in India.

As an interior designer, your salary depends on various factors such as your experience, location, job profile, and employer. Typically, the starting salary for a fresher in this field ranges from INR 15,000 to INR 25,000 per month. However, as you gain experience and expertise, your salary can go up to INR 1,00,000 or more per month.

In India, the average salary of an interior designer is around INR 5,00,000 per annum. However, this can vary depending on the factors mentioned above. For instance, if you work in a metropolitan city like Mumbai or Delhi, you can expect a higher salary as compared to other cities. Similarly, if you work for a reputed firm or have your own business, your salary can be higher.

To summarize, the salary of an interior designer in India can range from INR 15,000 to INR 1,00,000 or more per month, depending on various factors. The average salary in this field is around INR 5,00,000 per annum.

What Determines the Salary of an Interior Designer in India?

When it comes to determining the salary of an interior designer in India, there are several factors that come into play. Let's take a look at some of them.

Experience:

Experience is one of the most important factors that determine the salary of an interior designer in India. As you gain more experience in this field, your skills and expertise will also improve. This will make you more valuable to your employer, and you may get a higher salary.

Education:

While a degree in interior designing is not mandatory to become an interior designer, it can certainly help. If you have a degree or certification from a reputed institute, it can add value to your profile and help you get a higher salary.

My Personal Experience

As an interior designer with 5 years of experience, I can tell you that your salary can vary greatly depending on your location and job profile. When I started my career, I worked for a small firm in a Tier II city. My starting salary was INR 20,000 per month. However, as I gained more experience, I moved to a bigger city and joined a reputed firm. Here, my salary was INR 75,000 per month. Now that I have my own business, my income has increased significantly.

How to Increase Your Salary as an Interior Designer in India?

If you want to increase your salary as an interior designer in India, here are some tips that can help:

Get Certified:

As mentioned earlier, having a degree or certification in interior designing can help you get a higher salary. So, if you haven't already, consider getting certified from a reputed institute.

Upgrade Your Skills:

Interior designing is a field that is constantly evolving. To stay relevant and valuable to your employer, you need to upgrade your skills and knowledge regularly. Attend workshops, seminars, and conferences to stay updated with the latest trends and techniques.

Question and Answer

Q: Is interior designing a lucrative career in India?

A: Yes, interior designing can be a lucrative career in India, especially if you have the right skills and expertise.

Q: What is the average salary of an interior designer in Mumbai?

A: The average salary of an interior designer in Mumbai is around INR 6,00,000 per annum.

Q: Can you become an interior designer without a degree?

A: Yes, you can become an interior designer without a degree. However, having a degree or certification can help you get a higher salary and increase your job opportunities.

Q: Is it necessary to work for a firm to become a successful interior designer?

A: No, it is not necessary to work for a firm to become a successful interior designer. You can also start your own business and work as a freelancer.

Conclusion of What is the Salary of an Interior Designer in India

In conclusion, the salary of an interior designer in India can vary depending on various factors such as experience, location, job profile, and employer. While the starting salary may not be very high, as you gain more experience and expertise, your salary can increase significantly. To increase your salary, you need to upgrade your skills, get certified, and stay updated with the latest trends and techniques in this field.
