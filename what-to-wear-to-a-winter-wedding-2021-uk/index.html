What To Wear To A Winter Wedding 2021 Uk A Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Winter weddings in the UK can be a beautiful and magical affair, but they can also pose a challenge when it comes to deciding what to wear. With the chilly weather and potential snowfall, it's important to dress warmly while still looking stylish and elegant. In this guide, we'll provide you with some tips and ideas for what to wear to a winter wedding 2021 UK.

Attending a wedding can be stressful enough, but when you add in the factor of dressing appropriately for the winter season, it can become even more daunting. You want to look your best, but also need to stay warm and comfortable throughout the day. Not to mention, you don't want to be under or overdressed compared to the other guests.

When it comes to dressing for a winter wedding, it's all about layering and choosing the right fabrics. You'll want to opt for warm materials such as wool, cashmere, or velvet. It's also important to choose appropriate footwear such as boots or closed-toe heels to keep your feet warm and dry.

In summary, when deciding what to wear to a winter wedding 2021 UK, keep in mind the following tips: choose warm fabrics, layer appropriately, and opt for closed-toe footwear.

Formal Winter Wedding Attire

For a formal winter wedding, you'll want to dress in a long gown or a dressy suit. A floor-length velvet dress or a sequin gown would be a great choice for a winter wedding. For men, a dark suit with a tie or bowtie would be appropriate. Make sure to accessorize with statement jewelry or a scarf to add some extra warmth to your outfit.

Semi-Formal Winter Wedding Attire

If the dress code for a winter wedding is semi-formal, you can opt for a cocktail dress or a dressy skirt and top. For men, a suit in a lighter color such as grey or navy would be appropriate. Adding a blazer or a scarf can add some extra warmth and style to your outfit.

Choosing the Right Colors

When it comes to choosing the right colors for a winter wedding, you'll want to opt for darker and richer hues. Deep reds, emerald greens, and navy blues are all great choices. You can also add some metallic accents such as gold or silver to add some extra sparkle to your outfit.

How to Accessorize

Accessories can really make or break an outfit, and this is especially true for winter weddings. You'll want to choose statement jewelry such as a necklace or earrings to add some extra pizzazz to your outfit. A clutch or a small purse is also a great choice for carrying your essentials while still looking chic.

Personal Experience

Last year, I attended a winter wedding for a close friend in the UK. I opted for a long-sleeved velvet dress in a deep red hue. I accessorized with gold statement earrings and a matching clutch. I paired the dress with closed-toe heels to keep my feet warm throughout the day. I received many compliments on my outfit and felt both stylish and comfortable throughout the wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a coat to a winter wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a coat to a winter wedding. It's best to choose a coat that complements your outfit, such as a faux fur coat or a long wool coat.

Q: Can I wear a jumpsuit to a winter wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear a jumpsuit to a winter wedding. Opt for a dressy jumpsuit in a darker color such as black or navy, and accessorize with statement jewelry.

Q: Can I wear tights to a winter wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear tights to a winter wedding. Choose opaque tights in a dark color such as black or navy to stay warm and stylish.

Q: Can I wear open-toe shoes to a winter wedding?

A: While open-toe shoes can be stylish, they may not be the best choice for a winter wedding. Opt for closed-toe heels or boots instead to keep your feet warm and dry.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Winter Wedding 2021 UK

Attending a winter wedding in the UK can be a beautiful and magical experience, but it's important to dress appropriately for the season. By choosing warm fabrics, layering appropriately, and opting for closed-toe footwear, you can stay warm and stylish throughout the day. Remember to choose appropriate colors, accessorize with statement pieces, and have fun with your outfit.
