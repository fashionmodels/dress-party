Question and Answer

Q: What size gloves should I choose?

A: The size of gloves you choose will depend on your personal preferences and the type of training or sparring you'll be doing. As a general rule, most people choose gloves that are between 12 and 16 ounces.

Q: How do I know if my gloves fit properly?

A: Your gloves should fit snugly, but not be too tight. You should be able to make a fist comfortably, without any excess material around your fingers. If you're unsure about the fit of your gloves, try them on and make a few punches to see how they feel.

Q: Can I use the same gloves for sparring and bag work?

A: It's generally not recommended to use the same gloves for sparring and bag work. Sparring gloves are designed to provide extra padding and protection for your partner, while bag gloves are designed to offer more support and durability for heavy bag work.

Q: How often should I replace my gloves?

A: It's recommended to replace your gloves every 6-12 months, depending on how frequently you use them. Over time, the material in your gloves can break down and become less effective at providing protection and support.
