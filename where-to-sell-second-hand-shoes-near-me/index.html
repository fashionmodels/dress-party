Where To Sell Second Hand Shoes Near Me

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have a pile of shoes that you no longer wear? Are you tired of staring at them every day and wondering what to do with them? Whether you want to make some extra cash or just declutter your wardrobe, selling your second-hand shoes is a great solution. However, finding the right place to sell them can be a hassle.

If you're on the hunt for where to sell second-hand shoes near me, there are several options available. You can sell them online or offline, through consignment stores or marketplaces. Each option has its advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to consider your needs before making a decision.

Here are some of the best places to sell your second-hand shoes:

Local Consignment Stores

Consignment stores are a great option for those who want to sell their shoes locally. These stores will sell your shoes for you and take a cut of the profit. Some consignment stores specialize in high-end shoes or designer brands, while others accept a wider range of styles and brands.

Personal Experience: I once sold a pair of barely-worn boots at a local consignment store and made a decent profit. The store was located in a trendy neighborhood, and my boots fit right in with the store's aesthetic.

Online Marketplaces

Online marketplaces like eBay, Poshmark, and Depop are popular options for selling second-hand shoes. These platforms allow you to create a listing for your shoes, set a price, and ship them directly to the buyer. Some marketplaces also have a local pickup option for those who want to sell their shoes locally.

Personal Experience: I've sold several pairs of shoes on Poshmark and have had a positive experience overall. The platform is easy to use, and I've been able to sell shoes quickly and for a fair price.

Social Media

Social media platforms like Facebook Marketplace and Instagram are also great options for selling second-hand shoes. You can create a post or story showcasing your shoes and connect with potential buyers in your area. This option is especially useful if you have a large following or belong to a local buy-and-sell group.

Personal Experience: I've sold shoes on Facebook Marketplace before and have found it to be a convenient option. I was able to connect with a buyer in my area and make a quick sale without having to deal with shipping.

Garage Sales

If you're looking to sell more than just shoes, a garage sale might be a good option for you. You can set up a table with your shoes and other items you want to sell and attract buyers in your neighborhood. This option requires more effort than the others on this list, but it can be a fun way to declutter and make some extra cash.

Personal Experience: I've participated in several garage sales over the years and have always enjoyed the experience. It's a great way to connect with your neighbors and sell items in person.

Question and Answer:

Q: How much can I expect to make from selling my second-hand shoes?

A: The amount you can make from selling your second-hand shoes depends on several factors, including the brand, condition, and demand for the shoes. High-end designer shoes will typically sell for more than fast fashion shoes. Shoes in good condition will also sell for more than shoes with visible wear and tear.

Q: How do I know what price to set for my shoes?

A: Researching the prices of similar shoes on the platform or store you plan to sell on can give you an idea of what price to set. You can also consider the original price of the shoes, the condition, and the demand when setting a price.

Q: Should I clean my shoes before selling them?

A: Yes, cleaning your shoes before selling them can increase their value and make them more appealing to buyers. Be sure to clean them according to the material and follow any care instructions provided by the manufacturer.

Q: What should I do if my shoes don't sell?

A: If your shoes don't sell after a certain period, consider lowering the price or trying a different platform or store. You can also donate them to a local charity or thrift store.

Conclusion of Where to Sell Second Hand Shoes Near Me

Whether you choose to sell your second-hand shoes online or offline, there are several options available to you. Local consignment stores, online marketplaces, social media, and garage sales are all great options for selling your shoes and making some extra cash. Consider your needs and the condition of your shoes before choosing the best option for you.
