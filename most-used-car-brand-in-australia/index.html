Discovering The Most Used Car Brand In Australia

Written by Aprilia Oct 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you planning to buy a car in Australia? Are you wondering which car brand is the most popular in the country? You're not alone. Choosing the right car can be a daunting task, especially when you're not familiar with the market. In this article, we'll explore the most used car brand in Australia and why Australians prefer it.

If you're looking for a reliable, affordable, and fuel-efficient car, you might be interested in knowing the most used car brand in Australia. With so many options available in the market, it's easy to get overwhelmed and confused. However, by understanding the preferences and habits of Australian car buyers, you can make an informed decision and avoid costly mistakes.

The most used car brand in Australia is Toyota. According to the latest industry reports, Toyota has been the best-selling car brand in the country for several years in a row. In 2020, Toyota sold over 204,000 vehicles in Australia, which represents a market share of 19.4%. Other popular car brands in Australia include Mazda, Hyundai, Ford, and Mitsubishi, but none of them come close to Toyota's dominance.

In summary, Toyota is the most used car brand in Australia, and for a good reason. Australians appreciate Toyota's quality, reliability, safety, and resale value. Additionally, Toyota offers a wide range of models, from small cars to SUVs and hybrids, to cater to different needs and budgets. If you're looking for a car that can handle the Australian roads, climate, and lifestyle, Toyota is a safe bet.

Why Aussies Love Toyota

My personal experience with Toyota has been nothing but positive. I bought my first Toyota in 2015, and I've been a loyal customer ever since. I currently own a Toyota Corolla, and I'm very satisfied with its performance, features, and efficiency. What I love about Toyota is its attention to detail and commitment to quality. Toyota cars are built to last, and they're designed to meet the highest standards of safety, comfort, and convenience.

Toyota's Best-Selling Models in Australia

Toyota's popularity in Australia is due to its diverse and versatile product portfolio. Here are some of Toyota's best-selling models in the country:

  • Toyota Hilux: a reliable and rugged pickup truck that can handle tough terrains and heavy loads
  • Toyota Corolla: a popular and practical small car that offers great value for money and fuel efficiency
  • Toyota RAV4: a stylish and comfortable SUV that combines performance, safety, and technology
  • Toyota Camry: a spacious and refined sedan that delivers a smooth and responsive ride
  • Toyota Land Cruiser: a legendary off-road vehicle that is known for its strength, durability, and versatility

The Benefits of Buying a Toyota in Australia

Aside from its reputation and popularity, buying a Toyota in Australia comes with several advantages, such as:

  • Great resale value: Toyota cars hold their value well over time, which means you can get a good return on your investment if you decide to sell or trade-in your vehicle
  • Low maintenance costs: Toyota cars are known for their reliability and durability, which means you can save money on repairs and maintenance in the long run
  • Advanced safety features: Toyota cars come with advanced safety features, such as airbags, anti-lock brakes, electronic stability control, and collision avoidance technology, to keep you and your passengers safe on the road
  • Eco-friendly options: Toyota offers a range of hybrid and electric models that are eco-friendly and fuel-efficient, which can help you save money on gas and reduce your carbon footprint

How to Choose the Right Toyota for You

If you've decided to buy a Toyota in Australia, the next step is to choose the right model and trim level that suits your needs and preferences. Here are some factors to consider:

  • Budget: Toyota offers cars at different price points, so you need to determine how much you're willing to spend and what features are essential for you
  • Usage: Depending on your lifestyle and driving habits, you might need a car that is suitable for long trips, daily commutes, or outdoor adventures
  • Features: Toyota cars come with a variety of features, such as infotainment systems, navigation, leather seats, sunroofs, and more. Decide which features are important for you and which ones you can do without
  • Test drive: Before making a final decision, it's essential to test drive the car and see how it feels on the road. Pay attention to the steering, brakes, acceleration, and comfort level

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes Toyota the most used car brand in Australia?

A: Toyota's popularity in Australia is due to its reputation for quality, reliability, safety, and resale value. Additionally, Toyota offers a diverse and versatile product portfolio that caters to different needs and budgets.

Q: Are Toyota cars expensive in Australia?

A: Toyota cars are priced competitively in Australia, and they offer great value for money. Depending on the model and trim level, you can find a Toyota car that fits your budget and lifestyle.

A: While Toyota is the most used car brand in Australia, there are other reliable and reputable car brands in the country, such as Mazda, Hyundai, Ford, and Mitsubishi. It's essential to do your research and compare different options before making a purchase.

Q: Can I buy a Toyota car online in Australia?

A: Yes, you can buy a Toyota car online in Australia through the official Toyota website or authorized dealerships. You can browse through different models, compare prices, and customize your car according to your preferences.

Conclusion of Most Used Car Brand in Australia

In conclusion, Toyota is the most used car brand in Australia, and for a good reason. Australians trust Toyota's quality, reliability, safety, and resale value, and Toyota offers a wide range of models to cater to different needs and budgets. If you're in the market for a new or used car, consider buying a Toyota, and you won't be disappointed.
