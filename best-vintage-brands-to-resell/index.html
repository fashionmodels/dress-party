Best Vintage Brands To Resell A Guide For Fashion Enthusiasts

Written by Oliver Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of vintage fashion? Do you love scouring thrift stores and estate sales for hidden gems? If so, you may be wondering which vintage brands are the best to resell for a profit. In this guide, we'll discuss the top vintage brands that are in high demand among collectors and fashion enthusiasts alike.

If you're new to the world of vintage reselling, it can be overwhelming to navigate the vast array of brands and styles out there. It's important to understand the market and which brands are currently in demand to ensure that you're able to sell your finds for a profit.

So, which vintage brands should you be on the lookout for? Let's dive in.

Levi's

When it comes to vintage denim, Levi's is the king. The brand's classic 501 jeans from the 1950s and 60s are highly coveted by collectors and can fetch a high price. Look for jeans with a small "E" on the red tab, as these are the most sought-after. Other Levi's pieces, such as denim jackets and overalls, are also popular among vintage enthusiasts.

Ralph Lauren

Ralph Lauren is known for his timeless preppy style, and his vintage pieces are no exception. Look for classic polo shirts, button-downs, and blazers with the signature Ralph Lauren crest. The brand's vintage pieces are highly sought-after and can often fetch a high price.

Chanel

Chanel is a brand that needs no introduction. The iconic fashion house is known for its timeless designs and impeccable craftsmanship. Vintage Chanel pieces, such as tweed jackets and quilted handbags, are highly coveted among fashion enthusiasts and can fetch a high price. Keep an eye out for the brand's signature interlocking "CC" logo.

Tommy Hilfiger

Tommy Hilfiger is another preppy brand that has stood the test of time. Vintage Tommy Hilfiger pieces, such as polo shirts and windbreakers, are highly sought-after and can fetch a high price. Look for pieces with the signature red, white, and blue color scheme and the brand's iconic flag logo.

Lacoste

Lacoste is known for its classic polo shirts, which have been a staple in preppy fashion for decades. Vintage Lacoste pieces in good condition can fetch a high price, especially if they have the brand's iconic crocodile logo.

Question and Answer Section:

Q: How can I tell if a vintage piece is truly authentic?
A: Look for any tags or labels that indicate the brand and the year the piece was made. You can also do research online to compare your piece to authentic vintage pieces from the same brand and time period.

Q: What should I look for when inspecting a vintage piece for resale?
A: Look for any signs of wear or damage, as well as the overall condition of the piece. Make sure all buttons and zippers are intact and in working order. Also, consider the rarity and demand of the piece when determining its resale value.

Q: Where can I sell my vintage pieces?
A: There are a variety of online marketplaces, such as eBay and Etsy, that are great for selling vintage pieces. You can also sell your pieces at flea markets and vintage fairs, or even start your own online vintage shop.

Q: Are there any vintage brands that are currently trending?
A: Yes, vintage sportswear brands such as Champion, Fila, and Nike are currently in high demand among fashion enthusiasts. Vintage band t-shirts and concert merchandise are also popular among collectors.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Brands to Resell

When it comes to vintage reselling, it's important to do your research and understand which brands are currently in demand. By keeping an eye out for vintage pieces from brands such as Levi's, Ralph Lauren, Chanel, Tommy Hilfiger, and Lacoste, you can build a profitable vintage collection that appeals to collectors and fashion enthusiasts alike.
