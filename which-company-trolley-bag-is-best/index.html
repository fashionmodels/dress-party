Which Company Trolley Bag Is Best A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Traveling can be a hassle, especially when it comes to packing. Choosing the right trolley bag can make all the difference to your travel experience. However, with so many companies and models to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which company trolley bag is best for you. In this guide, we will explore the top companies and models to help you make an informed decision.

Pain Points

Choosing the wrong trolley bag can result in a lot of frustration and discomfort during your travels. Heavy bags, broken wheels, and inadequate storage space are common issues travelers face. Additionally, investing in a low-quality bag can lead to frequent replacements and added expenses.

Answer

After researching and testing various trolley bags, we have found that Samsonite offers the best quality and value for money. Samsonite has been a leading brand in the luggage industry for over 100 years and offers a wide range of trolley bags to suit different needs and preferences.

Summary

In this guide, we have established that choosing the right trolley bag is crucial for a comfortable travel experience. We have also discussed the pain points of selecting the wrong bag and identified Samsonite as the best company trolley bag on the market. Let's take a closer look at why Samsonite is the ideal choice.

Samsonite Trolley Bags

I have personally used Samsonite trolley bags for years and have never been disappointed. Their bags are durable, lightweight, and designed with the traveler in mind. The Samsonite Omni PC Hardside Spinner is a top pick for its scratch-resistant exterior, ample storage space, and 360-degree spinner wheels for effortless maneuvering.

Away Trolley Bags

Away is a newer brand that has gained popularity in recent years for its sleek and minimalist designs. Their bags are manufactured with high-quality materials and come with a lifetime warranty. The Away Carry-On is a favorite among frequent travelers for its compact size and convenient charging capabilities.

Briggs & Riley Trolley Bags

Briggs & Riley is another reputable brand that offers trolley bags with a lifetime warranty. Their bags are designed with practicality and organization in mind, featuring multiple compartments and pockets. The Briggs & Riley Baseline Expandable Spinner is a great option for its expandable feature, allowing for additional packing space.

How to Choose the Best Company Trolley Bag

When selecting a trolley bag, consider your travel needs and preferences. Think about the size and weight of the bag, the type of wheels, and the material. Look for features such as expandable storage, TSA-approved locks, and waterproof exteriors. It's also important to invest in a bag from a reputable company with a warranty to ensure long-term use.

Rimowa Trolley Bags

Rimowa is a German brand that is known for its high-end and luxurious trolley bags. Their bags are crafted with premium materials and feature unique design elements. The Rimowa Classic Cabin Spinner is a top pick for its iconic look and durability.

Question and Answer

Q: What size trolley bag should I choose?

A: This depends on the length of your trip and your personal preference. A carry-on sized bag is ideal for shorter trips or if you prefer to travel light. However, if you need to pack more items, a larger bag may be necessary.

Q: Are four wheels or two wheels better for a trolley bag?

A: Four wheels, also known as spinner wheels, are generally more convenient as they allow for easy maneuvering in any direction. However, two-wheeled bags may be more durable and suitable for rough terrain.

Q: Can I use a trolley bag as a checked-in bag?

A: Yes, most trolley bags are suitable for both carry-on and checked-in luggage. However, be sure to check the size and weight restrictions of your airline before traveling.

Q: What is the average lifespan of a trolley bag?

A: This depends on the quality of the bag and how frequently it is used. A high-quality trolley bag from a reputable brand can last for several years, while a low-quality bag may need to be replaced after just a few trips.

Conclusion of Which Company Trolley Bag is Best

Choosing the right trolley bag is essential for a smooth and comfortable travel experience. By considering your needs and preferences, investing in a high-quality bag from a reputable company, and taking care of it properly, you can ensure that your trolley bag will last for many trips to come. Samsonite, Away, Briggs & Riley, and Rimowa are all top companies that offer excellent trolley bag options to suit different budgets and styles. Happy travels!
