How To Measure A Man s Neck For A Dress Shirt

Written by Bardi Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you frustrated with buying dress shirts that don't fit your neck properly? Do you find it difficult to measure your neck for a dress shirt? Look no further, as we have got you covered. Here, we will guide you on how to measure a man's neck for a dress shirt in a simple and easy way.

One of the most common problems men face while purchasing dress shirts is finding the right neck size. Wearing a shirt that is too tight around the neck can be uncomfortable and make it difficult to breathe, while a shirt that is too loose can look sloppy and unprofessional. That's why it's important to know how to measure your neck size accurately.

How to Measure a Man's Neck for a Dress Shirt

To measure your neck size, you will need a measuring tape. Start by standing straight with your shoulders relaxed and your head up. Wrap the measuring tape around your neck, just below your Adam's apple. Make sure that the tape is snug but not too tight. You should be able to fit two fingers comfortably between the tape and your neck.

Take note of the measurement in inches. This is your neck size, which you can use to determine the correct size for your dress shirt.

Why is it Important to Measure Your Neck Size?

Measuring your neck size is important when purchasing dress shirts as it ensures a comfortable fit. A properly fitting dress shirt will not only look better, but it will also allow you to move freely and remain comfortable throughout the day.

Moreover, a dress shirt with the right neck size will complement your overall appearance, making you look confident and professional.

Factors to Consider When Measuring Your Neck Size

When measuring your neck size, there are a few factors to keep in mind. Firstly, it's important to stand straight with your shoulders relaxed and your head up. This will ensure that you get an accurate measurement. Additionally, make sure that the measuring tape is snug but not too tight. You should be able to fit two fingers between the tape and your neck.

How to Convert Neck Size to Dress Shirt Size

Once you have measured your neck size, you can determine your dress shirt size. Most dress shirts are sized based on neck size and sleeve length. To convert your neck size to dress shirt size, refer to a sizing chart. Generally, a dress shirt size is equal to your neck size plus one or two inches. For example, if your neck size is 16 inches, your dress shirt size will be 16.5 or 17 inches.

Common Mistakes to Avoid When Measuring Your Neck

One of the most common mistakes men make when measuring their neck size is pulling the tape too tightly. This can lead to an inaccurate measurement and a shirt that is too tight and uncomfortable to wear. Similarly, pulling the tape too loosely can result in a loose-fitting shirt that looks sloppy and unprofessional.

Another mistake to avoid is tilting your head forward or backward while measuring your neck. This can result in an incorrect measurement, as the tape will not be wrapped around the neck properly.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I measure my neck size?

A: It's recommended to measure your neck size at least once a year, as your neck size can change due to weight gain or loss.

Q: Is it better to go for a shirt with a slightly bigger neck size?

A: No, it's always better to go for a shirt with a neck size that fits well. A shirt that is too loose around the neck can look unsightly and unprofessional.

Q: What if my neck size falls between two sizes?

A: In this case, it's recommended to go for the larger size, as you can always get the shirt tailored for a better fit.

Q: Can I measure my neck size without a measuring tape?

A: No, a measuring tape is necessary to get an accurate neck size measurement.

Conclusion of How to Measure a Man's Neck for a Dress Shirt

Measuring your neck size accurately is crucial to finding the right dress shirt size. By following the steps outlined above, you can measure your neck size with ease. Remember to stand straight, keep your head up, and use a measuring tape that is snug but not too tight. With the right dress shirt size, you can look confident, professional, and stylish.
