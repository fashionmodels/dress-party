Question and Answer

1. Can I find Indian mens clothing in Western countries?

Yes, you can find Indian mens clothing in Western countries. Many Indian mens clothing shops have branches in Western countries, and you can also find Indian clothing on e-commerce websites.

2. What is the difference between traditional and modern Indian mens clothing?

Traditional Indian mens clothing includes sherwanis, kurtas, and dhotis, while modern Indian mens clothing includes suits, jackets, and trousers. Traditional clothing is typically worn for weddings and other traditional events, while modern clothing is suitable for formal and casual occasions.

3. What is the average price range of Indian mens clothing?

The price range of Indian mens clothing varies depending on the brand and the type of clothing. Traditional clothing can range from INR 1000 to INR 50,000, while modern clothing can range from INR 500 to INR 20,000.

4. Can I bargain at Indian mens clothing shops?

Bargaining is a common practice in India, and you can bargain at Indian mens clothing shops. However, it is essential to be respectful and not bargain excessively.
