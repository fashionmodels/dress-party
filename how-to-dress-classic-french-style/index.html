How To Dress Classic French Style A Relaxed English Tutorial

Written by Aprilia Sep 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of feeling like you have nothing to wear? Do you want to look effortlessly chic and polished like the French? Dressing in classic French style can seem intimidating, but with a few key pieces and some styling tips, you can achieve the timeless and sophisticated look you desire.

Many people struggle with how to dress classic French style because they think it requires a lot of money or a complete wardrobe overhaul. However, this is not the case. With a few simple adjustments to your current wardrobe, you can achieve the French-inspired look you desire.

To dress in classic French style, start by investing in timeless pieces such as a striped shirt, a tailored blazer, and a trench coat. These pieces will never go out of style and can be worn year after year. Pair these items with well-fitting jeans or trousers, and you have the foundation for a classic French outfit. From there, add a silk scarf, a pair of ballet flats, and statement jewelry to complete the look.

In summary, to achieve classic French style, invest in timeless pieces, focus on fit, and accessorize with statement pieces. Keep your color palette simple and neutral, and don't be afraid to mix and match patterns.

Invest in Timeless Pieces

When it comes to dressing in classic French style, quality over quantity is key. Invest in pieces that are well-made and will last for years to come. A classic trench coat, black blazer, and striped shirt are all great investment pieces that will never go out of style. You can mix and match these items to create a variety of outfits, making them a worthwhile investment.

Personally, I invested in a high-quality trench coat that I wear year after year. It instantly elevates any outfit and adds a touch of sophistication. I also have a collection of striped shirts in various colors and styles that I wear with jeans, trousers, and skirts. These pieces are versatile and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Focus on Fit

One of the hallmarks of French style is impeccable fit. Clothes that fit well will instantly make you look more put together and polished. Take the time to find pieces that fit your body type and have them tailored if necessary. Avoid clothes that are too tight or too loose, as they can detract from the overall look.

Personally, I always make sure to try on clothes before purchasing them. I also have a go-to tailor who I trust to make adjustments as needed. This attention to fit ensures that my clothes look and feel great, which is essential for achieving classic French style.

Accessorize with Statement Pieces

To add a touch of personality to your classic French outfit, accessorize with statement pieces. A silk scarf, bold necklace, or colorful handbag can instantly elevate an outfit and make it your own. However, it's important to remember that less is more when it comes to accessorizing. Stick to one or two statement pieces per outfit to avoid looking overdone.

Personally, I love to accessorize with scarves. They are versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, from tied around the neck to worn as a headband. I also have a collection of statement necklaces that I wear to add a pop of color to my outfits.

Mix and Match Patterns

French style is all about mixing and matching patterns. Don't be afraid to pair a striped shirt with a floral skirt, or a polka dot blouse with plaid trousers. The key is to stick to a simple color palette and let the patterns do the talking. When in doubt, stick to classic patterns such as stripes, polka dots, and plaid.

Personally, I love to mix and match patterns. I often pair a striped shirt with a blazer and plaid trousers for a classic French-inspired look. I also like to mix florals and stripes for a fun and feminine outfit.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some other classic French pieces to invest in?

A: Other classic French pieces to invest in include a black dress, a pair of loafers, and a leather jacket.

Q: Can I wear color when dressing in classic French style?

A: Yes, you can wear color when dressing in classic French style. Stick to a simple color palette and add pops of color with accessories.

Q: How do I wear a silk scarf?

A: There are many ways to wear a silk scarf, from tying it around your neck to wearing it as a headband. Experiment with different styles to find what works for you.

Q: How do I mix and match patterns without looking overdone?

A: Stick to a simple color palette and let the patterns do the talking. When in doubt, stick to classic patterns such as stripes, polka dots, and plaid.

Conclusion of How to Dress Classic French Style

Dressing in classic French style is all about investing in timeless pieces, focusing on fit, and accessorizing with statement pieces. Keep your color palette simple and neutral, and don't be afraid to mix and match patterns. With these tips, you can achieve a timeless and sophisticated look that will never go out of style.
