Discover The Best Men s Suits Shops In London

Written by Daniel Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you in need of a new suit for a special occasion, but feeling overwhelmed by the options available in London? Finding the right men's suits shop can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. With so many options out there, it's important to know what you're looking for and where to find it.

The target of men's suits shops in London is to provide a wide range of high-quality suits to men who are looking for style, sophistication, and class. With so many different styles, fabrics, and colours available, it can be difficult to know where to start your search.

To help you navigate the world of men's suits in London, we've put together a guide to the best shops in the city. From bespoke tailors to high-end retailers, we've got you covered.

Harrods

One of the most iconic department stores in London, Harrods offers a wide range of luxury suits from some of the biggest names in fashion. Whether you're looking for a classic black suit or something a little more daring, Harrods has something for everyone.

Personally, I had a great experience at Harrods when I was looking for a suit for my brother's wedding. The staff were incredibly helpful and knowledgeable, and I was able to find the perfect suit in no time.

Savile Row

If you're looking for a truly bespoke experience, Savile Row is the place to go. This street in Mayfair is famous for its tailors, who have been creating custom suits for over 200 years. From Prince Charles to Mick Jagger, some of the most stylish men in history have had suits made on Savile Row.

I had the pleasure of getting a suit made on Savile Row for my own wedding, and it was truly a one-of-a-kind experience. The attention to detail and level of craftsmanship was unparalleled.

Burberry

Burberry is a British fashion powerhouse, known for its iconic trench coats and signature check pattern. But they also offer a great selection of men's suits, ranging from classic to modern styles. Their flagship store on Regent Street is definitely worth a visit if you're in the market for a new suit.

Moss Bros.

If you're on a budget but still want a stylish suit, Moss Bros. is a great option. They offer a range of affordable suits in a variety of styles, colours, and fabrics. They also have a helpful online sizing guide, so you can ensure a perfect fit.

Question and Answer

Q: How much should I expect to pay for a high-quality suit in London?

A: It really depends on where you go and what you're looking for. You can find suits at Moss Bros. for as little as £100, while bespoke suits on Savile Row can cost thousands of pounds.

Q: How long does it take to get a bespoke suit made on Savile Row?

A: It typically takes around 8-12 weeks to get a bespoke suit made on Savile Row. However, some tailors offer rush services for an additional fee.

Q: Can I return a suit if it doesn't fit?

A: It depends on the shop's policy, so be sure to ask before making a purchase. Most shops will allow returns or alterations if the suit doesn't fit properly.

Q: How should I care for my new suit?

A: Always follow the care instructions on the label, but in general, you should avoid machine-washing or dry-cleaning your suit. Instead, hang it up after each wear and use a steamer or iron to remove any wrinkles.

Conclusion of Men's Suits Shops in London

Whether you're looking for a bespoke suit on Savile Row or a budget-friendly option at Moss Bros., there's no shortage of men's suits shops in London. With a little research and some expert guidance, you can find the perfect suit for any occasion.
