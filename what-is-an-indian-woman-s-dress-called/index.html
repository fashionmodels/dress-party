What Is An Indian Woman s Dress Called

Written by Daniel Aug 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever wonder what Indian women wear in their daily lives or at special occasions? Are you confused about the different types of dresses that Indian women wear? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will explore what is an Indian woman's dress called and some related keywords.

An Indian woman's dress is called a saree or a sari. It is a long piece of cloth, usually six yards in length, that is draped around the body in a unique style. The saree is worn with a blouse and a petticoat or an underskirt. The blouse covers the upper body, while the petticoat covers the lower body.

In summary, an Indian woman's dress is called a saree or a sari, and it is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body in a unique style. It is worn with a blouse and a petticoat or an underskirt.

What is a Saree?

As mentioned earlier, a saree is a long piece of cloth, usually six yards in length, that is draped around the body in a unique style. It is the most common and popular dress worn by Indian women. A saree can be made of various fabrics such as silk, cotton, chiffon, georgette, and many more. It is available in a wide range of colors, designs, and patterns.

I remember the first time I wore a saree. It was my cousin's wedding, and I was just thirteen years old. My mother helped me drape the saree, and I felt like a princess. I was fascinated by the way the saree made me look and feel. Since then, I have worn sarees on many occasions and have always received compliments.

Types of Sarees

There are many types of sarees available in India, each with its unique style and design. Some popular types of sarees are:

Banarasi Saree

A Banarasi saree is a type of saree made in Varanasi, a city in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh. It is known for its intricate designs and heavy work. Banarasi sarees are usually made of silk and are very popular among Indian brides.

How to Wear a Saree

Wearing a saree may seem daunting at first, but with a little practice, it can be done easily. Here are the steps to wear a saree:

1. Start by wearing a blouse and a petticoat or an underskirt.

2. Tuck the saree into the petticoat at the waist.

3. Take the loose end of the saree and drape it over the left shoulder, making sure the length is equal on both sides.

4. Bring the saree around the back and tuck it into the petticoat on the right side.

5. Take the remaining saree and drape it over the left shoulder, making pleats in the front.

6. Tuck the pleats into the petticoat at the waist.

7. Adjust the saree and make sure it is draped evenly.

Kurti

A kurti is a type of tunic that is worn with leggings or churidar pants. It is a popular dress among Indian women as it is comfortable and easy to wear. Kurtis are available in various designs, patterns, and colors.

I love wearing kurtis as they are stylish yet comfortable. I usually wear them to work or when I am out shopping with friends.
