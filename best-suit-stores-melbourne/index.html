The Best Suit Stores In Melbourne Where To Find Your Perfect Fit

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of scouring the city for a suit that fits just right? Look no further than Melbourne's top suit stores. Whether you need a suit for a wedding, job interview, or just want to upgrade your wardrobe, these stores have got you covered.

When it comes to finding the perfect suit, it's important to consider fit, style, and, of course, price. Melbourne has a plethora of suit stores, but not all are created equal. Some may have great prices but lack quality, while others may have top-notch suits but come with a hefty price tag. That's why we've compiled a list of the best suit stores in Melbourne, so you can find your perfect fit without breaking the bank.

If you're looking for quality and affordability, look no further than MJ Bale. With multiple locations throughout Melbourne, MJ Bale offers a wide range of suits in various styles and fits. The store's knowledgeable staff can help you find the perfect suit for any occasion, and they offer alterations in-store to ensure the perfect fit.

Another great option is Oscar Hunt Tailors. Located in the heart of Melbourne, Oscar Hunt Tailors offers bespoke and made-to-measure suits for the ultimate personalized experience. The store's expert tailors will work with you to create a suit that fits your body and style perfectly.

For those looking for a luxury experience, Harrolds is the place to go. With a range of top designer brands, including Tom Ford and Saint Laurent, Harrolds offers the ultimate shopping experience for the fashion-forward man.

The Target of Best Suit Stores Melbourne

The target of best suit stores Melbourne is the fashion-conscious man who wants a suit that fits his body and style perfectly. Whether it's for a wedding, job interview, or just to upgrade his wardrobe, these stores offer a wide range of options to suit any occasion and budget.

Personally, I've had great experiences at both MJ Bale and Oscar Hunt Tailors. At MJ Bale, I was able to find a suit that fit me perfectly and didn't break the bank. The staff was knowledgeable and helpful, and the alterations they made in-store were spot-on. At Oscar Hunt Tailors, I was blown away by the personalized experience. The tailor took the time to get to know me and my style preferences, and the end result was a suit that fit me like a glove.

Other Best Suit Stores Melbourne

In addition to the aforementioned stores, there are plenty of other great options for suit shopping in Melbourne. Calibre offers a range of stylish suits at affordable prices, while P. Johnson Tailors specializes in handmade suits crafted from the finest materials. For those looking for a mix of affordability and luxury, H&M and Zara both offer a range of suits in various styles and fits.

Why Fit is Important

When it comes to suits, fit is everything. A poorly fitting suit can make even the most stylish of men look sloppy and unkempt. On the other hand, a suit that fits perfectly can make a man look sharp and put-together. That's why it's important to take the time to find a suit that fits your body type and style preferences.

How to Find Your Perfect Fit

The best way to find your perfect suit fit is to try on multiple options and get expert advice from the store's staff. Don't be afraid to ask for alterations to ensure a perfect fit. It's also important to consider the occasion you'll be wearing the suit for, as this may affect the style and fit you choose.

Conclusion of Best Suit Stores Melbourne

When it comes to finding the perfect suit in Melbourne, there are plenty of options to suit any occasion and budget. From affordable options like MJ Bale and Calibre to luxury experiences like Harrolds, there's a store for every style preference. Just remember, fit is everything, so take the time to find a suit that fits your body type and style perfectly.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I consider when buying a suit?

A: When buying a suit, consider fit, style, and price. It's important to find a suit that fits your body type and style preferences, while also fitting your budget.

Q: How many suit stores are in Melbourne?

A: Melbourne has a plethora of suit stores, ranging from affordable options to luxury experiences. There are too many to count!

Q: What's the difference between bespoke and made-to-measure suits?

A: Bespoke suits are made from scratch, with the tailor creating a pattern specifically for the individual's body. Made-to-measure suits are based on pre-existing patterns, but are altered to fit the individual's body.

Q: Do I need to spend a lot of money to get a good suit?

A: Not necessarily. There are plenty of affordable options for quality suits, such as MJ Bale and Calibre. However, if you want a luxury experience, you may need to spend a little more.
