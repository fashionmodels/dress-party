Question and Answer about Best Place to Sell Designer Clothes UK

Q: How much can I expect to earn from selling designer clothes?

A: The amount that you can earn from selling designer clothes depends on a variety of factors, including the brand, the condition of the item, and the demand for the item. Generally, you can expect to earn anywhere from 30% to 70% of the original price.
