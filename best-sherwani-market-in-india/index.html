Best Sherwani Market In India A Guide To Finding The Perfect Traditional Outfit

Written by Bardi Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best sherwani market in India to find the perfect traditional outfit for your special occasion? It can be a daunting task to navigate through the numerous options available and find the right place to buy a sherwani that suits your taste, budget, and occasion. With this guide, we aim to make your search easier by providing you with all the information you need about the best sherwani market in India.

The target of the best sherwani market in India is to provide a platform where you can find a wide variety of sherwanis, from classic to contemporary designs, in different fabrics, colors, and styles. The market caters to the needs of grooms, groomsmen, and wedding guests who want to make a statement with their traditional attire.

In summary, the best sherwani market in India provides a one-stop-shop for all your traditional outfit needs, with a vast selection of sherwanis available at various price points, styles, and fabrics.

The Best Sherwani Market in India: Chandni Chowk

Chandni Chowk in Old Delhi is one of the oldest and most popular markets for traditional Indian attire, including sherwanis. It is a bustling market that offers a vast range of options, from affordable to high-end sherwanis, in different fabrics such as silk, brocade, and velvet. The market is known for its intricate embroidery, zari work, and embellishments that make the sherwanis stand out.

My personal experience at Chandni Chowk was overwhelming, with so many shops and options to choose from. However, I found that the best way to navigate the market is to do your research beforehand and have a clear idea of what you want. Also, don't be afraid to negotiate the price, as it is customary in Indian markets.

The Best Sherwani Market in India: Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is another popular destination for traditional Indian attire, including sherwanis. The city is famous for its vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and attention to detail, which is reflected in its sherwanis. The sherwanis in Jaipur are known for their royal look, with intricate embroidery, embellishments, and a regal style.

My experience in Jaipur was fantastic, with a wide range of options available, from classic to contemporary designs. The sherwanis in Jaipur are slightly more expensive than other markets, but the quality and attention to detail make them worth the price.

Choosing the Right Sherwani

When choosing a sherwani, there are several factors to consider, such as the occasion, your personal style, and your budget. It is essential to choose a sherwani that suits your body type and enhances your features. For instance, if you are tall and slim, opt for a fitted sherwani that accentuates your frame. On the other hand, if you are on the heavier side, choose a sherwani that is loose-fitting and comfortable.

How to Style a Sherwani

When styling a sherwani, it is essential to keep the occasion in mind. For weddings and formal events, opt for a traditional sherwani with intricate embroidery and embellishments, paired with churidar pants and jutti shoes. For a more contemporary look, you can opt for a fusion sherwani with a Nehru collar or a jacket-style sherwani paired with trousers and loafers.

Conclusion of Best Sherwani Market in India

In conclusion, the best sherwani market in India offers a vast range of options for traditional attire, with Chandni Chowk and Jaipur being popular destinations. When choosing a sherwani, consider factors such as occasion, personal style, and budget, and when styling it, keep in mind the event's formality level. With this guide, we hope you find the perfect sherwani that makes you feel confident and stylish on your special day.

Question and Answer

Q: What is a sherwani?

A: A sherwani is a traditional Indian outfit worn by men for formal events such as weddings and religious ceremonies. It is a long coat-like garment that is usually worn over a kurta and paired with churidar pants and jutti shoes.

Q: What is the best fabric for a sherwani?

A: The best fabric for a sherwani depends on the occasion and the weather. For weddings and formal events, silk, brocade, and velvet are popular choices, while for summer events, cotton and linen are more suitable.

Q: How much does a sherwani cost?

A: The cost of a sherwani depends on several factors such as the fabric, style, and embellishments. A basic sherwani can cost anywhere from INR 5,000 to INR 20,000, while a high-end designer sherwani can cost upwards of INR 1,00,000.

Q: Can I rent a sherwani?

A: Yes, several stores offer sherwani rental services for grooms and groomsmen. This is a cost-effective option if you don't want to invest in a sherwani that you may not wear again.
