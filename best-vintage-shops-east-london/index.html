Discover The Best Vintage Shops In East London

Written by Petter Jun 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a vintage enthusiast looking for the best vintage shops in East London? Do you want to stand out from the crowd with unique and stylish vintage pieces? Look no further because we’ve got you covered!

Searching for the perfect vintage item can be overwhelming, especially with so many shops to choose from. However, we’ve narrowed down the best vintage shops in East London to make your shopping experience easier and more enjoyable.

In this article, we’ll highlight the top vintage shops in East London and provide you with all the information you need to find the perfect vintage piece for your collection.

Brick Lane Market

Brick Lane Market is a vibrant and bustling market that takes place every Sunday. It’s the perfect place to find vintage treasures with over 200 stalls selling vintage clothing, accessories, furniture, and much more.

One of our favorite vintage shops in Brick Lane Market is Beyond Retro. It’s a massive store with an extensive collection of vintage clothing from the 1920s to the 1990s. Their pieces are carefully selected and curated, so you’re guaranteed to find something unique and stylish.

The East End Thrift Store

The East End Thrift Store is a non-profit organization that sells vintage and pre-loved clothing, accessories, and homeware. They have a vast collection of vintage items, and the best part is that all profits go towards charities that support vulnerable people in the community.

I recently visited the East End Thrift Store and found a beautiful vintage leather jacket that I’ve been wearing ever since. The staff were friendly and helpful, and the prices were very reasonable.

Blitz Vintage

Blitz Vintage is a vintage department store that sells clothing, accessories, homeware, and furniture. It’s a massive store with four floors of vintage treasures, and the pieces are carefully selected and curated.

I love visiting Blitz Vintage because there’s always something new to discover. The store has a great atmosphere, and the staff are knowledgeable and passionate about vintage fashion.

Atika London

Atika London is a vintage store that sells unique and stylish vintage pieces. They have a vast collection of vintage clothing, accessories, and homeware, and the pieces are carefully selected and curated.

The store has a great atmosphere, and the staff are friendly and knowledgeable. It’s the perfect place to find a one-of-a-kind vintage piece that will make you stand out from the crowd.

How to Shop for Vintage Clothing

Shopping for vintage clothing can be a bit different from shopping for contemporary clothing. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Know your measurements: Vintage clothing sizes can be different from contemporary clothing sizes, so it’s essential to know your measurements to ensure a good fit.

2. Check for damage: Vintage clothing may have wear and tear, so it’s important to check for any damage before buying. Look for stains, holes, and tears.

3. Look beyond the size tag: Don’t be afraid to try on clothing that’s not your usual size. Vintage clothing can have a different fit, so it’s important to try on items to see how they look and feel.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Shops in East London

East London is the perfect place to find unique and stylish vintage pieces. Whether you’re looking for clothing, accessories, homeware, or furniture, the vintage shops in East London have it all.

Remember to keep an open mind when shopping for vintage clothing and to have fun exploring the many vintage shops in East London. Who knows what treasures you’ll find!
