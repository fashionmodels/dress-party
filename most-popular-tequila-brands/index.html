The Ultimate Guide To Most Popular Tequila Brands

Written by Luffy Jun 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Tequila is one of the most popular alcoholic beverages in the world. It is a distilled spirit made from the blue agave plant, primarily grown in the Mexican state of Jalisco. However, with so many tequila brands available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we will explore the most popular tequila brands and help you find the perfect one for your taste buds. If you're a tequila lover, you know that the wrong choice can ruin your entire evening. But with so many different brands and types of tequila, it can be tough to know where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to help you make an informed decision. Firstly, let's take a look at the different types of tequila. There are five types of tequila: Blanco, Reposado, Añejo, Extra Añejo, and Joven or Gold. Each type has its unique flavor profile, aroma, and color, so it's essential to know what you're looking for before choosing a brand. In this article, we'll be focusing on the top five most popular tequila brands: Patrón, Don Julio, Jose Cuervo, Sauza, and 1800. These brands have been around for decades and have a reputation for producing high-quality tequila.

Patrón

If you're looking for a high-end tequila brand, Patrón is the one for you. It is a premium tequila brand that is known for its smooth taste and superior quality. Patrón has a range of tequilas, including Silver, Reposado, Añejo, and Extra Añejo. The Silver tequila is perfect for margaritas, while the Añejo is ideal for sipping. Personally, I have tried Patrón's Silver and Reposado, and I must say, they are some of the smoothest tequilas I have ever tasted. Patrón's Silver has a crisp, clean taste with a hint of citrus, while the Reposado has a slightly oaky and caramel flavor.

Don Julio

Don Julio is another premium tequila brand that has been around since 1942. It is known for its smooth and rich flavor, which is due to its unique distillation process. Don Julio has a range of tequilas, including Blanco, Reposado, Añejo, and 1942. The 1942 is their high-end tequila, which is aged for two and a half years and has a rich and complex flavor. I have personally tried Don Julio's Reposado and Añejo, and I must say, they are both exceptional. The Reposado has a light and fruity taste, while the Añejo has a more robust and full-bodied flavor with hints of vanilla and caramel.

Jose Cuervo

Jose Cuervo is one of the most well-known and oldest tequila brands in the world, dating back to 1758. It is known for its affordable price and excellent quality. Jose Cuervo has a range of tequilas, including Blanco, Reposado, Añejo, and Reserva de la Familia. The Reserva de la Familia is their high-end tequila, which is aged for three years and has a smooth and complex flavor. I have tried Jose Cuervo's Blanco and Reposado, and I must say, they are both excellent tequilas. The Blanco has a crisp and clean taste, while the Reposado has a slightly sweet and oaky flavor.

Sauza

Sauza is a budget-friendly tequila brand that has been around since 1873. It is known for its smooth and light taste and is perfect for cocktails. Sauza has a range of tequilas, including Blanco, Reposado, Añejo, and Hornitos. The Hornitos is their high-end tequila, which is aged for 18 months and has a smooth and complex flavor. I have tried Sauza's Blanco and Reposado, and I must say, they are both excellent tequilas for their price point. The Blanco has a light and citrusy taste, while the Reposado has a slightly sweet and oaky flavor.

1800

1800 is a premium tequila brand that has been around since 1800. It is known for its smooth and complex flavor and is perfect for sipping. 1800 has a range of tequilas, including Silver, Reposado, Añejo, and Cristalino. The Cristalino is their high-end tequila, which is aged for 16 months and has a smooth and rich flavor. I have tried 1800's Silver and Reposado, and I must say, they are both excellent tequilas. The Silver has a crisp and clean taste, while the Reposado has a slightly sweet and oaky flavor.

What to Look for When Choosing a Tequila Brand

When choosing a tequila brand, there are several factors you should consider. Firstly, you should consider the type of tequila you want to buy. Do you want a tequila that is perfect for cocktails, or do you want one that is ideal for sipping? Secondly, you should consider the price point. Tequila can range from budget-friendly to high-end, so it's essential to know what you're willing to spend. Thirdly, you should consider the brand's reputation. Stick to brands that have been around for a while and have a reputation for producing high-quality tequila.

How to Drink Tequila

Tequila can be enjoyed in several ways, including sipping, shooting, or mixed in cocktails. If you're sipping tequila, it's best to serve it neat or on the rocks. However, if you're shooting tequila, it's best to serve it chilled with a slice of lime and a bit of salt. Finally, if you're mixing tequila in cocktails, it's best to use a high-quality tequila like one of the brands mentioned above.

Conclusion of Most Popular Tequila Brands

In conclusion, tequila is an excellent spirit that can be enjoyed in several ways. However, with so many brands available in the market, it can be tough to choose the right one. Stick to the most popular tequila brands like Patrón, Don Julio, Jose Cuervo, Sauza, and 1800, and you can't go wrong. Remember to consider the type of tequila, price point, and brand reputation when choosing a tequila brand. Finally, enjoy your tequila responsibly and in moderation. Cheers!
