What To Study After 10Th To Become A Fashion Designer

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in fashion designing and wondering what to study after 10th to become a fashion designer? It can be overwhelming to choose the right path when there are so many options available. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to becoming a fashion designer after completing your 10th grade.

The fashion industry is highly competitive, and to succeed in this field, you need to have a keen eye for detail, be creative, and have excellent communication skills. It's essential to choose the right course that will give you the knowledge and skills you need to create a successful career in fashion designing.

The first step to becoming a fashion designer is to complete your 10th grade. After that, you can pursue a diploma or degree in fashion designing. There are various courses available, such as Diploma in Fashion Designing, Bachelor in Fashion Designing, and Master in Fashion Designing, among others.

To get admission to these courses, you need to meet specific eligibility criteria, such as completing your 10+2 education from a recognized board. Some colleges may require you to appear for an entrance test or submit a portfolio of your work to assess your creativity and skills.

My Personal Experience

When I completed my 10th grade, I was passionate about fashion designing and knew that was what I wanted to pursue as a career. I enrolled in a Diploma in Fashion Designing course, which gave me a strong foundation in the basics of fashion designing. The course covered topics such as color theory, sketching, pattern making, and garment construction.

After completing my diploma, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Fashion Designing, which gave me a more in-depth understanding of the fashion industry. The course covered topics such as fashion history, fashion marketing, and fashion merchandising. I also got the opportunity to intern with a fashion designer, which gave me hands-on experience in the field.

Courses to Consider

Here are some of the popular courses you can consider after completing your 10th grade to become a fashion designer:

Diploma in Fashion Designing

A Diploma in Fashion Designing is a one-year course that covers the basics of fashion design, including sketching, pattern making, garment construction, and fashion illustration. This course is ideal for those who want to get started in the fashion industry quickly.

Bachelor in Fashion Designing

A Bachelor in Fashion Designing is a three-year course that provides a more in-depth understanding of the fashion industry. The course covers topics such as fashion history, fashion marketing, and fashion merchandising. Students also get the opportunity to intern with fashion designers to gain hands-on experience.

Career Opportunities

After completing your course in fashion designing, you can explore various career opportunities in the fashion industry. Some of the popular career options include:

Fashion Designer

As a fashion designer, you will be responsible for creating new designs and overseeing the production of garments. You will need to have a keen eye for detail, be creative, and have excellent communication skills.

Fashion Merchandiser

As a fashion merchandiser, you will be responsible for promoting and selling fashion products. You will need to have a good understanding of the fashion industry and consumer behavior.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I pursue fashion designing after completing my 12th grade?
A: Yes, you can pursue fashion designing after completing your 12th grade.

Q: Do I need to have any specific skills to become a fashion designer?
A: Yes, you need to have a keen eye for detail, be creative, and have excellent communication skills to become a fashion designer.

Q: What are the eligibility criteria to apply for a course in fashion designing?
A: The eligibility criteria vary from college to college, but most colleges require you to complete your 10+2 education from a recognized board. Some colleges may also require you to appear for an entrance test or submit a portfolio of your work.

Q: What are the career opportunities after completing a course in fashion designing?
A: After completing a course in fashion designing, you can explore various career opportunities such as fashion designer, fashion merchandiser, fashion stylist, and fashion illustrator, among others.

Conclusion of What to Study After 10th to Become a Fashion Designer

Choosing the right course after completing your 10th grade is crucial to becoming a successful fashion designer. You need to have a good understanding of the fashion industry and the skills required to create new designs and oversee the production of garments. We hope this article has given you a better understanding of what to study after 10th to become a fashion designer.
