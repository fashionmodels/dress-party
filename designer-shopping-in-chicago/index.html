Designer Shopping In Chicago The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Chicago is known for its bustling shopping scene, with designer stores and boutiques scattered throughout the city. However, navigating this world of high-end fashion can be overwhelming and intimidating for many. From finding the right stores to knowing what to look for, designer shopping in Chicago can be a daunting task. But fear not, as we have put together the ultimate guide to designer shopping in Chicago to make your experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

If you're looking to invest in high-quality designer pieces or simply want to indulge in some luxury shopping, Chicago has you covered. However, with so many options available, it can be hard to know where to start. Whether you're a seasoned shopper or a first-timer, this guide will help you navigate the world of designer shopping in Chicago.

In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about designer shopping in Chicago, including the best stores to visit, the latest fashion trends, and insider tips to help you get the most out of your shopping experience.

Our Personal Experience with Designer Shopping in Chicago

As locals, we've had the pleasure of exploring Chicago's designer shopping scene extensively. From the iconic Michigan Avenue to the trendy boutiques in Lincoln Park, we've scoured the city to find the best designer stores and hidden gems. Our personal experience has given us valuable insights into what makes designer shopping in Chicago so unique and exciting.

Best Designer Stores in Chicago

Chicago is home to some of the best designer stores in the world, with a wide range of options to suit every taste and budget. Here are some of the top designer stores to visit in Chicago:

Marc Jacobs

Located in the heart of Chicago's Gold Coast, Marc Jacobs is a must-visit for any fashion lover. The store is a treasure trove of the latest fashion trends, with a wide range of clothing, accessories, and footwear to choose from. Whether you're looking for a statement piece or a classic staple, Marc Jacobs has something for everyone.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Visit to Marc Jacobs

When visiting Marc Jacobs, be sure to take your time and explore the entire store. The displays are beautifully curated, with each item carefully selected to create a cohesive and stylish look. Don't be afraid to ask the staff for assistance, as they are always happy to help you find the perfect piece.

Saks Fifth Avenue

Saks Fifth Avenue is a luxury department store that offers an unparalleled shopping experience. The store is home to a wide range of designer brands, including Gucci, Prada, and Saint Laurent. From high-end clothing to luxury accessories, Saks Fifth Avenue has everything you need to elevate your wardrobe.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Visit to Saks Fifth Avenue

When visiting Saks Fifth Avenue, be sure to take advantage of the store's personal shopping service. The team of expert stylists can help you find the perfect outfit for any occasion, taking into account your personal style and preferences. Additionally, be sure to check out the store's beauty section, which offers a wide range of luxury skincare and makeup products.

Neiman Marcus

Neiman Marcus is another luxury department store that is a must-visit for any fashion lover. The store is home to a wide range of designer brands, including Chanel, Givenchy, and Alexander McQueen. From designer handbags to high-end shoes, Neiman Marcus has everything you need to elevate your wardrobe.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Visit to Neiman Marcus

When visiting Neiman Marcus, be sure to check out the store's in-house cafe, the Zodiac Room. The restaurant offers a chic and sophisticated atmosphere, with delicious food and a wide range of cocktails to choose from. Additionally, be sure to take advantage of the store's personal shopping service, which can help you find the perfect outfit for any occasion.

FAQs About Designer Shopping in Chicago

Q: What are the best neighborhoods for designer shopping in Chicago?

A: Some of the best neighborhoods for designer shopping in Chicago include the Magnificent Mile, the Gold Coast, and Lincoln Park.

Q: What designer brands can I find in Chicago?

A: Chicago is home to a wide range of designer brands, including Gucci, Prada, Chanel, and Saint Laurent.

Q: Are there any outlet malls for designer shopping in Chicago?

A: Yes, Chicago is home to several outlet malls that offer discounted designer clothing and accessories, including the Fashion Outlets of Chicago and the Chicago Premium Outlets.

Q: What should I wear when designer shopping in Chicago?

A: We recommend wearing comfortable shoes and clothing that is easy to change in and out of, as many stores have dressing rooms available.

Conclusion of Designer Shopping in Chicago

Designer shopping in Chicago is a thrilling and exciting experience, with a wide range of designer stores and boutiques to choose from. Whether you're looking to invest in high-end pieces or simply want to indulge in some luxury shopping, Chicago has something for everyone. By following our guide, you can navigate the world of designer shopping in Chicago with ease and confidence, ensuring that you get the most out of your shopping experience.
