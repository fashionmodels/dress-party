Discovering Indian Brands In Uae A Guide For Shoppers

Written by Frank May 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an Indian shopper living in the UAE and feeling homesick for your favorite brands? Or are you a curious shopper looking to explore the world of Indian brands available in the UAE? Either way, this guide is for you. Here, we'll explore the Indian brands available in the UAE, their target audience, and some personal experiences of shopping for these brands.

Shopping for Indian brands in the UAE can be challenging, especially if you're new to the country. You may struggle to find the brands you're used to, or you may find that the prices are higher than what you're used to paying back home. Furthermore, you may find that the quality of the products is not up to your standards.

Despite these challenges, Indian brands have a strong presence in the UAE, and they cater to a diverse audience. From traditional clothing brands to modern tech companies, Indian brands offer something for everyone.

In this guide, we'll explore the various Indian brands available in the UAE, their target audience, and what makes them unique. We'll also share some personal experiences of shopping for these brands in the UAE.

Indian Clothing Brands in the UAE

One of the most popular categories of Indian brands in the UAE is clothing. Indian clothing is known for its vibrant colors, intricate detailing, and comfortable fabrics. Many Indian clothing brands cater to women, but there are also numerous options for men and children.

Personally, I have always been a fan of Indian clothing brands, and I was thrilled to discover that many of my favorite brands were available in the UAE. Some of the most popular Indian clothing brands in the UAE include Fabindia, Biba, and W. These brands offer a wide range of clothing options, from traditional sarees and salwar kameezes to modern fusion wear.

Indian Food Brands in the UAE

Indian food is beloved all over the world, and the UAE is no exception. If you're a fan of Indian food, you'll be pleased to know that there are numerous Indian food brands available in the UAE. These brands cater to a range of tastes and preferences, from spicy to sweet.

One of my personal favorites is Haldiram's, a popular Indian snack brand that offers a wide range of sweet and savory snacks. Another popular brand is Amul, which offers a range of dairy products, including milk, cheese, and butter.

Indian Beauty Brands in the UAE

Indian beauty brands are gaining popularity in the UAE, thanks to their focus on natural ingredients and affordability. These brands offer a wide range of skincare and makeup products that cater to a diverse audience.

One of the most popular Indian beauty brands in the UAE is Himalaya Herbals, which offers a range of skincare products made with natural ingredients. Another popular brand is Lotus Herbals, which offers a range of makeup and skincare products that are free from harmful chemicals.

How to Find Indian Brands in the UAE

Now that you know about some of the most popular Indian brands available in the UAE, you may be wondering how to find them. The easiest way to find Indian brands is to visit an Indian supermarket or grocery store. These stores typically carry a wide range of Indian brands, from food to clothing to beauty products.

You can also find Indian brands online, through e-commerce sites like Amazon.ae and Noon.com. These sites offer a wide range of Indian brands, and you can often find good deals and discounts.

Conclusion of Indian Brands in UAE

Overall, Indian brands have a strong presence in the UAE, catering to a diverse audience of shoppers. Whether you're looking for traditional clothing or modern tech products, there's an Indian brand for you. By exploring the various Indian brands available in the UAE, you can connect with your culture and discover new products that you'll love.
