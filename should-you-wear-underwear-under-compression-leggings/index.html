Should You Wear Underwear Under Compression Leggings

Written by Eveline Aug 04, 2023 · 5 min read

Compression leggings have become a staple in the world of fitness and athletic wear. They offer numerous benefits, such as improved circulation and reduced muscle fatigue. However, one question that often arises is whether or not you should wear underwear under compression leggings. This article will explore the pros and cons of wearing underwear under compression leggings and help you make an informed decision.

The idea of going commando under tight-fitting leggings may be uncomfortable for some. On the other hand, wearing underwear can sometimes cause irritation or chafing when combined with compression fabric. These pain points can make it difficult to determine the best course of action.

The answer to whether or not you should wear underwear under compression leggings ultimately depends on personal preference. Some people find it more comfortable to wear underwear, while others prefer to go without. Ultimately, it comes down to what feels best for your body.

In summary, here are the main points to consider when deciding whether or not to wear underwear under compression leggings:

- Wearing underwear can cause irritation or chafing when combined with compression fabric.

- Going commando may be uncomfortable for some.

- The decision ultimately comes down to personal preference.

Comfort and Hygiene

When it comes to wearing underwear under compression leggings, comfort and hygiene are the two biggest concerns. For some, wearing underwear can provide a layer of protection and comfort that is not found when going commando. However, others may find that wearing underwear causes more discomfort than going without.

Personally, I prefer to wear underwear under my compression leggings. I find that it provides an extra layer of protection and helps prevent any unwanted chafing. Additionally, wearing underwear can help with hygiene by absorbing sweat and preventing any unwanted odors.

Performance and Support

Another factor to consider when deciding whether or not to wear underwear under compression leggings is performance and support. Compression leggings are designed to provide support to the muscles, and wearing underwear may interfere with this support. However, this is largely dependent on the type of underwear you are wearing.

If you choose to wear underwear under your compression leggings, it is essential to choose a pair that is made of moisture-wicking material and does not interfere with the compression of the leggings. Avoid underwear made of cotton, as it can trap moisture and cause irritation or chafing.

Types of Underwear to Wear

If you decide to wear underwear under your compression leggings, there are a few types of underwear that are better suited for this purpose. These include:

- Moisture-wicking underwear: This type of underwear is designed to pull moisture away from the skin and keep you dry.

- Seamless underwear: Seamless underwear is designed to be smooth and comfortable, reducing the risk of chafing or irritation.

- Thong underwear: Thong underwear can be a good option for those who do not want to deal with visible panty lines.

How to Choose the Right Underwear

Choosing the right underwear to wear under compression leggings can be a bit of trial and error. It is essential to choose a pair that is comfortable, does not interfere with the compression of the leggings, and is made of moisture-wicking material.

When shopping for underwear to wear under your compression leggings, try on a few different styles and materials to see what feels best for your body. If possible, opt for seamless or moisture-wicking underwear to minimize any irritation or chafing.

Conclusion

At the end of the day, the decision to wear underwear under compression leggings comes down to personal preference. Some people prefer the added layer of protection and comfort that comes with wearing underwear, while others find it more comfortable to go commando. Whatever you choose, make sure to choose a pair of underwear that is comfortable, moisture-wicking, and does not interfere with the compression of the leggings.

Question and Answer

Q: Can wearing underwear under compression leggings cause irritation?

A: Yes, wearing underwear under compression leggings can cause irritation if the underwear is made of cotton or does not fit properly. It is essential to choose underwear made of moisture-wicking material and try on a few different styles to find what works best for your body.

Q: Will wearing underwear under compression leggings affect their performance?

A: It depends on the type of underwear you are wearing. Underwear made of moisture-wicking material that does not interfere with the compression of the leggings should not affect their performance.

Q: Should I wear thong underwear under my compression leggings?

A: Thong underwear can be a good option for those who do not want to deal with visible panty lines. However, it is essential to choose a pair made of moisture-wicking material and ensure that it does not interfere with the compression of the leggings.

Q: Is it better to go commando under compression leggings?

A: It ultimately comes down to personal preference. Some people find it more comfortable to go commando, while others prefer the added layer of protection and comfort that comes with wearing underwear.
