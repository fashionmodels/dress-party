The Best Eateries for Authentic South Indian Food in Pune

Our first recommendation for authentic South Indian food in Pune is Vaishali. Vaishali is a popular eatery known for its delicious and authentic South Indian cuisine. They have been serving customers for over six decades and have become a household name in Pune. The restaurant is located in the heart of the city and is always buzzing with customers.

My personal experience at Vaishali was fantastic. The food was delicious, and the service was excellent. I highly recommend trying their dosas and filter coffee.

Another great option for authentic South Indian food in Pune is Shree Krishna. Shree Krishna is a small restaurant located in Kothrud, Pune. They serve a variety of South Indian dishes, including dosas, idlis, vadas, and uttapams.

I've been to Shree Krishna several times, and I must say their food is fantastic. The masala dosa is a must-try, and their chutneys are delicious.
