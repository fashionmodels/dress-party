Do You Wear Running Shorts With Underwear

Written by Robby Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a runner who is unsure whether to wear underwear under your running shorts? Do you find yourself wondering if wearing underwear is necessary or if it would just cause discomfort during your run? If you're looking for answers to these questions, then keep reading.

The simple answer to whether you should wear underwear under your running shorts is no. Running shorts are designed to be worn without underwear, as they are made from lightweight, breathable materials that help prevent chafing and discomfort during your run.

Wearing underwear under your running shorts can actually cause more harm than good. Underwear can trap sweat and moisture, leading to irritation and chafing. It can also create extra layers of fabric, which can make your shorts feel tighter and more uncomfortable.

In summary, running shorts are meant to be worn without underwear. They are designed specifically for running and provide the necessary support and comfort needed during your workout. Not wearing underwear can also help prevent chafing and irritation, which can be a real pain point for runners.

My Personal Experience

As a runner myself, I used to wear underwear under my running shorts. I thought it would provide extra support and prevent any embarrassing moments during my workout. However, I quickly realized that it was causing more harm than good.

After a few runs, I started to experience chafing and irritation in my groin area. It was uncomfortable and made running nearly unbearable. I did some research and discovered that wearing underwear under running shorts was not recommended. I decided to give it a try, and since then, I have never looked back.

The Benefits of Not Wearing Underwear Under Running Shorts

There are many benefits to not wearing underwear under your running shorts. Firstly, it can help prevent chafing and irritation, which can be a real pain point for runners. Secondly, it allows the fabric of your shorts to breathe, which can help regulate your body temperature and prevent excess sweating. Thirdly, it eliminates any extra layers of fabric, which can make your shorts feel tighter and more uncomfortable.

Tips for Wearing Running Shorts Without Underwear

If you're new to running without underwear, here are a few tips to help you get started:

1. Choose shorts that are specifically designed for running. Look for lightweight, breathable materials that are moisture-wicking and quick-drying.

2. Make sure your shorts fit properly. They should be snug but not tight, with enough room to move freely.

3. Use a lubricant such as Body Glide to help prevent chafing and irritation in your groin area.

How to Choose the Right Running Shorts

When choosing running shorts, it's important to consider the type of running you'll be doing. For shorter, faster runs, you may want to opt for shorts that are shorter and more form-fitting. For longer runs, you may want to choose shorts that are longer and looser for added comfort and support.

FAQs

Conclusion of Do You Wear Running Shorts With Underwear

In conclusion, wearing underwear under your running shorts is not necessary and can actually cause discomfort and irritation. Running shorts are designed to be worn without underwear, and doing so can help prevent chafing and regulate your body temperature. Remember to choose shorts that fit properly and practice good hygiene habits to prevent any potential hygiene issues.
