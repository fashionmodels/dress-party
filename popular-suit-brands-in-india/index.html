Cool Popular Suit Brands In India Trend In 2023

Discover the Best Suit Brands in India That Will Elevate Your Style Game

When it comes to formalwear for men, a suit is a wardrobe staple that every man needs. Whether it's for a job interview, a wedding, or a formal event, a well-fitted suit can make you look sharp and sophisticated. However, with so many brands and styles to choose from, finding the perfect suit can be overwhelming. Not to mention, the quality and fit of the suit can make all the difference in how you look and feel.

If you're looking for the best suit brands in India that offer quality, style, and a perfect fit, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the top suit brands in India and what makes them stand out.

First, let's answer the question: what are the best suit brands in India? Some of the most popular suit brands in India include Raymond, Louis Philippe, Van Heusen, and Blackberrys. These brands offer a wide range of suits in different styles, colors, fabrics, and price points, making it easier to find the perfect suit for any occasion.

Raymond

Raymond is one of the most well-known and respected suit brands in India. Founded in 1925, the brand has been a leader in the textile industry for over 90 years. Raymond offers a variety of suits in different fabrics, including wool, linen, and cotton. Their suits are known for their quality, durability, and timeless style.

Personally, I've always been a fan of Raymond's classic black suits. They're perfect for formal occasions and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. I also appreciate that Raymond offers custom tailoring services, so you can get a suit that fits you perfectly.

Louis Philippe

Louis Philippe is another popular suit brand in India that offers high-quality suits in classic styles. The brand was founded in 1989 and has since become a favorite among men who appreciate timeless style and impeccable craftsmanship. Louis Philippe suits are known for their attention to detail, including fine stitching, quality fabrics, and flattering cuts.

I recently purchased a navy blue Louis Philippe suit for a wedding, and I was impressed with the quality and fit. The suit was comfortable to wear all day, and I received several compliments on how sharp I looked.

Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a brand that's been around for over 100 years, and they're known for their classic and modern suits. The brand offers a range of suits in different colors and fabrics, including wool, linen, and cotton. Van Heusen suits are designed to fit well and flatter any body type, making them a popular choice for men of all ages.

I recently purchased a gray Van Heusen suit for a job interview, and it was the perfect choice. The suit was comfortable to wear and looked professional, which helped me feel more confident during the interview.

Blackberrys

Blackberrys is a relatively new suit brand in India, founded in 1991. However, the brand has quickly gained a reputation for offering high-quality suits in modern styles. Blackberrys suits are known for their slim fit and attention to detail, including quality fabrics and fine stitching.

Personally, I love the modern style of Blackberrys suits. They're perfect for younger men who want a more contemporary look while still maintaining a classic feel.

How to Choose the Right Suit Brand for You

Choosing the right suit brand can be a daunting task, but there are a few things to consider that can make the process easier. First, think about the occasion for which you need the suit. If it's for a job interview, you may want to go for a more classic style. If it's for a wedding or formal event, you may want to opt for a more modern style.

Next, consider your body type and personal style. If you're on the shorter side, you may want to go for a slim fit suit to elongate your frame. If you're tall and lean, a classic fit suit may be more flattering.

Finally, consider your budget. While a high-quality suit is an investment, it doesn't have to break the bank. Look for brands that offer a range of price points, so you can find a suit that fits your style and your wallet.

Conclusion of Popular Suit Brands in India

When it comes to finding the perfect suit, there are plenty of options to choose from. Some of the best suit brands in India include Raymond, Louis Philippe, Van Heusen, and Blackberrys. These brands offer quality, style, and a perfect fit, making it easier to find the perfect suit for any occasion. By considering your body type, personal style, and budget, you can choose the right suit brand for you and elevate your style game in no time.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best fabric for a suit in India?

A: The best fabric for a suit in India depends on the occasion and the weather. For formal occasions, wool and linen are popular choices. For warmer weather, cotton is a good option.

Q: Do suit brands in India offer custom tailoring services?

A: Yes, many suit brands in India offer custom tailoring services. This allows you to get a suit that fits you perfectly and looks great.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a high-quality suit in India?

A: The cost of a high-quality suit in India can vary depending on the brand and the materials used. You can expect to spend anywhere from INR 5,000 to INR 50,000 or more.

Q: Can I wear a suit for casual occasions in India?

A: While a suit is traditionally considered formalwear, you can dress it down for more casual occasions. For example, you could wear a suit jacket with jeans and a t-shirt for a night out with friends.
