FAQs About Boxing Gloves

Here are some frequently asked questions about boxing gloves:

1. What size boxing gloves should I purchase?

The size of boxing gloves you should purchase depends on your body weight and the intended use. Generally, 8-10 oz gloves are suitable for competition, while 12-16 oz gloves are ideal for training.

2. How do I know if my boxing gloves fit properly?

Your boxing gloves should fit snugly, but not too tightly. You should be able to make a fist comfortably, and the gloves should not slip or move around during training or competition.

3. How do I clean my boxing gloves?

You can clean your boxing gloves by wiping them down with a damp cloth after each use. You can also use a leather cleaner to keep them looking and smelling fresh.

4. How often should I replace my boxing gloves?

You should replace your boxing gloves every 6-12 months, depending on how often you use them and the level of wear and tear they experience.
