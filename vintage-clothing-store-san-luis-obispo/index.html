How to Style Vintage Clothing from San Luis Obispo's Vintage Clothing Store

Styling vintage clothing can be intimidating, but it's all about finding the right balance between vintage and modern. Here are a few tips on how to style vintage clothing from San Luis Obispo's vintage clothing store:

1. Mix and Match

Don't be afraid to mix and match vintage pieces with modern ones. For example, pair a vintage floral dress with a leather jacket or a vintage denim jacket with a modern t-shirt and jeans.

2. Accessorize

Accessorizing is key to elevating any outfit. Add a modern twist to a vintage outfit by accessorizing with statement jewelry, a belt, or a hat.

3. Experiment with Layers

Layering vintage pieces is a great way to create a unique and interesting outfit. Layer a vintage blouse over a modern t-shirt or a vintage denim jacket over a dress.
