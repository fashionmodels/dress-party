There are several types of traditional Indian male attire, including:

Nehru jacket: A hip-length jacket with a mandarin collar, often worn over a kurta or shirt.

Sherwani: A long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar pants, often worn on special occasions like weddings or formal events.
