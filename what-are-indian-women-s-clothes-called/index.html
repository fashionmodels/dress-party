What Are Indian Women s Clothes Called

Written by Petter Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever been to an Indian wedding or cultural event and wondered what the different types of clothing worn by women are called? Or maybe you're planning a trip to India and want to be familiar with the attire? Understanding what are Indian women's clothes called can be a bit daunting, but don't worry, we've got you covered.

Indian women's clothing can vary greatly depending on the region, occasion, and personal style. However, there are some common types of attire that are worn throughout the country. The different types of clothing can be overwhelming, and it can be challenging to know what to wear when attending an event or visiting a specific region.

The traditional clothing worn by Indian women is called a saree. It is a long piece of fabric, typically six to nine yards, that is draped around the body in various styles. Another common type of clothing is the salwar kameez, which consists of a long tunic top and loose pants. Dupatta is a long scarf used to cover the head or shoulders. Lehenga choli is a long skirt with a matching blouse and dupatta worn for special occasions.

In summary, Indian women's clothing includes saree, salwar kameez, dupatta, and lehenga choli. Each of these has different variations depending on the region and style.

What is a Saree?

A saree is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body in various styles. It is typically six to nine yards long and is worn with a blouse and petticoat. Sarees come in different materials like cotton, silk, and chiffon, and they can be plain or embellished with embroidery, sequins, or beads. Wearing a saree can be challenging for someone who is not familiar with it. It can take some time to learn how to drape it correctly, but once you do, it can be very elegant and beautiful.

What is a Salwar Kameez?

A salwar kameez is a popular type of clothing worn by Indian women. It consists of a long tunic top called the kameez and loose pants called the salwar. The outfit is usually accompanied by a dupatta, a long scarf used to cover the head or shoulders. Salwar kameez is comfortable and easy to wear, and it is suitable for everyday wear as well as special occasions. It comes in various materials, colors, and designs, making it a versatile outfit.

What is a Dupatta?

A dupatta is a long scarf that is worn with Indian traditional clothing like sarees and salwar kameez. It is draped around the head or over the shoulders, and it can be used to cover the head, chest, or arms. Dupattas come in different materials like cotton, silk, and chiffon, and they can be plain or embellished with embroidery, sequins, or beads. Dupattas can be used to add a pop of color or to complement the outfit.

What is a Lehenga Choli?

A lehenga choli is a type of clothing worn by Indian women for special occasions like weddings and festivals. It consists of a long skirt called the lehenga, a blouse called the choli, and a dupatta. The outfit is usually made of silk or other luxurious materials and is heavily embellished with embroidery, sequins, or beads. Lehenga choli comes in different styles and designs, making it a popular choice for formal events.

Question and Answer

Q: Are sarees only worn by married women?
A: No, sarees can be worn by women of all ages and marital status.

Q: Can salwar kameez be worn without a dupatta?
A: Yes, salwar kameez can be worn without a dupatta, especially for everyday wear.

Q: Can lehenga choli be worn by women of all body types?
A: Yes, lehenga choli comes in different styles and designs that can flatter different body types.

Q: What is the difference between a saree and a lehenga choli?
A: A saree is a long piece of cloth draped around the body, while a lehenga choli consists of a long skirt and a blouse.

Conclusion of what are Indian Women's Clothes Called

Indian women's clothing is diverse and beautiful, and knowing what are Indian women's clothes called can help you appreciate and understand the culture better. The different types of clothing can be overwhelming, but with a little guidance, you can find the perfect outfit for any occasion. Whether it's a saree, salwar kameez, dupatta, or lehenga choli, each outfit has its unique style and beauty that can make any woman feel elegant and confident.
