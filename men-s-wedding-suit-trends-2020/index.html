Men s Wedding Suit Trends 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you getting married in 2020 and looking for the perfect suit to wear on your big day? Do you want to stay on top of the latest men's wedding suit trends? Look no further! In this guide, we'll give you an overview of the top trends for men's wedding suits in 2020.

Choosing the right suit for your wedding can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. You want to look stylish and modern, but also timeless and elegant.

The target of men's wedding suit trends 2020 is to help you navigate the latest styles and find the perfect suit for your wedding day. Whether you're looking for a classic tuxedo or a more relaxed suit, we've got you covered.

In this article, we'll cover the main trends for men's wedding suits in 2020, including colors, fabrics, and styles. We'll also provide tips on how to choose the right suit for your body type and the type of wedding you're having. So, let's get started!

Color Trends

One of the biggest trends for men's wedding suits in 2020 is the use of bold, bright colors. While traditional black and navy suits will always be in style, more and more grooms are opting for something a little more daring.

Burgundy is a popular choice for 2020. This rich, deep color adds a touch of sophistication and elegance to any suit. Other popular colors include emerald green, royal blue, and even shades of pink.

When choosing a color for your wedding suit, it's important to consider the color scheme of your wedding. You want your suit to complement the overall aesthetic, not clash with it.

Fabric Trends

The fabric you choose for your wedding suit is just as important as the color. In 2020, we're seeing a lot of lightweight, breathable fabrics such as linen and cotton.

These fabrics are perfect for warm weather weddings or outdoor ceremonies. They're comfortable and easy to wear, while still looking stylish and put-together.

For a more formal look, wool is always a classic choice. Look for lightweight wool fabrics that won't weigh you down or make you overheat.

Style Trends

When it comes to style, there are a few key trends for men's wedding suits in 2020. One of the biggest trends is a more relaxed, casual style. Grooms are opting for suits that are comfortable and easy to wear, rather than stiff and formal.

Another trend is the use of patterns and prints. From classic pinstripes to bold florals, patterns are a great way to add personality and style to your wedding suit.

Finally, we're seeing a lot of grooms ditching the tie in favor of a more casual, open-collar look. This is perfect for a beach or outdoor wedding, or for grooms who want to feel more comfortable and relaxed on their big day.

Choosing the Right Suit for Your Body Type

When choosing a wedding suit, it's important to consider your body type. Different styles and cuts will look better on different body shapes.

If you're tall and slim, a slim-fit suit will look great on you. If you're shorter or stockier, a more traditional, classic fit suit will be more flattering.

It's also important to consider any areas you want to highlight or downplay. For example, if you have broad shoulders, look for a suit with a wider lapel. If you have a larger midsection, opt for a suit with a higher button stance to elongate your torso.

How to Accessorize Your Wedding Suit

Accessories can make or break your wedding suit. Here are a few tips for accessorizing your suit in 2020:

  • Choose a statement watch or cufflinks to add a touch of luxury to your look.
  • Consider a patterned or colored pocket square to add interest to your suit.
  • Invest in a good pair of dress shoes that complement your suit.
  • Don't be afraid to add a pop of color with your socks or tie.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the most popular color for men's wedding suits in 2020?

A: Burgundy is a popular choice for 2020.

Q: What fabrics are popular for men's wedding suits in 2020?

A: Lightweight, breathable fabrics such as linen and cotton are popular choices for 2020.

Q: Can I wear a patterned suit to my wedding?

A: Absolutely! Patterns are a great way to add personality and style to your wedding suit.

Q: Should I wear a tie with my wedding suit?

A: It's up to you! A tie is a classic choice, but more and more grooms are opting for a more casual, open-collar look.

Conclusion of Men's Wedding Suit Trends 2020

Choosing the perfect wedding suit can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can find a suit that makes you look and feel your best. In 2020, we're seeing a lot of bold colors, lightweight fabrics, and more relaxed styles. When choosing a suit, consider your body type and the overall aesthetic of your wedding. And don't forget to accessorize! With these tips, you'll be sure to look stylish and modern on your big day.
