Best Luxury Footwear Brands In India

Written by Eveline Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the best luxury footwear brands in India? Do you want to elevate your fashion game with a pair of high-quality and stylish shoes? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the top luxury footwear brands in India that offer comfort, style, and durability.

If you are someone who values comfort, quality, and style when it comes to footwear, you know how hard it can be to find the perfect pair of shoes. Whether you are looking for dress shoes, casual shoes, or sports shoes, the market is saturated with options, making it overwhelming to choose the right one. Furthermore, if you are looking for luxury footwear brands, the options can be even more limited.

So, what are the best luxury footwear brands in India? Let's find out.

1. Hush Puppies

One of the most well-known luxury footwear brands in India is Hush Puppies. With a focus on comfort, style, and quality, Hush Puppies offer a wide range of shoes for men and women. From dress shoes to casual shoes, boots, and sandals, Hush Puppies have something for everyone. I personally own a pair of Hush Puppies dress shoes, and they are the most comfortable dress shoes I have ever worn. The leather is soft and supple, and the cushioned footbed provides all-day comfort.

2. Clarks

Another popular luxury footwear brand in India is Clarks. Known for their high-quality leather shoes, Clarks offer a range of shoes for men and women, including dress shoes, casual shoes, boots, and sandals. What sets Clarks apart is their commitment to sustainable and ethical practices. They use eco-friendly materials and ensure that their workers are treated fairly. I recently purchased a pair of Clarks boots, and I love how comfortable and stylish they are.

3. Bata

Bata is a well-established brand in India that offers a range of footwear options at different price points. However, they also have a luxury collection that offers high-quality leather shoes for men and women. The Bata luxury collection features elegant designs, premium materials, and superior craftsmanship. I have a pair of Bata dress shoes that I wear to formal events, and I always receive compliments on them.

4. Red Tape

Red Tape is a relatively new luxury footwear brand in India, but they have quickly gained popularity due to their trendy designs and high-quality materials. They offer a range of shoes for men, including dress shoes, casual shoes, and sports shoes. I recently purchased a pair of Red Tape sneakers, and they are the most comfortable sneakers I have ever owned.

5. Louis Philippe

Louis Philippe is a luxury fashion brand that offers a range of clothing, accessories, and footwear for men. Their footwear collection features elegant designs, premium materials, and superior craftsmanship. From dress shoes to casual shoes, Louis Philippe has something for every occasion. I own a pair of Louis Philippe dress shoes, and they are the perfect mix of style and comfort.

Conclusion of Best Luxury Footwear Brands in India

In conclusion, if you are looking for the best luxury footwear brands in India, you can't go wrong with Hush Puppies, Clarks, Bata, Red Tape, and Louis Philippe. These brands offer a range of shoes for men and women, including dress shoes, casual shoes, boots, and sandals. They prioritize comfort, quality, and style, so you can be sure that you are investing in a pair of shoes that will last you for years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which luxury footwear brand offers the most comfortable shoes?

A: Hush Puppies is known for their comfortable shoes, thanks to their cushioned footbeds and soft leather.

Q: Which luxury footwear brand is the most affordable?

A: Bata offers a luxury collection at a more affordable price point compared to other luxury brands.

Q: Which luxury footwear brand is the most sustainable?

A: Clarks is committed to sustainable and ethical practices, using eco-friendly materials and ensuring fair treatment of workers.

Q: Which luxury footwear brand offers the most stylish shoes?

A: Red Tape is known for their trendy designs and fashionable footwear.
