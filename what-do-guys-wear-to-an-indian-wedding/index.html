What Do Guys Wear To An Indian Wedding

Written by Bardi Sep 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Indian weddings are a grand affair that involve a lot of festivities, rituals, and ceremonies. One of the most crucial aspects of an Indian wedding is the attire. While women have a plethora of options to choose from, men often struggle with what to wear to an Indian wedding. If you're one of them, don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the best options for what guys wear to an Indian wedding.

Attending an Indian wedding can be overwhelming for men, especially if they are not familiar with Indian culture or traditions. The pressure to dress appropriately and look good can add to the confusion. Moreover, Indian weddings are known for their vibrant colors, intricate designs, and traditional attire. Hence, it can be challenging to find the right balance between style, comfort, and cultural appropriateness.

When it comes to an Indian wedding, there are a few things that men need to keep in mind. Firstly, the dress code is usually traditional, which means that Western wear such as suits and tuxedos are not suitable. Secondly, the attire should be comfortable and breathable as Indian weddings can last for several hours. Lastly, the color palette should be in sync with the wedding theme and the cultural significance of the event.

To summarize, the key points to remember while deciding what guys wear to an Indian wedding are that the attire should be traditional, comfortable, and in sync with the color palette.

What is the Best Attire for Men at an Indian Wedding?

As mentioned earlier, traditional attire is the way to go for men at an Indian wedding. The most popular options are the sherwani, kurta pajama, and bandhgala. The sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is worn with a kurta and churidar pajama. It is usually made of silk or brocade and is adorned with intricate embroidery or embellishments. The kurta pajama is a more casual option that consists of a long shirt and loose-fitting pants. It can be paired with a Nehru jacket or a waistcoat for added style. The bandhgala is a more modern take on the traditional attire and is similar to a suit jacket but with an Indian touch. It can be worn with a kurta or a shirt and pants.

What Colors Should Men Wear to an Indian Wedding?

The color palette for men's attire at an Indian wedding should be in sync with the wedding theme and cultural significance. It is best to stick to traditional colors such as gold, beige, maroon, and navy blue. Avoid wearing black or white as they are considered inauspicious in Indian culture. If you're unsure about the color scheme, you can always ask the bride or groom for guidance.

How to Accessorize for an Indian Wedding?

Accessories can add a touch of elegance and style to your Indian wedding attire. The most common accessories for men are the turban, safa, or pagdi, which are worn with the sherwani or bandhgala. They come in various colors and designs and can be matched with the attire or the wedding theme. Another popular accessory is the stole or scarf, which can be draped over the shoulders or around the neck. It is usually made of silk or cotton and can be embellished with embroidery or prints. Other accessories such as cufflinks, watches, and pocket squares can be added for a more refined look.

How to Dress for a Sangeet or Cocktail Party?

A sangeet or cocktail party is an informal event that precedes the wedding ceremony. Men can opt for a more casual look for these events. Indo-Western fusion wear such as the fusion kurta, Jodhpuri pants, or the dhoti pants with a shirt or a t-shirt are popular choices. You can also experiment with colors and prints for a more modern look. However, it is still important to keep the attire in sync with the wedding theme and cultural significance.

Conclusion of What Do Guys Wear to an Indian Wedding?

Attending an Indian wedding can be a memorable experience, and dressing appropriately can make it even more special. Traditional attire such as the sherwani, kurta pajama, and bandhgala are the best options for men at an Indian wedding. The color palette should be in sync with the wedding theme, and accessories can add a touch of elegance and style. For informal events such as the sangeet or cocktail party, Indo-Western fusion wear is the way to go. Keep these tips in mind, and you'll be sure to make a lasting impression at the wedding.

A: No, suits are not suitable for Indian weddings as the dress code is usually traditional. Men should opt for traditional attire such as the sherwani, kurta pajama, or bandhgala.

A: Yes, men should avoid wearing black or white as they are considered inauspicious in Indian culture.

A: The dress code for a sangeet or cocktail party is usually more casual than the wedding ceremony. Men can opt for Indo-Western fusion wear such as the fusion kurta, Jodhpuri pants, or the dhoti pants with a shirt or a t-shirt.
