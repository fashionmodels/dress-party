Cool Spots In Indianapolis A Guide To The Best Places To Visit

Written by Frank Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you find yourself wondering where to go in Indianapolis? Are you tired of visiting the same old tourist traps? Well, there are plenty of cool spots in Indianapolis that you may have never heard of before. Whether you're a local or a tourist, this guide will help you discover new and exciting places to visit in the city.

Indianapolis is home to a variety of unique and interesting locations. From museums to parks to restaurants, there is something for everyone in the city. This guide will explore the best cool spots in Indianapolis and provide you with all the information you need to plan your next adventure.

In this article, we will highlight some of the best cool spots in Indianapolis that you should add to your bucket list. We will cover the Indianapolis Museum of Art, The Canal Walk, The Eagle's Nest, and The Children's Museum of Indianapolis. Each of these locations offers a unique experience that will leave you wanting more.

Indianapolis Museum of Art

The Indianapolis Museum of Art is a must-visit for art lovers. With over 54,000 works of art from all around the world, the museum is a treasure trove of culture and history. The museum is also home to beautiful gardens and outdoor sculptures, which makes it a great place to relax and unwind.

I had the pleasure of visiting the museum last summer, and I was blown away by the variety of exhibits. The contemporary art collection was particularly impressive, and I spent hours exploring the different galleries. If you're looking for a unique and educational experience, the Indianapolis Museum of Art is definitely worth a visit.

The Canal Walk

The Canal Walk is a scenic six-mile loop that runs through downtown Indianapolis. The walkway is lined with beautiful gardens, fountains, and public art displays, making it a great place to take a stroll or go for a run.

During my visit to Indianapolis, I made sure to take a walk along the canal. The scenery was stunning, and I was able to see some of the city's most iconic landmarks from a new perspective. Whether you're looking for a peaceful escape or an active adventure, the Canal Walk is a cool spot in Indianapolis that everyone should experience.

The Eagle's Nest

The Eagle's Nest is a revolving restaurant on the 21st floor of the Hyatt Regency Indianapolis. The restaurant offers panoramic views of the city, making it the perfect spot for a romantic dinner or a special occasion.

I went to The Eagle's Nest for my anniversary, and I was blown away by the breathtaking views. The food was also delicious, and the service was impeccable. If you're looking for a unique dining experience, The Eagle's Nest is definitely worth a visit.

The Children's Museum of Indianapolis

The Children's Museum of Indianapolis is a fun and educational destination for families with children. The museum is home to over 120,000 artifacts and exhibits, including a dinosaur exhibit and a planetarium.

I took my nephew to the Children's Museum last summer, and he had a blast. The exhibits were interactive and engaging, and he was able to learn about history and science in a fun way. If you're looking for a cool spot in Indianapolis to take your kids, the Children's Museum is a must-visit.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Visit

If you want to get the most out of your visit to these cool spots in Indianapolis, here are a few tips:

  • Plan ahead and make reservations if necessary.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and clothing, especially if you plan on walking or hiking.
  • Bring a camera to capture the stunning views and unique experiences.
  • Take your time and enjoy the journey.

Conclusion of Cool Spots in Indianapolis

If you're looking for an adventure in Indianapolis, these cool spots should be at the top of your list. From art museums to scenic walks to revolving restaurants, there is something for everyone in this vibrant and diverse city. So, what are you waiting for? Start planning your trip to Indianapolis today!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best time to visit The Canal Walk?

A: The best time to visit The Canal Walk is during the spring or fall when the weather is mild and the foliage is at its peak.

Q: Is The Eagle's Nest expensive?

A: The Eagle's Nest is a fine dining restaurant, so it is more expensive than other restaurants in Indianapolis. However, the views and experience make it worth the price.

Q: Is the Children's Museum of Indianapolis suitable for all ages?

A: While the Children's Museum is geared towards families with children, there are exhibits and activities that are suitable for all ages.

Q: Is the Indianapolis Museum of Art free?

A: Admission to the Indianapolis Museum of Art is free, but some special exhibits may require a fee.
