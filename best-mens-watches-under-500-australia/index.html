How to Choose the Best Men's Watch Under $500 in Australia?

Question and Answer

Q: Can I find a good quality automatic watch under $500?

A: Yes, you can find many good quality automatic watches under $500. Seiko, Citizen, and Timex are some of the top brands that offer affordable automatic watches.

Q: Are leather strap watches durable?

A: Yes, leather strap watches can be durable if taken care of properly. It is recommended to clean and condition the leather regularly to keep it in good condition.

Q: What is the water resistance rating for everyday wear watches?

A: For everyday wear watches, a water resistance rating of 50m is sufficient. It means that the watch can withstand splashes of water, but it is not suitable for swimming or diving.

Q: Can I wear a casual watch to formal events?

A: It depends on the style of the watch and the dress code of the event. Generally, it is recommended to wear a dress watch to formal events, but if the event is casual, you can wear a casual watch.
