How to Choose the Right Men's Dress Shirt

Choosing the right men's dress shirt can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some factors to consider when shopping for a dress shirt:

1. Fit

The fit of a dress shirt is crucial for both comfort and style. It's important to choose a shirt that fits well in the shoulders, chest, and waist. Make sure to measure yourself before purchasing a dress shirt, or try it on in-store if possible.

2. Material

The material of a dress shirt can affect its comfort, durability, and appearance. Cotton is the most common material for dress shirts, but there are also options like linen, silk, and polyester. Consider the occasion and your personal preferences when choosing a material.

3. Style

The style of a dress shirt can vary depending on the collar, cuffs, and design. Choose a shirt that fits your personal style and the occasion. Classic styles like button-down collars and plain designs are versatile options that can be worn for any occasion.

FAQs About Men's Dress Shirts

1. What is the difference between a slim fit and a regular fit dress shirt?

A slim fit dress shirt is more fitted in the waist and chest, while a regular fit dress shirt is looser. It's important to choose a fit that is comfortable and fits your body type.

2. How should I care for my dress shirts?

It's important to follow the care instructions on the label of your dress shirt. Generally, dress shirts should be washed in cold water and hung to dry. Avoid using the dryer, as this can cause shrinkage and damage to the fabric.

3. Can I wear a patterned dress shirt to a formal event?

Yes, you can wear a patterned dress shirt to a formal event. However, it's important to choose a subtle pattern and pair it with a solid-colored suit and tie.

4. How many dress shirts should I own?

It's recommended to own at least three dress shirts in neutral colors like white, blue, and gray. This will give you versatility and ensure that you always have a clean shirt for any occasion.

Conclusion of Best Men's Dress Shirt 2021

Our top picks for 2021 offer a range of options for different body types and style preferences. Remember to consider factors like fit, material, and style when choosing a dress shirt, and always follow the care instructions to ensure longevity and durability.
