Where To Get Cargo Pants A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you find yourself constantly searching for the perfect pair of cargo pants? Are you tired of going from store to store with no luck? Finding the right cargo pants can be a challenge, but with the right information, it doesn't have to be.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to finding cargo pants is knowing where to look. Department stores may have a limited selection, and online shopping can be overwhelming with so many options to choose from.

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of cargo pants, there are a few options to consider. You can visit outdoor retailers, military surplus stores, or browse online retailers for the best selection.

In summary, when searching for cargo pants, it's best to explore a variety of options, including outdoor retailers, military surplus stores, and online retailers.

Outdoor Retailers

Outdoor retailers such as REI and Bass Pro Shops are a great place to start when looking for cargo pants. These stores specialize in outdoor gear and often carry a wide selection of cargo pants in varying styles and colors. They also tend to carry high-quality brands known for their durability and functionality, such as Columbia and The North Face.

Personally, I have had great success finding cargo pants at REI. Not only do they carry a wide range of sizes, but their staff is always knowledgeable and helpful in finding the right fit and style for my needs.

Military Surplus Stores

Military surplus stores are another great option for finding cargo pants. These stores specialize in surplus military gear, which often includes cargo pants. The benefit of shopping at a military surplus store is that the gear is often high-quality and designed for durability in rough conditions.

One military surplus store I have had success with is Army Navy Sales. They have a wide selection of cargo pants, from classic military styles to more modern designs. Plus, their prices are often more affordable than other retailers.

Online Retailers

If you prefer the convenience of online shopping, there are plenty of online retailers that specialize in cargo pants. Sites such as Amazon and Zappos carry a wide range of brands and styles, making it easy to find the perfect pair from the comfort of your own home.

One thing to keep in mind when shopping online is to check the sizing chart carefully. Different brands may have different sizing, so it's important to measure yourself and compare to the chart to ensure a proper fit.

Customized Cargo Pants

If you're looking for a truly unique pair of cargo pants, consider getting them customized. Several online retailers, such as Customized Girl and Zazzle, offer the ability to design your own cargo pants. This allows you to choose the style, color, and even add your own design or logo.

One thing to keep in mind when ordering customized cargo pants is that they may have a longer wait time for delivery. However, the end result can be well worth the wait.

Question and Answer

Q: Are cargo pants only for outdoor activities?

A: No, cargo pants can be worn for a variety of activities and occasions. They are great for outdoor activities such as hiking and camping, but can also be worn for casual outings or even in the workplace depending on the dress code.

Q: What material are cargo pants typically made of?

A: Cargo pants can be made of a variety of materials, but are typically made of cotton or a cotton blend. This allows for comfort and breathability, while still being durable enough for outdoor activities.

Q: Can cargo pants be dressed up for formal occasions?

A: While cargo pants are typically seen as casual wear, they can be dressed up for formal occasions depending on the dress code. Pairing them with a button-up shirt and dress shoes can elevate the look and make them appropriate for events such as weddings.

Q: How do I care for my cargo pants?

A: It's important to follow the care instructions on the label of your cargo pants, but generally they can be washed in cold water and tumble dried on low heat. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the material.

Conclusion of Where to Get Cargo Pants

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of cargo pants, it's important to explore a variety of options. Outdoor retailers, military surplus stores, and online retailers all offer a wide selection of styles and brands to choose from. By considering your needs and preferences, you can find the perfect pair of cargo pants to suit your lifestyle.
