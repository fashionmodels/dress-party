Nike is a popular brand among sports enthusiasts, known for its high-quality athletic shoes, apparel, and accessories. I have used Puma shoes for running and found them to be comfortable and durable. Puma also offers products for sports such as soccer, golf, and basketball.

Under Armour is a relatively new brand but has gained popularity for its high-quality sports equipment and apparel.
