Tips for Getting the Perfect Fit

When trying on dress shirts, there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure you get the perfect fit. First, make sure the shirt fits well in the shoulders. The shoulder seam should sit right at the edge of your shoulder, not too far in or too far out.
