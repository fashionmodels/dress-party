Discover The Best Vintage Clothing Shops In Edinburgh

Written by Robby Aug 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same mass-produced clothing found in every high street shop? Do you crave unique pieces that express your individuality? Look no further than vintage clothing shops in Edinburgh. From quirky and eclectic to classic and timeless, Edinburgh has a variety of vintage shops that cater to all tastes and budgets.

If you're someone who loves to stand out from the crowd and make bold fashion statements, then vintage clothing is the perfect choice for you. Vintage clothing shops in Edinburgh offer a diverse range of styles, including retro, bohemian, punk, and vintage designer pieces. But with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

The aim of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best vintage clothing shops in Edinburgh. We'll cover everything from the most popular stores to hidden gems, and provide personal experiences and insider tips to make your shopping experience as enjoyable as possible.

Armstrong's Vintage Emporium

Located in the heart of Edinburgh's Grassmarket, Armstrong's Vintage Emporium is one of the city's most popular vintage shops. This spacious store offers a wide range of vintage clothing, accessories and homeware, spanning from the 1920s to the 1990s. The staff are knowledgeable and friendly, and the prices are reasonable, making Armstrong's a must-visit for any vintage lover.

Godiva Boutique

If you're looking for something a little more high-end, Godiva Boutique is the place to go. This luxury vintage shop offers a curated selection of designer pieces, including Chanel, Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent. The shop is beautifully presented, with a chic and sophisticated atmosphere, making it feel like you're shopping in a boutique rather than a vintage store.

The Edinburgh Vintage Emporium

The Edinburgh Vintage Emporium is a treasure trove of vintage finds. This large store is divided into different sections, each with its own unique theme. From vintage clothing to vinyl records, antique furniture to retro toys, there's something for everyone at this eclectic emporium.

How to Shop for Vintage Clothing in Edinburgh

Shopping for vintage clothing can be overwhelming, especially if you're new to the game. Here are a few tips to help you make the most of your vintage shopping experience:

1. Know your measurements – Vintage clothing sizes can differ greatly from modern sizes, so it's important to know your measurements before you start shopping.

2. Take your time – Vintage stores are often packed with unique pieces, so take your time to explore and don't be afraid to dig through the racks.

3. Try everything on – Vintage clothing can be tricky to fit, so it's important to try everything on before you buy.

4. Don't be afraid to haggle – Vintage stores often expect you to haggle, so don't be afraid to ask for a better price.

Conclusion of Best Vintage Clothing Shops Edinburgh

Edinburgh is a treasure trove of vintage clothing shops, offering a diverse range of styles and price points. From high-end designer pieces to quirky retro finds, there's something for everyone in this vibrant and eclectic city. So why not step outside of your comfort zone and embrace the unique and individualistic world of vintage fashion?

Question and Answer

Q: Is vintage clothing expensive?

A: Vintage clothing prices can vary greatly depending on the store and the item. However, many vintage stores offer affordable options, and you can often find great deals by digging through the racks.

Q: How do I know if a vintage piece is worth buying?

A: Look for high-quality fabrics and construction, and make sure the piece is in good condition. It's also important to consider if the piece fits well and if it suits your personal style.

Q: Can I sell my vintage clothing to vintage shops?

A: Some vintage shops do buy vintage clothing from individuals, but it's best to contact the store beforehand to see if they're interested and what their buying process is.

Q: How do I care for vintage clothing?

A: Vintage clothing should be treated with care, as many pieces are fragile and delicate. Always read the care label before washing or dry cleaning, and store your vintage pieces in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
