Floyd Mayweather vs. Manny Pacquiao

One of the most highly anticipated fights in boxing history was Floyd Mayweather vs. Manny Pacquiao. The fight generated over $600 million in revenue, making it the highest grossing boxing match of all time.

Floyd Mayweather vs. Conor McGregor

Another highly anticipated fight was Floyd Mayweather vs. Conor McGregor. The fight, which took place in 2017, pitted the undefeated boxing champion against the brash and confident MMA fighter. I was not a big fan of McGregor before the fight, but I have to admit that he put up a good fight against Mayweather. In the end, Mayweather emerged victorious, but McGregor earned the respect of many boxing fans.

Oscar De La Hoya vs. Floyd Mayweather

Oscar De La Hoya vs. Floyd Mayweather was another highly anticipated fight, and it took place in 2007. The fight generated over $136 million in revenue, making it one of the highest grossing boxing matches of all time.

De La Hoya was one of the biggest names in boxing at the time, and Mayweather was a rising star. The fight was billed as a battle between youth and experience, and it did not disappoint. Mayweather emerged victorious, but De La Hoya put up a good fight and proved that he was still a force to be reckoned with.

Evander Holyfield vs. Mike Tyson II

The rematch between Evander Holyfield and Mike Tyson was one of the most highly anticipated fights of the 90s.
