How to Care for Rare and Valuable Cards

Collectors who own rare and valuable cards like the Honus Wagner must take great care to preserve them. Exposure to light, heat, and moisture can all damage cards, as can improper handling and storage. To keep cards in top condition, collectors should store them in acid-free sleeves and top-loaders, and keep them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. It's also important to handle cards with clean hands and avoid bending or creasing them.
