Top Indian Clothing Brands: FabIndia

One of the top Indian clothing brands is FabIndia, which is known for its high-quality, authentic, and ethically-sourced clothing. The brand was founded in 1960 and has since become one of India's most popular clothing brands. FabIndia's target audience is individuals who appreciate traditional Indian clothing and want to showcase the vibrant culture of India through their clothing.

Another top Indian clothing brand is W, which is known for its trendy and affordable clothing options for women.
