Bridesmaid Dresses Price In Sri Lanka Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for bridesmaid dresses in Sri Lanka but worried about the prices? You're not alone. Many bridesmaids-to-be face the same concern when searching for the perfect dress. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we will guide you through everything you need to know about bridesmaid dresses price in Sri Lanka.

When it comes to bridesmaid dresses, finding the perfect one can be stressful, especially if you're on a tight budget. You want to look your best on your friend's big day without breaking the bank. It's essential to find affordable options that fit your style and budget. In Sri Lanka, bridesmaid dresses' prices can vary, depending on various factors such as material, design, and brand.

The average price range for a bridesmaid dress in Sri Lanka is between LKR 10,000 to LKR 30,000. However, the cost can go up to LKR 50,000 or more for designer dresses. The price also depends on the number of bridesmaids and the type of dress that the bride chooses for her bridal party.

In summary, when it comes to bridesmaid dresses price in Sri Lanka, you can expect to pay between LKR 10,000 to LKR 30,000 on average. However, the cost can vary depending on the factors mentioned above.

Where to Find Affordable Bridesmaid Dresses in Sri Lanka?

Personally, I had trouble finding affordable bridesmaid dresses for my friend's wedding. I didn't want to spend too much money on a dress that I would only wear a few times. After searching for hours, I finally found some great options at online stores like Kapruka and Myshop.lk. They offer a wide range of bridesmaid dresses at affordable prices that fit my style and budget. I highly recommend checking them out.

Factors that Affect Bridesmaid Dresses Price in Sri Lanka

Several factors can impact bridesmaid dresses' prices in Sri Lanka, such as:

Material

The type of material used in the dress can significantly affect its price. Dresses made of high-quality materials like silk, chiffon, or lace are more expensive than those made from synthetic fabrics.

Design

The dress's design is another factor that can affect its price. Designer dresses are often more expensive than non-designer dresses. Dresses with intricate details like beading and embroidery also tend to be pricier.

How to Save Money on Bridesmaid Dresses in Sri Lanka

If you're on a tight budget, there are several ways to save money on bridesmaid dresses in Sri Lanka, such as:

1. Shop during the off-season when prices are lower.

2. Look for sales and discounts.

3. Consider renting dresses instead of buying them.

4. Choose simple designs that are less expensive.

Conclusion of Bridesmaid Dresses Price in Sri Lanka

Now that you know everything about bridesmaid dresses price in Sri Lanka, you can start shopping with confidence. Remember to consider the factors that affect the price and use the tips we've provided to save money. With the right dress, you'll look stunning on your friend's big day without breaking the bank.
