Are you a fashion enthusiast who loves to score great deals? Do you find it challenging to find one-stop-shop destinations for all your shopping needs? If yes, then you're in luck! Top Fashion Stores to Check Out at Aurora Outlets

As a fashion enthusiast, one of the main reasons to visit Aurora Outlets is the wide range of fashion stores available. From trendy clothing to chic accessories, the mall has it all. Here are a few stores that you should check out:

1. Banana Republic Factory

A personal favorite of mine, Banana Republic Factory is a go-to destination for all your classic clothing needs. The store offers a wide range of clothing, including workwear, casual wear, and formal wear. You can also find accessories such as shoes, bags, and jewelry at the store.

2. Nike Factory Store

For all the fitness enthusiasts out there, Nike Factory Store is a must-visit. The store offers a wide range of athletic wear, including shoes, clothing, and accessories. Whether you're a runner, a gym-goer, or a yoga lover, you'll find something to suit your needs at the store.

3. Coach Outlet

If you're a lover of luxury handbags and accessories, then Coach Outlet is the store for you. The store offers a wide range of high-end handbags, wallets, and accessories at discounted prices.

4. Calvin Klein

For all your underwear and loungewear needs, head over to Calvin Klein. The store offers a wide range of underwear, sleepwear, and loungewear for both men and women. You can also find a range of clothing and accessories at the store.

Home Decor Stores to Check Out at Aurora Outlets

Aside from fashion stores, Aurora Outlets also has a wide range of home decor stores that cater to all your interior design needs. Whether you're looking for furniture, bedding, or decorative items, these stores have got you covered. Here are a few stores that you should check out:

1. Pottery Barn Outlet

If you're a lover of classic and elegant home decor, then Pottery Barn Outlet is the store for you. The store offers a wide range of furniture, bedding, and decorative items that cater to a classic and timeless aesthetic.

2. Yankee Candle Outlet

For all the candle lovers out there, Yankee Candle Outlet is a must-visit. The store offers a wide range of scented candles, candle holders, and other accessories that cater to all your candle needs.

3. Bed Bath & Beyond

For all your household needs, head over to Bed Bath & Beyond. The store offers a wide range of bedding, bath, and kitchen essentials that cater to all your home needs.

FAQs About Aurora Outlets

Q: What are Aurora Outlets' opening hours?

A: Aurora Outlets' opening hours vary depending on the day of the week. However, the mall is generally open from 10 am to 9 pm on weekdays and Saturdays, and from 10 am to 7 pm on Sundays.

Q: Is there a food court at Aurora Outlets?

A: Yes, Aurora Outlets has a food court that offers a wide range of food options, including fast food, Mexican, Italian, and Asian cuisine.

Q: Does Aurora Outlets have a parking lot?

A: Yes, Aurora Outlets has a large parking lot that offers free parking for all shoppers.

Q: Are there any kids' stores at Aurora Outlets?

A: Yes, Aurora Outlets has a few kids' stores, including The Children's Place, Carter's, and OshKosh B'gosh.

Conclusion

Overall, Aurora Outlets is a shopping paradise that caters to all your fashion and lifestyle needs. Whether you're looking for high-end designer stores or affordable fashion brands, the mall has it all. From fashion to home decor, there's something for everyone at Aurora Outlets. So, gather your friends and family, and head over to Aurora Outlets for a fun-filled shopping spree.
