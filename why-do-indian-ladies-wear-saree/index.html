How to Drape a Saree

Draping a saree can seem daunting at first, but with practice, it becomes easy. There are different ways to drape a saree, depending on the occasion and personal preference. The most common way to drape a saree is the Nivi style, which involves pleating the saree and tucking it into the waistband. The pallu, or the loose end of the saree, can be draped over the shoulder or the head, depending on the occasion.

There are also regional variations in saree draping styles, such as the Bengali style, Gujarati style, and Maharashtrian style. Each style has its unique charm and adds to the beauty of saree.
