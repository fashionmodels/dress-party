How To Choose High Quality Leather Shoes For Men

Written by Christine Jul 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you struggle to find high quality leather shoes that are both comfortable and stylish? Are you tired of buying shoes that fall apart after just a few wears? Finding the right pair of shoes can be a challenge, but with the right knowledge, you can make an informed decision that will help you find the perfect pair of high quality leather shoes for men.

When it comes to high quality leather shoes for men, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider the type of leather used in the shoe. Full-grain leather is the highest quality and most durable, while top-grain leather is a close second. Avoid shoes made from corrected-grain leather or bonded leather, as these materials are less durable and prone to cracking and peeling over time.

In addition to the type of leather, pay attention to the construction of the shoe. Look for shoes that are welted or stitched, rather than glued, as this will ensure that the shoe is more durable and can be resoled when needed. The sole of the shoe should also be made from high-quality materials, such as leather or rubber.

In summary, when choosing high quality leather shoes for men, look for shoes made from full-grain or top-grain leather that are welted or stitched and have a high-quality sole. Avoid shoes made from corrected-grain or bonded leather, as these materials are less durable.

The Importance of Proper Fit

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing high quality leather shoes for men is proper fit. Ill-fitting shoes can cause discomfort, blisters, and even foot pain over time. When trying on shoes, make sure to wear the type of socks you would normally wear with the shoes and walk around the store to ensure a proper fit.

For a more personalized fit, consider visiting a shoe store that specializes in custom-made shoes. This will ensure that the shoes are made to fit your feet perfectly and can provide a higher level of comfort and support.

Caring for Your High Quality Leather Shoes

Proper care is essential to maintaining the quality and durability of your high quality leather shoes. Regularly clean and condition your shoes to prevent dirt and dust buildup, and use a quality leather protector to keep the leather supple and moisturized.

When storing your shoes, use a shoe tree to help maintain the shape of the shoe and prevent creases from forming. Avoid exposing leather shoes to extreme temperatures or moisture, as this can cause the leather to dry out or crack.

The Benefits of High Quality Leather Shoes

There are many benefits to investing in high quality leather shoes for men. Firstly, they are more durable and long-lasting than cheaper alternatives, which can save you money in the long run. They also provide better support and comfort for your feet, which can help prevent foot pain and other issues.

In addition, high quality leather shoes are more versatile and can be worn with a wider range of outfits and styles. They also tend to look better with age, as the leather develops a unique patina over time.

How to Break In Your High Quality Leather Shoes

Breaking in a new pair of high quality leather shoes can take some time, but it's important to do it properly to ensure a comfortable fit. Start by wearing the shoes for short periods of time, gradually increasing the length of wear over several days or weeks. This will allow the leather to mold to the shape of your foot.

You can also use a leather conditioner or shoe cream to soften the leather and make it more pliable. Apply the conditioner or cream to the inside of the shoe and wear it for several hours to help break it in.

Choosing the Right Style

When it comes to high quality leather shoes for men, there are many different styles to choose from. From dress shoes to boots to casual loafers, it's important to choose a style that fits your needs and personal style.

Consider the occasion and dress code when choosing a style, as well as the color and material of the shoe. A black leather dress shoe, for example, is a classic and versatile option that can be worn with a suit or dress pants, while a brown leather boot is a more casual option that pairs well with jeans and a sweater.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high quality leather shoes worth the investment?

A: Yes, high quality leather shoes are worth the investment as they are more durable and long-lasting than cheaper alternatives.

Q: How often should I clean and condition my leather shoes?

A: It's recommended to clean and condition your leather shoes every 6-8 weeks, or more frequently if they are exposed to dirt, dust, or moisture.

Q: Can high quality leather shoes be repaired?

A: Yes, high quality leather shoes can be repaired by a professional cobbler. They can be resoled, reconditioned, and even re-stitched if necessary.

Q: How long do high quality leather shoes last?

A: With proper care and maintenance, high quality leather shoes can last for many years, even up to a decade or more.

Conclusion of High Quality Leather Shoes for Men

Investing in high quality leather shoes for men is a wise decision that can provide many benefits, including durability, comfort, and style. When choosing a pair of shoes, consider the type of leather, construction, and fit, and make sure to care for your shoes properly to ensure a long lifespan. With the right pair of high quality leather shoes, you can step out in style and confidence for years to come.
