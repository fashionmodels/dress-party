Best Green Companies To Invest In 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 06, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for sustainable investment options? Do you want to invest in companies that prioritize environmental health and social responsibility? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then this guide on the best green companies to invest in 2021 is for you.

The rising concerns about climate change and the depletion of natural resources have led to a growing demand for sustainable investments. Green companies are those that prioritize sustainability in their business models, products, and services. These companies are not only profitable but also make a positive impact on the environment and society.

Investing in green companies not only benefits the environment but also yields high returns. Sustainable investments outperformed traditional investments in recent years. According to a Morningstar report, the majority of sustainable funds outperformed their peers in 2020.

If you are interested in investing in green companies, here are some options you can consider:

1. Tesla

Tesla is a leading electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer that aims to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. Tesla's mission is to create a sustainable energy ecosystem that reduces the world's dependence on fossil fuels. Tesla's market capitalization has skyrocketed in the past year, making it one of the most valuable car manufacturers in the world.

2. Beyond Meat

Beyond Meat is a plant-based meat alternative company that aims to reduce the environmental impact of animal agriculture. The company's products are made from plant-based ingredients that mimic the taste and texture of meat. Beyond Meat's market share has grown significantly in recent years, and the company has partnered with major fast-food chains such as McDonald's and KFC.

3. Brookfield Renewable Partners

Brookfield Renewable Partners is a renewable energy company that develops, owns, and operates renewable power assets. The company operates hydroelectric, wind, solar, and storage facilities across North and South America, Europe, and Asia. Brookfield Renewable Partners aims to provide clean and reliable energy while delivering high returns to investors.

4. NextEra Energy

NextEra Energy is a leading clean energy company that generates and distributes electricity from renewable sources. The company operates solar and wind energy facilities across the United States and Canada. NextEra Energy aims to provide affordable and reliable clean energy while reducing carbon emissions.

Q&A Section

Q: Why should I invest in green companies?

A: Investing in green companies not only benefits the environment but also yields high returns. Sustainable investments outperformed traditional investments in recent years.

Q: Which green companies should I invest in?

A: Some of the best green companies to invest in 2021 include Tesla, Beyond Meat, Brookfield Renewable Partners, and NextEra Energy. However, it's important to conduct thorough research before making any investment decisions.

Q: Are green companies profitable?

A: Yes, green companies have proven to be profitable. Many sustainable investments have outperformed traditional investments in recent years.

Q: How can I invest in green companies?

A: You can invest in green companies through sustainable mutual funds, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), or individual stocks. It's important to consult with a financial advisor before making any investment decisions.

Conclusion of Best Green Companies to Invest in 2021

Investing in green companies is not only a wise financial decision but also an ethical and responsible choice. The companies listed above are just a few of the many green investment options available. Before making any investment decisions, it's important to do your research and consult with a financial advisor. By investing in green companies, you can make a positive impact on the environment and society while yielding high returns.
