Ebira traditional attire is called "Ohire" or "Aneku Aneva". It is a colorful and vibrant outfit that consists of a long-sleeved shirt, a pair of pants, and a hat for men, while women wear a wrapper, blouse, headgear, and accessories like beads and earrings.
