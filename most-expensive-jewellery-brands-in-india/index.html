Discovering The Most Expensive Jewellery Brands In India

Written by Aprilia Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India has a rich heritage of jewellery making that dates back centuries. From traditional Kundan and Polki to modern diamond and gold jewellery, India has it all. However, with the rise of luxury brands, the jewellery industry in India has witnessed a significant transformation. In this blog post, we will explore the most expensive jewellery brands in India.

When it comes to buying jewellery, most people look for quality, craftsmanship, and unique designs. However, some people are willing to pay a premium for luxury brands that offer exclusivity and status. The most expensive jewellery brands in India cater to this niche segment of the market.

The target of this blog post is to provide information about the most expensive jewellery brands in India, their unique selling propositions, and what makes them stand out from other brands.

In summary, we will be discussing the most expensive jewellery brands in India, their price range, target audience, and what makes them unique.

Tanishq

Tanishq is one of the most popular jewellery brands in India, known for its exquisite designs and craftsmanship. The brand offers a wide range of jewellery, including gold, diamond, and Kundan. Tanishq's target audience is the modern Indian woman who values tradition and contemporary designs. The brand's price range starts from INR 10,000 and goes up to INR 50 lakhs.

I remember my sister's wedding, where she wore a beautiful Kundan necklace from Tanishq. The intricate design and craftsmanship of the necklace were breathtaking. It was undoubtedly the highlight of her bridal look. Tanishq's jewellery is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity, making it a favourite among Indian women.

Nirav Modi

Nirav Modi is a luxury jewellery brand that caters to the affluent segment of the market. The brand is known for its exquisite diamond jewellery, including engagement rings and necklaces. Nirav Modi's jewellery is crafted by skilled artisans, and the brand uses only the finest diamonds in its creations. The brand's price range starts from INR 5 lakhs and goes up to INR 20 crores.

My friend's husband gifted her a Nirav Modi engagement ring, and it was a sight to behold. The sparkling diamond and unique design made it stand out from other engagement rings. Nirav Modi's jewellery is a symbol of luxury and status, making it a favourite among the elite.

Sunita Shekhawat

Sunita Shekhawat is a Jaipur-based jewellery designer known for her exquisite enamel work. The brand offers a wide range of jewellery, including chokers, necklaces, and earrings. Sunita Shekhawat's jewellery is a fusion of traditional Indian designs and modern aesthetics. The brand's price range starts from INR 50,000 and goes up to INR 25 lakhs.

I had the pleasure of attending a friend's wedding, where she wore a Sunita Shekhawat choker. The intricate enamel work and unique design of the choker were awe-inspiring. Sunita Shekhawat's jewellery is a perfect blend of tradition and innovation, making it a favourite among young Indian women.

Amrapali

Amrapali is a luxury jewellery brand that draws inspiration from India's rich heritage. The brand offers a wide range of jewellery, including gold, silver, and gemstone. Amrapali's jewellery is crafted by skilled artisans, and the brand uses only the finest materials in its creations. The brand's price range starts from INR 10,000 and goes up to INR 50 lakhs.

My mother is a big fan of Amrapali's jewellery. She owns a few pieces of their silver jewellery, and she loves the intricate designs and craftsmanship. Amrapali's jewellery is a celebration of India's rich heritage and culture, making it a favourite among art and history enthusiasts.

Conclusion of Most Expensive Jewellery Brands in India

The most expensive jewellery brands in India cater to a niche segment of the market that values exclusivity and status. These brands offer unique designs, exquisite craftsmanship, and the finest materials, making them stand out from other brands. Tanishq, Nirav Modi, Sunita Shekhawat, and Amrapali are some of the most popular and sought-after jewellery brands in India.
