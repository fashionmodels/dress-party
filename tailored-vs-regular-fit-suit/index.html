Tailored Vs Regular Fit Suit Which One Is Right For You

Written by Bardi Sep 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you confused about whether to go for a tailored or regular fit suit? Choosing the right fit is essential to look your best and feel comfortable. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is right for you. In this article, we will explore the differences between tailored vs regular fit suits to help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to suits, the fit is crucial. Ill-fitting suits can make you look sloppy and unprofessional. On the other hand, a well-fitted suit can enhance your appearance and boost your confidence. Therefore, choosing the right fit is crucial, and it largely depends on your body type, personal preference, and the occasion.

The target of tailored vs regular fit suit is to find the best fit for your body type and style. A tailored fit suit is custom-made and tailored to your specific measurements. It fits closely to your body and accentuates your shape, providing a sleek and modern look. On the other hand, a regular fit suit is a standard size that fits most body types. It offers a more relaxed fit, with a looser cut and more room for movement.

In summary, the main points to consider when choosing between tailored vs regular fit suits are your body type, personal style, and the occasion. Tailored fit suits are ideal for formal occasions or those who prefer a modern and sleek look, while regular fit suits are perfect for those who prioritize comfort and mobility.

Tailored Fit Suit

A few years ago, I had my first experience with a tailored fit suit, and I have never looked back since. The suit was made to my exact measurements, and it fit like a glove. The suit hugged my body in all the right places, providing a sharp and modern look. It was perfect for the formal event I was attending, and I received many compliments on how well the suit fit me.

Tailored fit suits are a great choice for those who want a more modern and sleek look. The suit is made to measure, ensuring that it fits perfectly to your body shape. This type of suit is ideal for formal occasions, such as weddings, business meetings, or black-tie events. It provides a sharp and tailored appearance, making you look confident and sophisticated.

Regular Fit Suit

Before I discovered tailored fit suits, I used to wear regular fit suits. While they were comfortable, they did not fit me as well as I would have liked. The suit jacket was often too loose, and the trousers were too baggy. However, I still wear regular fit suits on occasions when I prioritize comfort and mobility over a sharp and modern look.

Regular fit suits are a great option for those who want to prioritize comfort and mobility. They are a standard size that fits most body types, and they offer a looser cut and more room for movement. They are perfect for everyday wear, such as work or casual events.

How To Choose The Right Fit

The best way to choose the right fit is to consider your body type, personal style, and the occasion. If you are attending a formal event, such as a wedding or business meeting, a tailored fit suit is an excellent choice. It will provide a sharp and modern look, making you look confident and sophisticated. On the other hand, if you prioritize comfort and mobility, a regular fit suit is a better option.

How To Get Your Suit Tailored

If you decide to go for a tailored fit suit, the best way to get it is to visit a tailor. A tailor will take your measurements and make a suit that fits you perfectly. You can choose the fabric, color, and style of the suit, ensuring that it suits your personal style and the occasion. While a tailored fit suit may be more expensive than a regular fit suit, it is worth the investment, as it will last longer and provide a more refined look.

Tailored Vs Regular Fit Suit: Which One To Choose?

Choosing between tailored vs regular fit suits depends on your body type, personal style, and the occasion. If you want a sharp and modern look, a tailored fit suit is the way to go. It will be custom-made to your exact measurements, ensuring a perfect fit. On the other hand, if you prioritize comfort and mobility, a regular fit suit is a better choice. It offers a looser cut and more room for movement.

Question and Answer

Q. Is a tailored fit suit more expensive than a regular fit suit?

A. Yes, a tailored fit suit is usually more expensive than a regular fit suit. This is because it is custom-made to your specific measurements, and it requires more time and effort to make.

Q. Can I wear a tailored fit suit for everyday wear?

A. While a tailored fit suit is ideal for formal occasions, such as weddings and business meetings, it can also be worn for everyday wear. However, a regular fit suit may be more comfortable for everyday wear, as it offers more room for movement.

Q. How do I know if a suit fits me well?

A. A suit should fit you well without any wrinkles or creases. The jacket should fit snugly around your shoulders and chest, and the sleeves should be the correct length. The trousers should sit comfortably on your waist, with no sagging or bunching.

Q. Can I alter a regular fit suit to fit me better?

A. Yes, you can alter a regular fit suit to fit you better. You can take it to a tailor, who can make adjustments to the jacket and trousers to provide a better fit. However, it may not fit you as well as a custom-made tailored fit suit.

Conclusion of Tailored Vs Regular Fit Suit

Choosing the right fit between tailored vs regular fit suit is crucial to look your best and feel comfortable. It largely depends on your body type, personal style, and the occasion. A tailored fit suit is ideal for formal occasions and those who want a modern and sleek look, while a regular fit suit is perfect for everyday wear and those who prioritize comfort and mobility. Ultimately, the right fit is the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
