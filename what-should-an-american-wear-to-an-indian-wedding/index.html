What Should An American Wear To An Indian Wedding

Written by Christine Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Attending an Indian wedding can be an exciting and unique experience for Americans. However, it can also be overwhelming when it comes to deciding what to wear. Indian weddings are known for their vibrant and colorful outfits, which can make it difficult for Americans to determine what is appropriate attire. Additionally, cultural differences and expectations can add to the confusion. This article will provide guidance on what Americans should wear to an Indian wedding.

One of the main pain points for Americans attending an Indian wedding is understanding the cultural norms and expectations. It can be challenging to determine what is considered appropriate attire for the occasion. Additionally, guests may feel overwhelmed by the vast array of colors and styles present at Indian weddings.

The appropriate attire for an American attending an Indian wedding is typically a traditional Indian outfit. For men, this means wearing a kurta pajama or a sherwani. Women should wear a saree or a salwar kameez. It is important to note that these outfits should be colorful and vibrant to match the atmosphere of an Indian wedding. If you are unsure about what to wear, do not hesitate to ask the bride or groom for guidance.

In summary, Americans attending an Indian wedding should wear traditional Indian outfits that are colorful and vibrant. It is important to understand the cultural norms and expectations of the occasion to ensure that your attire is appropriate. If you are unsure about what to wear, don’t be afraid to ask for guidance.

Choosing the Right Outfit

Choosing the right outfit for an Indian wedding can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. I attended an Indian wedding last year and was unsure of what to wear. I decided to go with a saree, which is a traditional Indian outfit for women. The saree I chose was a vibrant pink color with gold embellishments. I paired it with a matching blouse and gold jewelry. I received many compliments on my outfit throughout the wedding.

When choosing an outfit for an Indian wedding, it is essential to consider the color and style. The outfit should be colorful and vibrant to match the atmosphere of the wedding. Additionally, it should be comfortable to wear as weddings can be long events. Pay attention to the dress code mentioned in the invitation or consult with the bride or groom to avoid any confusion.

Accessories

Accessories are an essential part of an Indian outfit. For women, it is common to wear bangles, earrings, and necklaces. Gold jewelry is a popular choice for Indian weddings. It is essential to keep in mind that the jewelry should complement the outfit and not overpower it. For men, a turban or a dupatta can be worn to add to the outfit.

Makeup and Hair

Makeup and hair are an essential part of an Indian outfit. For women, it is common to wear bold makeup with a focus on the eyes. The hair can be styled in a traditional bun or left loose with accessories. Men can style their hair in a neat and clean look.

Where to Shop

If you are looking to purchase a traditional Indian outfit, there are many options available. Local Indian boutiques or online stores are great places to start. Additionally, some cities have Indian districts that specialize in Indian clothing and accessories.

Personal Experience

Attending an Indian wedding was a unique experience for me. The colors, music, and food were all memorable. However, choosing the right outfit was a challenge. I decided to go with a traditional saree, which was comfortable and stylish. I received many compliments on my outfit throughout the wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a western outfit to an Indian wedding?

A: It is recommended to wear a traditional Indian outfit to an Indian wedding. However, if you are unsure, it is best to consult with the bride or groom.

Q: What color should I avoid wearing to an Indian wedding?

A: It is best to avoid wearing white or black to an Indian wedding as they are traditionally associated with funerals.

Q: Can I wear jeans to an Indian wedding?

A: It is not appropriate to wear jeans to an Indian wedding. Guests are expected to dress formally and wear traditional Indian outfits.

Q: Can I wear sneakers to an Indian wedding?

A: Sneakers are not appropriate for an Indian wedding. Formal shoes or sandals would be a better option.

Conclusion of What Should an American Wear to an Indian Wedding

In conclusion, attending an Indian wedding can be a unique and exciting experience for Americans. It is essential to understand the cultural norms and expectations to ensure that your attire is appropriate. Traditional Indian outfits are the recommended attire for guests. It is important to choose an outfit that is colorful, comfortable, and complements your accessories. Don’t be afraid to ask for guidance if you are unsure about what to wear.
