Factors That Affect Hemming Cost

As mentioned earlier, the cost of hemming at Men's Wearhouse can vary depending on a few factors. Here are some of the main things that can affect the cost:

Type of Pants

The type of pants you have can affect the cost of hemming. For example, hemming a pair of jeans may be more difficult than hemming a pair of dress pants, so the cost may be slightly higher.

Material

The material of your pants can also affect the cost of hemming. Thicker materials, such as denim, may be more difficult to hem than thinner materials, which can drive up the cost.

Amount of Work Required

Finally, the amount of work required to hem your pants can also affect the cost. If your pants require a lot of alterations, such as tapering or adjusting the waistband, the cost may be higher than if the alterations are relatively simple.

How to Save Money on Hemming

If you're concerned about the cost of hemming, there are a few things you can do to save money. Here are some tips:

Look for Coupons

Negotiate

Learn to Hem Your Own Pants

If you're handy with a sewing machine, you can save money by hemming your own pants at home. There are plenty of tutorials available online that can teach you how to do this.
