Can You Wear Underwear During Acl Surgery

Written by Robby Aug 14, 2023 · 5 min read

ACL surgery can be a daunting experience, and many patients worry about what they can and cannot wear during the procedure. One of the most common questions that people ask is whether they can wear underwear during ACL surgery. In this post, we will explore the answer to this question, and provide you with some helpful information to make your surgery experience as comfortable as possible.

Undergoing surgery can be a nerve-wracking experience, and it's common for patients to have concerns about the procedure. One of the most common pain points is figuring out what they can and cannot wear. Patients worry about wearing clothes that might interfere with the surgery, or that might make them uncomfortable during the procedure.

The answer is yes, you can wear underwear during ACL surgery. However, it's important to wear loose-fitting, comfortable underwear that won't interfere with the surgery. Your surgeon will likely provide you with a hospital gown to wear during the procedure, so it's important to wear underwear that won't bunch up or create any unnecessary pressure points.

In summary, it is okay to wear underwear during ACL surgery, but it is important to wear loose-fitting, comfortable underwear that won't interfere with the surgery. Your surgeon will provide you with a hospital gown, so make sure you wear underwear that won't create any pressure points or bunch up during the procedure.
