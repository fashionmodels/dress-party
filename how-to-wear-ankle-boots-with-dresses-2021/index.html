How To Wear Ankle Boots With Dresses 2021 A Beginner s Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you struggle with finding the perfect shoes to go with your favorite dress? Have you considered wearing ankle boots with your dresses but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to wear ankle boots with dresses in 2021.

When it comes to pairing ankle boots with dresses, there are a few pain points to consider. Many people are unsure about which dress styles work best with ankle boots, how to choose the right pair of boots, and how to style them for different occasions. Not to worry, we've got you covered!

The key to wearing ankle boots with dresses is to find the right balance between the length and style of the dress and the height and color of the boots. Here are some tips to get you started:

Pair a Flowy Dress with Ankle Boots

One of the easiest ways to wear ankle boots with dresses is to pair a flowy or maxi dress with ankle boots. This creates a bohemian-chic look that is perfect for casual outings or outdoor events. Make sure the dress is long enough to cover the top of the boots and choose a pair of boots in a neutral color like black, brown, or beige.

Dress Up with Ankle Boots and a Midi Dress

If you're looking for a dressier outfit, try pairing ankle boots with a midi dress. This creates a sophisticated and stylish look that is perfect for a night out. Choose a dress in a bold color or pattern and pair it with ankle boots in a matching color. Add a statement necklace or earrings to complete the look.

Accessorize with Tights and Ankle Boots

When the weather turns cooler, you can still wear ankle boots with dresses by adding a pair of tights. This creates a layered look that is both stylish and practical. Choose a pair of opaque tights in a neutral color and pair them with ankle boots in a complementary color. This is a great way to transition your summer dresses into fall.

Play with Proportions

One of the key things to keep in mind when wearing ankle boots with dresses is to play with proportions. If you're wearing a shorter dress, choose ankle boots with a higher heel to elongate your legs. If you're wearing a longer dress, choose ankle boots with a lower heel or flat boots to balance out the proportions.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a formal dress?

A: Yes, you can! If you're going to a formal event, choose ankle boots in a dressy material like leather or suede and pair them with a long formal dress. Add some statement jewelry to complete the look.

Q: What kind of ankle boots should I wear with a mini dress?

A: For a mini dress, choose ankle boots with a higher heel to elongate your legs. Stick to ankle boots in a neutral color like black or beige to keep the focus on the dress.

Q: Can I wear ankle boots with a summer dress?

A: Absolutely! Ankle boots can be worn with summer dresses to create a boho or edgy look. Stick to ankle boots in a light color like tan or beige and pair them with a flowy summer dress for a casual daytime look.

Q: How do I choose the right color of ankle boots to wear with a dress?

A: When in doubt, stick to neutral colors like black, brown, or beige. These colors work well with a variety of dress styles and colors. If you want to add a pop of color to your outfit, choose ankle boots in a bold color like red or blue.

Conclusion of How to Wear Ankle Boots with Dresses 2021

Wearing ankle boots with dresses is a great way to add some edge and style to your outfit. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a variety of looks that are both stylish and practical. Remember to play with proportions, choose the right color of boots, and have fun with your outfit!
