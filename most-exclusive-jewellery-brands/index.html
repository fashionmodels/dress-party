Tiffany & Co.

One of the most recognizable names in luxury jewellery, Tiffany & Co. has been creating iconic pieces since 1837. Known for their signature blue boxes and stunning diamond designs, Tiffany & Co. remains a go-to for engagement rings and special occasion jewellery.

Cartier

Founded in Paris in 1847, Cartier is a brand that has become synonymous with luxury and refinement. Known for their iconic Love Bracelets and Tank watches, Cartier has a wide range of stunning jewellery designs that are crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail.

My personal experience with Cartier was when I purchased a Love Bracelet for my wife's birthday. The bracelet was beautifully crafted with the signature screw motif and was a true statement piece that has become a staple in her jewellery collection.

Bulgari

Founded in Rome in 1884, Bulgari has become one of the most exclusive jewellery brands in the world. Known for their bold designs and use of colourful gemstones, Bulgari creates pieces that are both classic and daring.

From statement necklaces to intricate watches, Bulgari's attention to detail and quality materials make their pieces truly special. If you're looking for a unique piece that will stand the test of time, Bulgari is a brand worth exploring.

Harry Winston

When it comes to diamonds, Harry Winston is the ultimate authority. Known as the "King of Diamonds," Winston's designs are coveted by collectors and celebrities alike.

My personal experience with Harry Winston was when I had the opportunity to try on a stunning diamond necklace at one of their boutiques. The sparkle and brilliance of the diamonds were truly awe-inspiring, and it's no wonder that Harry Winston remains one of the most exclusive jewellery brands in the world.
