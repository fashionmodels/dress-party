Materials for Best Budget Formal Shoes in India

The material of your formal shoes plays a crucial role in both its comfort and durability. The following are some of the most common materials used for formal shoes:

Genuine Leather: Genuine leather is the most popular material used for formal shoes. It provides both comfort and durability, making it a popular choice among customers.

Synthetic Leather: Synthetic leather is a more affordable alternative to genuine leather. While it may not be as durable, it is still a popular choice among customers who are on a budget.

Suede: Suede is a softer material that is perfect for casual formal shoes. While it may not be as durable as leather, it is still a popular choice among customers who are looking for a more relaxed look.
