Top Readymade Garment Brands In India

Written by Frank May 27, 2023 · 5 min read

India has a rich textile and garment industry that caters to the needs of millions of people across the country. However, finding the right readymade garment brand can be a daunting task for many, especially with the plethora of options available in the market. It is important to choose the right brand that offers quality, affordability, and fashion-forward designs.

If you are looking for the top readymade garment brands in India, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will take a detailed look at the most popular brands in the country and what makes them stand out from the rest.

Here are some of the best readymade garment brands in India:

1. Allen Solly

Allen Solly is a popular readymade garment brand in India that offers a wide range of clothing options for men, women, and children. The brand is known for its trendy designs, high-quality fabrics, and affordable pricing. With a focus on innovation and style, Allen Solly has become a household name in the country.

2. Peter England

Peter England is another top readymade garment brand in India that is known for its classic designs and impeccable quality. The brand offers a variety of clothing options for men, including formal wear, casual wear, and ethnic wear. With a strong presence across the country, Peter England is a go-to brand for many men.

3. Biba

Biba is a popular readymade garment brand in India that offers a wide range of ethnic wear for women. The brand is known for its vibrant designs, high-quality fabrics, and affordable pricing. With a focus on comfort and style, Biba has become a favorite among women across the country.

4. Levi's

Levi's is a global brand that has a strong presence in India. The brand offers a range of clothing options for men and women, including jeans, jackets, and t-shirts. With a focus on quality and style, Levi's has become a popular choice for many in the country.

Why are these brands popular in India?

The top readymade garment brands in India are popular for a variety of reasons. Firstly, these brands offer high-quality clothing options that are made from the finest fabrics. Secondly, they offer trendy designs that cater to the fashion needs of people across the country. Lastly, these brands offer affordable pricing, making them accessible to a wide range of audiences.

How to choose the right readymade garment brand?

Choosing the right readymade garment brand in India can be a daunting task, given the plethora of options available in the market. Here are some tips to help you choose the right brand:

1. Look for brands that offer high-quality fabrics and trendy designs.

2. Consider your budget and look for brands that offer affordable pricing.

3. Read reviews and ratings of the brand before making a purchase.

5. W for Woman

W for Woman is a popular readymade garment brand in India that offers a wide range of ethnic wear for women. The brand is known for its contemporary designs, high-quality fabrics, and affordable pricing. With a focus on comfort and style, W for Woman has become a favorite among women across the country.

6. Raymond

Raymond is a popular readymade garment brand in India that is known for its classic designs and impeccable quality. The brand offers a variety of clothing options for men, including formal wear, casual wear, and ethnic wear. With a strong presence across the country, Raymond is a go-to brand for many men.

What are the latest trends in readymade garments in India?

The latest trends in readymade garments in India include fusion wear, sustainable fashion, and athleisure wear. Fusion wear combines traditional Indian clothing with modern designs, while sustainable fashion focuses on creating eco-friendly clothing options. Athleisure wear combines athletic and leisure wear to create comfortable and stylish clothing options.

7. Fabindia

Fabindia is a popular readymade garment brand in India that offers a wide range of ethnic wear for men and women. The brand is known for its handcrafted designs, high-quality fabrics, and sustainable practices. With a focus on preserving traditional Indian crafts, Fabindia has become a favorite among people who value sustainability and craftsmanship.
