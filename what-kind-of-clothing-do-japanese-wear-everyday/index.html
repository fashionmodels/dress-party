Traditional Clothing

Traditional clothing in Japan is known as kimono. The kimono is a long, flowing robe that is usually made of silk, cotton, or polyester. It is worn by both men and women and is often associated with formal events such as weddings and funerals. Kimonos come in a variety of colors and designs, each with its own meaning and symbolism.

Streetwear

Streetwear is a popular clothing style in Japan, especially among young people. It is characterized by its casual and comfortable look. Streetwear includes items such as sneakers, t-shirts, and hoodies. Popular streetwear brands in Japan include A Bathing Ape, Comme des Garcons, and Undercover.

Casual Clothing

Casual clothing in Japan is similar to what you might see in other countries. It includes items such as jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers. However, the Japanese have a unique way of styling these items. Business Attire

Business attire in Japan is formal and conservative. Men typically wear suits and ties, while women wear conservative dresses or suits. The color black is often preferred for business attire as it is considered to be a serious and professional color.
