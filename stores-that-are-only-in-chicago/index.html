1. The Tie Bar

The Tie Bar is a Chicago-based company that sells high-quality ties and accessories for men. The company was founded in 2004, and since then, it has become one of the most popular stores in the city. The Tie Bar has a wide range of products, including bow ties, pocket squares, and cufflinks.

I personally love The Tie Bar because of their exceptional customer service. The staff is always willing to help you find the perfect tie for any occasion. The Tie Bar is a must-visit store if you're looking for unique and stylish accessories for men.

2. Garrett Popcorn Shops

Garrett Popcorn Shops is a Chicago institution, selling delicious popcorn since 1949. The company's signature flavor is the Garrett Mix, which is a combination of caramel and cheese popcorn. Garrett Popcorn Shops has several locations throughout the city, making it easy to get your popcorn fix.

I remember visiting Garrett Popcorn Shops for the first time and being blown away by the delicious smell of popcorn. The store has a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, and the staff is always friendly and helpful. If you're a popcorn lover, you won't want to miss this iconic Chicago store.

3. Alcala's Western Wear

Alcala's Western Wear is a family-owned store that specializes in cowboy boots, hats, and other western wear. The store has been around since 1972 and is located in the heart of Chicago's Pilsen neighborhood.

What I love about Alcala's Western Wear is the authentic western feel of the store. The staff is knowledgeable about western wear and can help you find the perfect boots or hat for any occasion. If you're looking for a unique and authentic shopping experience, be sure to check out Alcala's Western Wear.

4. Uncle Fun

Uncle Fun is a quirky and fun store that sells a variety of unique items, including toys, games, and gag gifts. The store has been around since 1989 and is located in the Lakeview neighborhood.

What makes Uncle Fun so special is the store's playful and whimsical atmosphere. You'll find items here that you won't find anywhere else, making it the perfect place to shop for a gift or a fun souvenir. Uncle Fun is a must-visit store if you're looking for a unique shopping experience.
