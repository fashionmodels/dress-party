Top Shirt Brands In India 2021

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wasting your time and money on shirts that don't fit well or don't last long? Do you want to know the top shirt brands in India that offer quality, durability, and style? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best shirt brands in India for 2021.

When it comes to buying shirts, there are many factors to consider, such as size, material, style, and brand reputation. Choosing the wrong brand can lead to discomfort, disappointment, and a waste of money. That's why it's essential to know the top shirt brands in India that offer the best value for your money.

The target of this article is to help you make an informed decision when buying shirts by providing a list of the top shirt brands in India for 2021.

To summarize, the main points of this article include a list of top shirt brands in India, their target audience, quality, style, and price range. We will also share personal experiences and provide in-depth explanations of each brand's features and benefits.

Allen Solly

Allen Solly is a popular shirt brand in India that offers a wide range of stylish and comfortable shirts for men and women. Their target audience is young professionals who want to look sharp and feel comfortable at work or social events.

Personally, I have been a fan of Allen Solly shirts for a long time, as they are made of high-quality cotton that feels soft and breathable on the skin. Their shirts come in various colors, patterns, and sizes, making it easy to find the perfect fit for any occasion.

Peter England

Peter England is another top shirt brand in India that caters to men of all ages and styles. Their shirts are known for their durability, affordability, and classic design, making them a popular choice for everyday wear.

I have owned several Peter England shirts over the years, and I can attest to their quality and comfort. The shirts are made of 100% cotton and are available in various sizes and colors, ranging from formal to casual wear.

Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a premium shirt brand in India that offers a range of stylish and sophisticated shirts for men and women. Their target audience is fashion-conscious individuals who want to make a statement with their clothing.

If you're looking for a high-end shirt brand that offers exceptional quality and style, Van Heusen is an excellent choice. Their shirts are made of premium fabrics, such as Egyptian cotton and linen, and come in various designs, including stripes, solids, and prints.

How to Choose the Right Shirt Brand

When choosing a shirt brand, consider the following factors:

  • Quality of material and stitching
  • Fit and comfort
  • Style and design
  • Price and value for money
  • Brand reputation and customer reviews

By considering these factors, you can make an informed decision when buying a shirt and avoid wasting your time and money on low-quality brands.

Levi's

Levi's is a well-known brand in India that offers a range of denim and non-denim shirts for men and women. Their shirts are known for their durability, style, and versatility, making them a popular choice for casual wear.

I have owned several Levi's shirts over the years, and I can attest to their comfort and quality. The shirts are made of high-quality cotton and come in various sizes and colors, making it easy to find the perfect fit for any occasion.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the price range of top shirt brands in India?

A: The price range of top shirt brands in India varies from affordable to premium, depending on the brand and the quality of the shirt. You can find good quality shirts for as low as Rs. 500, while premium brands may cost upwards of Rs. 5000.

Q: What is the most popular shirt brand in India?

A: The most popular shirt brand in India varies depending on the target audience and the region. However, some of the most well-known brands include Allen Solly, Peter England, Van Heusen, and Levi's.

Q: Are there any eco-friendly shirt brands in India?

A: Yes, there are several eco-friendly shirt brands in India that use sustainable materials and ethical production practices. Some of these brands include No Nasties, Bhu:sattva, and Tula.

Q: Can I buy top shirt brands in India online?

A: Yes, most top shirt brands in India are available online through their official websites or e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, or Myntra.

Conclusion of Top Shirt Brands in India 2021

Choosing the right shirt brand can make a significant difference in your comfort, style, and confidence. By considering the top shirt brands in India for 2021 and their features and benefits, you can make an informed decision and enjoy the best value for your money.
