Cool Thrift Stores In Columbus A Guide To Finding Hidden Gems

Written by Petter Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of sifting through the same old racks at your local mall? Do you want to add unique pieces to your wardrobe without breaking the bank? If so, then it's time to explore the world of thrift shopping. However, finding cool thrift stores in Columbus can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's hard to know where to start.

If you're on a budget or looking to add some unique pieces to your wardrobe, then thrift shopping is the way to go. Cool thrift stores in Columbus offer a variety of clothing and accessories at affordable prices. You can find vintage pieces, designer items, and one-of-a-kind treasures that you can't find anywhere else. Plus, thrift shopping is eco-friendly and sustainable, making it a win-win for both your wallet and the planet.

Now that you know why thrift shopping is worth your time, let's dive into the best cool thrift stores in Columbus. Here are some hidden gems in the city that you don't want to miss:

1. Rag-O-Rama

Rag-O-Rama is a Columbus staple when it comes to thrift shopping. With a wide selection of clothing and accessories for men and women, this store has something for everyone. What sets Rag-O-Rama apart is its unique inventory and affordable prices. You can find everything from vintage band tees to designer handbags at a fraction of the cost.

2. Volunteers of America Thrift Store

Volunteers of America Thrift Store is a great place to find hidden gems. This store has a large selection of clothing, furniture, and home decor at affordable prices. What's unique about this thrift store is that it supports a good cause. All proceeds go to Volunteers of America, a non-profit organization that helps those in need.

3. Second Chance Consignment Boutique

If you're looking for high-end designer pieces at a fraction of the cost, then Second Chance Consignment Boutique is the place to go. This store specializes in designer clothing, handbags, and accessories for men and women. You can find brands like Chanel, Gucci, and Louis Vuitton at prices you won't believe.

4. Goodwill Columbus Outlet Store

The Goodwill Columbus Outlet Store is a treasure trove for thrift shoppers. This store offers a unique shopping experience where you can buy items by the pound. You can find clothing, household items, and even electronics at rock-bottom prices. This store is perfect if you're looking for a bargain and a fun shopping experience.

5. Out of the Closet

Out of the Closet is a thrift store with a cause. This store supports the AIDS Healthcare Foundation, and all proceeds go to HIV/AIDS research and treatment. You can find a variety of clothing, accessories, and home decor items at affordable prices. Plus, shopping at Out of the Closet is a great way to support a good cause.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I bring with me when thrift shopping?

A: It's always a good idea to bring cash, as some thrift stores don't accept credit cards. Also, wear comfortable clothing and shoes, as you'll be doing a lot of walking and trying on clothes.

Q: How can I find the best deals at thrift stores?

A: Look for sales and discounts, and don't be afraid to negotiate prices. Thrift stores are often willing to negotiate if you're buying multiple items.

Q: What should I look for when thrift shopping?

A: Look for unique pieces that you won't find anywhere else. Check for quality and make sure that the items are in good condition. Also, be open to trying on different styles and sizes.

Q: How often should I go thrift shopping?

A: It depends on your budget and how often new items are added to the store's inventory. Some thrift stores have new items daily, while others have weekly or monthly rotations.

Conclusion of Cool Thrift Stores in Columbus

In conclusion, cool thrift stores in Columbus offer a unique shopping experience that you won't find at your local mall. From vintage pieces to high-end designer items, these stores have something for everyone. Not only is thrift shopping affordable and sustainable, but it's also a fun way to explore the city and find hidden gems. So, grab your cash and comfortable shoes and start exploring the best cool thrift stores in Columbus.
