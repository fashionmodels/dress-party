Is It Ok To Wear Underwear Under Compression Shorts

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Compression shorts have become a popular clothing item for athletes and fitness enthusiasts. They provide support, reduce muscle fatigue, and increase blood flow to the muscles. However, a common question that arises is whether it is okay to wear underwear under compression shorts. In this article, we will answer this question and provide you with all the information you need to know about wearing underwear under compression shorts.

One of the main pain points related to wearing underwear under compression shorts is the potential for discomfort and chafing. Compression shorts are designed to fit tightly against the skin, and adding an extra layer of clothing can cause rubbing and irritation. This can make it difficult to focus on your workout or sport, and can even lead to skin irritation or infection in the long run.

The simple answer to the question of whether you can wear underwear under compression shorts is no, you should not. Compression shorts are designed to be worn without underwear, and doing so can compromise their effectiveness. Wearing underwear underneath can also cause bunching and shifting, which can be distracting and uncomfortable.

In summary, if you are wearing compression shorts, it is best to go commando and not wear any underwear. This will ensure that you get the full benefits of the compression technology and avoid any discomfort or irritation. However, if you are uncomfortable going without underwear, you can look for compression shorts with built-in underwear or choose underwear made from moisture-wicking and breathable fabric.

Why You Shouldn't Wear Underwear Under Compression Shorts

As mentioned earlier, wearing underwear under compression shorts can cause discomfort and chafing. It can also compromise the effectiveness of the compression technology, which is designed to improve blood flow and reduce muscle fatigue. When you wear underwear underneath, it can create additional pressure and friction, which can interfere with the compression process. Additionally, underwear can bunch up and shift, which can be distracting and uncomfortable during your workout or sport.

Personally, I used to wear underwear under my compression shorts, but I found that it caused too much discomfort and irritation. Once I switched to going commando, I found that my compression shorts were much more effective and that I was able to focus better on my workout.

What to Look for in Compression Shorts

If you are uncomfortable going without underwear, there are still options available to you. Look for compression shorts that come with built-in underwear or choose underwear made from moisture-wicking and breathable material. This will help to prevent moisture buildup and reduce the risk of chafing and irritation.

Benefits of Compression Shorts

Compression shorts have become popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts for a reason. They offer a range of benefits, including improved blood flow, reduced muscle fatigue, and increased support. Compression technology works by applying pressure to the muscles, which helps to improve blood flow and reduce the buildup of lactic acid. This can help to reduce muscle soreness and fatigue, and improve overall performance.

How to Choose the Right Compression Shorts

When choosing compression shorts, it is important to consider the level of compression you need. If you are looking for mild compression, you may be able to get away with a lighter weight fabric. However, if you need more support, you may want to look for a heavier weight fabric with more compression. It is also important to choose shorts that fit properly and are comfortable to wear. Look for shorts that are made from moisture-wicking and breathable material to reduce the risk of chafing and irritation.

Personal Experience

As someone who has been wearing compression shorts for years, I can attest to the fact that going commando is the way to go. While it may feel uncomfortable at first, you will quickly get used to it and find that it provides a much better overall experience. I have found that my compression shorts are more effective and that I am able to focus better on my workout when I go without underwear.
