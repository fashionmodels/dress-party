Fabric Spark

One of the most popular online retailers for quilting fabric in Canada is Fabric Spark. Based in Toronto, they offer a wide selection of high-quality, designer fabrics from some of the top brands in the industry. Their website is easy to navigate and includes helpful features such as fabric swatches and a project gallery to inspire your next creation.

Mad About Patchwork

Another great option is Mad About Patchwork, based out of Stittsville, Ontario. They offer a diverse selection of fabrics, including modern and contemporary prints, as well as traditional designs. They also offer a variety of precut fabrics and quilting kits to make your project even easier.

Thread Count Fabrics

Thread Count Fabrics is a newer online retailer based in Woodstock, Ontario. They specialize in modern and contemporary fabrics, with a focus on sustainable and eco-friendly materials. They also offer curated fabric bundles and subscription boxes for those who want a little extra inspiration.
