Types of Men's Ethnic Wear in Pakistan

There are several types of men's ethnic wear in Pakistan, including sherwanis, kurtas, waistcoats, and shalwar kameez. Sherwanis are a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and paired with fitted pants. Kurtas are a long shirt that is paired with pajama or shalwar. Waistcoats are a sleeveless jacket that is worn over a kurta or shirt. Shalwar kameez is a traditional outfit that consists of a long shirt and loose-fitted pants.
