The Cost of Food and Treats for a Boxer Puppy

Boxers are a medium-sized breed and require a diet that is high in protein and nutrients. The cost of food can vary depending on the brand and quality. On average, you can expect to spend around $50 to $100 per month on food and treats for your boxer puppy.

Vet visits are an important part of keeping your boxer puppy healthy. Your puppy will need regular check-ups, vaccinations, and potentially spaying or neutering. The cost of these visits can vary depending on your location and the vet you choose. On average, you can expect to spend around $200 to $500 per year on vet visits.
