Types Of Dickies A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you work in a profession that requires you to wear a uniform, you know how important it is to have the right clothing. One essential item for many professionals is a Dickie. It's a type of clothing accessory that can make your workday more comfortable and efficient. However, with so many different types of Dickies available, it can be challenging to know which one is right for you.

In this blog post, we'll discuss the different types of Dickies and how to choose the right one for your needs.

Before we dive into the different types of Dickies, let's first define what a Dickie is. A Dickie is a type of clothing accessory that is worn under a uniform or work attire. It's designed to give the appearance of an undershirt without the extra layer of clothing. Dickies come in various materials, colors, and styles, making them a versatile option for many professions.

In this post, we'll go over the most common types of Dickies, including turtleneck, mock turtleneck, crew neck, and V-neck, and explain their differences. We'll also discuss the materials used to make Dickies and their advantages.

Turtleneck Dickies

A turtleneck Dickie is a type of neck covering that extends up to the chin. It's perfect for professionals who work in colder environments or are required to wear a tie. The turtleneck Dickie provides extra warmth while still keeping a professional appearance. It's also a great option for those who prefer a more conservative look.

I used to work in a hospital, and during the winter months, it was freezing. The turtleneck Dickie was a lifesaver and kept me warm throughout the day. I also liked how it looked under my scrubs.

Mock Turtleneck Dickies

A mock turtleneck Dickie is similar to a turtleneck but doesn't extend as high. It's a great option for those who want the warmth of a turtleneck but prefer a more relaxed fit. The mock turtleneck Dickie is also perfect for individuals who find high-necked clothing uncomfortable.

When I worked in retail, I had to wear a uniform that included a mock turtleneck Dickie. At first, I was hesitant to wear it because I didn't like the feeling of high-necked clothing. However, I was surprised at how comfortable it was, and it kept me warm during the colder months.

Crew Neck Dickies

A crew neck Dickie is a type of neck covering that sits at the base of the neck. It's a versatile option that's perfect for individuals who don't want a high or tight neck covering. The crew neck Dickie is also an excellent choice for those who want a more relaxed fit.

When I worked in a restaurant, I had to wear a uniform that included a crew neck Dickie. It was comfortable to wear, and I liked how it looked under my uniform shirt.

V-Neck Dickies

A V-neck Dickie is a type of neck covering that dips down in a V shape. It's perfect for individuals who want a more stylish look or prefer to wear a tie. The V-neck Dickie is also an excellent option for those who want a relaxed fit and a more open neck covering.

When I worked in a law firm, I had to wear a uniform that included a V-neck Dickie. It was comfortable to wear and gave my uniform a more professional look.

Material

Dickies are made from various materials, including cotton, polyester, and spandex. Cotton is a popular material for Dickies because it's breathable, comfortable, and easy to care for. Polyester is another popular material because it's durable, moisture-wicking, and resistant to wrinkles. Spandex is often added to Dickies to provide additional stretch and flexibility.

How to Choose the Right Dickie

When choosing a Dickie, consider your profession, work environment, and personal preferences. If you work in a colder environment, consider a turtleneck or mock turtleneck Dickie. If you prefer a more relaxed fit, consider a crew neck or V-neck Dickie. Also, consider the material and choose one that's comfortable and easy to care for.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a Dickie as an undershirt?

A: Yes, Dickies are designed to be worn under a uniform or work attire as an undershirt.

Q: Are Dickies only available in white?

A: No, Dickies come in various colors and styles.

Q: Can I wear a Dickie without a uniform?

A: Yes, Dickies can be worn as a regular shirt or under a sweater or jacket.

Q: Are Dickies only for men?

A: No, Dickies are available for both men and women.

Conclusion of Types of Dickies

Choosing the right Dickie for your profession and personal preferences can make a significant difference in your workday. Whether you prefer a turtleneck, mock turtleneck, crew neck, or V-neck, there's a Dickie out there that's perfect for you. Consider the material, color, and style when choosing a Dickie and enjoy the added comfort and warmth it provides.
