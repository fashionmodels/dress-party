Discover The Best Men s Consignment Stores In Toronto A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 28, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on clothes? Do you want to look stylish without breaking the bank? Look no further than men's consignment stores in Toronto. These stores offer high-quality, gently-used clothing at a fraction of the price of new items.

If you're like most people, you probably don't have an unlimited budget for clothing. Yet, you still want to look good and feel confident in your clothes. Men's consignment stores in Toronto offer a solution to this problem. They allow you to buy designer and high-end clothing without paying full price.

So, what exactly is a men's consignment store? Essentially, it's a store that sells secondhand clothing on behalf of the original owner. The store takes a percentage of the sale price as commission, and the owner receives the rest. This arrangement benefits both parties. The store gets a constant supply of high-quality, unique items, while the owner gets to make some extra cash and clear out their closet.

In this article, we'll explore the best men's consignment stores in Toronto and what makes them stand out. We'll also provide tips for shopping at these stores and answer some common questions about the consignment process.

Black Market Clothing

Located in the heart of Kensington Market, Black Market Clothing is a staple in the Toronto consignment scene. They specialize in vintage clothing and accessories, but also carry a selection of contemporary pieces. The store has a welcoming, laid-back atmosphere and is staffed by friendly, knowledgeable employees.

I recently visited Black Market Clothing and was blown away by the selection. They had racks upon racks of unique, one-of-a-kind items that you won't find anywhere else. I ended up leaving with a vintage leather jacket and a pair of designer boots, both at a fraction of their original cost.

Common Sort

With three locations across Toronto, Common Sort is a go-to destination for men's consignment clothing. They carry a wide range of brands, from high-end designer labels to more affordable contemporary pieces. The store is well-organized, making it easy to find what you're looking for.

I've shopped at Common Sort many times and have always been impressed by the quality of their items. They have a rigorous selection process, so you can trust that everything in the store is in excellent condition. Plus, their prices are very reasonable, especially considering the quality of the clothing.

The Future of Men's Fashion

As the fashion industry becomes more focused on sustainability and reducing waste, men's consignment stores are poised to become even more popular. By buying secondhand clothing, you're not only saving money but also helping to reduce the environmental impact of the fashion industry.

Plus, shopping at men's consignment stores is a great way to support local businesses. Many of these stores are independently owned and operated, and they rely on the support of their community to stay in business.

How to Shop at Men's Consignment Stores

If you're new to shopping at men's consignment stores, here are some tips to get you started:

1. Come prepared: Wear comfortable clothing and shoes that are easy to take on and off. You'll likely be trying on a lot of clothes, so you want to be comfortable and efficient.

2. Look for quality: Check the clothing carefully for any signs of wear or damage. Make sure the seams are intact, the zippers work properly, and there are no stains or holes.

3. Know your size: Men's clothing sizes can vary widely depending on the brand and the era. Check the store's sizing chart and don't be afraid to ask for help from the staff.

4. Be patient: Consignment shopping requires a bit of patience and persistence. You may have to sift through a lot of items before you find something you love, but the thrill of the hunt is part of the fun.

The Bottom Line

Men's consignment stores in Toronto offer a unique and affordable way to update your wardrobe. Whether you're looking for vintage pieces, designer labels, or everyday basics, these stores have something for everyone. By shopping at consignment stores, you're not only saving money but also supporting local businesses and reducing waste. So, what are you waiting for? Head to one of these great stores and start shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: How do I sell my clothes at a men's consignment store?

A: Each store may have a slightly different process, but in general, you'll need to bring your items to the store for evaluation. The staff will look at the items and determine if they meet the store's criteria for quality and style. If they do, the store will set a price and take a percentage of the sale price as commission.

Q: Are consignment stores only for high-end designer clothing?

A: No, consignment stores carry a wide range of brands and styles. While some stores may specialize in high-end designer labels, others carry more affordable contemporary clothing.

Q: Are the prices at consignment stores negotiable?

A: It depends on the store. Some stores may be willing to negotiate on price, especially if an item has been in the store for a while. However, others may have a firm policy of no negotiations.

Q: Can I return items I buy at a consignment store?

A: It depends on the store's policy. Some stores may allow returns within a certain timeframe, while others have a strict no-return policy. Be sure to check the store's policy before making a purchase.

Conclusion of Men's Consignment Stores Toronto

Men's consignment stores in Toronto offer a unique shopping experience that combines affordability, sustainability, and style. Whether you're looking for vintage pieces or contemporary clothing, these stores have something for everyone. By shopping at consignment stores, you're not only saving money but also supporting local businesses and reducing waste. So, next time you're in need of a wardrobe update, head to one of these great stores and start shopping!
