The History of Vintage Round Eyeglass Frames

The history of eyeglasses dates back to ancient times, but the concept of using lenses to correct vision didn't become widespread until the Renaissance era. As eyewear evolved over the centuries, different styles and materials came into fashion, including metal, plastic, and even ivory.

One of the most iconic styles of eyewear is the round frame, which has been popular since the 1800s. In the mid-20th century, round frames gained even more popularity thanks to the rise of iconic figures like John Lennon and Harry Potter, who made them a symbol of individuality and creativity.

Today, vintage round eyeglass frames are experiencing a resurgence in popularity, thanks to their unique style and timeless appeal.
