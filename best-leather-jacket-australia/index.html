Best Leather Jacket Australia A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Fit

Written by Oliver Aug 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you in the market for a new leather jacket but unsure of where to start your search? Look no further than the best leather jacket Australia has to offer. With its high-quality materials and stylish designs, a leather jacket is a must-have addition to any wardrobe. However, finding the perfect fit can be a daunting task. In this guide, we will explore the best leather jacket Australia has to offer and provide tips on how to find the one that suits you best.

When it comes to purchasing a leather jacket, there are a few pain points to consider. Firstly, the price can be a concern as a high-quality leather jacket can be a significant investment. Additionally, finding the right style and fit can be a challenge as there are many options to choose from. Finally, understanding the different types of leather and their qualities can be overwhelming.

The target of this guide is to provide you with the necessary information to confidently purchase the best leather jacket Australia has to offer. From understanding the different types of leather to finding the right size, we have got you covered.

Why Choose a Leather Jacket?

Before diving into how to choose the best leather jacket Australia has to offer, it is essential to understand why a leather jacket is a must-have item in any wardrobe. A good leather jacket is an investment piece that will last for years to come. It is versatile and can be worn in a variety of settings, from casual outings to dressed-up events. Additionally, a leather jacket can add a touch of edginess to any outfit while still remaining classic and timeless.

Personally, I fell in love with leather jackets after purchasing my first one several years ago. It quickly became my go-to jacket for any occasion, and I have since added several more to my collection. They are durable, stylish, and elevate any outfit.

Understanding Different Types of Leather

When it comes to choosing the best leather jacket Australia has to offer, it is essential to understand the different types of leather and their qualities. The most common types of leather used in jackets are cowhide, sheepskin, and lambskin.

Cowhide is the most durable and rugged of the three, making it an excellent choice for a classic, long-lasting jacket. Sheepskin is softer and lighter, making it ideal for a more fitted, feminine look. Finally, lambskin is the softest and lightest of the three, providing a sleek and modern look.

When selecting the type of leather for your jacket, consider the look you are going for and the level of durability you require. Cowhide is an excellent choice for a classic, long-lasting jacket, while lamb or sheepskin is ideal for a more fashion-forward, fitted look.

Finding the Right Fit

One of the most critical factors in choosing the best leather jacket Australia has to offer is finding the right fit. A well-fitted jacket should be snug in the shoulders and chest but still allow for movement in the arms. Additionally, the length should hit just above the hips for a flattering silhouette.

When trying on jackets, be sure to wear a lightweight shirt or sweater to ensure a proper fit. Leather jackets should fit snugly, but not be too tight. Additionally, be sure to move around in the jacket to ensure it allows for proper movement.

Caring for Your Leather Jacket

Once you have found the perfect leather jacket, it is essential to take proper care of it to ensure it lasts for years to come. Leather should be cleaned and conditioned regularly to prevent cracking and drying out. Additionally, be sure to avoid getting the jacket wet, as water can damage the leather.

Where to Buy the Best Leather Jacket Australia Has to Offer

When it comes to purchasing the best leather jacket Australia has to offer, there are a few key retailers to consider. Some of the most popular options include David Jones, Country Road, and MJ Bale. Additionally, there are many online retailers, such as The Iconic and ASOS, that offer a wide selection of leather jackets at various price points.

Conclusion of Best Leather Jacket Australia

When it comes to finding the best leather jacket Australia has to offer, it is essential to consider the style, fit, and type of leather. With these factors in mind, you can confidently select a jacket that will last for years to come and elevate any outfit. Remember to take proper care of your leather jacket to ensure it remains in top condition. Happy shopping!

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a leather jacket is high-quality?

A: Look for jackets made from high-quality leather, such as full-grain cowhide or lambskin. Additionally, check the stitching and hardware for durability and quality.

Q: How much should I expect to spend on a leather jacket?

A: A high-quality leather jacket can range from $500 to $1000 or more. However, there are options available at lower price points, but be sure to check the quality of the leather and construction before purchasing.

Q: Can I wear a leather jacket in different seasons?

A: Yes! Leather jackets are versatile and can be worn in different seasons. In colder months, layer a sweater or scarf underneath for added warmth, while in warmer months, pair with a lightweight shirt or dress.

Q: How should I store my leather jacket?

A: Leather jackets should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid hanging the jacket for extended periods as it can cause stretching and misshaping. Consider investing in a garment bag or cover to protect the jacket from dust and dirt.
