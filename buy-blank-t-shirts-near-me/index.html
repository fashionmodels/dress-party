FAQs on Buying Blank T-Shirts Near Me

Q1. What is the best material for blank t-shirts?

A1. The best material for blank t-shirts is 100% cotton or a cotton/polyester blend. These materials are soft, durable, and easy to print on.

Q2. How much do blank t-shirts cost?

A2. The cost of blank t-shirts varies depending on the quality, brand, and quantity. On average, a good quality blank t-shirt can cost between $5-$15.

Q3. Can I print on blank t-shirts myself?

A3. Yes, you can. Blank t-shirts are ideal for DIY projects, and you can easily print on them using a heat press or screen printing method.

Q4. How do I know what size to buy?

A4. It's essential to check the sizing chart before buying. Most t-shirts come in standard sizes, but some brands may have different measurements. Make sure to measure yourself and compare it with the sizing chart before buying.
