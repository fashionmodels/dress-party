FAQs about Online Indian Clothing Stores in USA

Q: How do I know if an online Indian clothing store is reliable?

A: Look for online reviews from previous customers, check the store’s policies for returns and exchanges, and verify their contact information and payment options. Avoid stores that have suspiciously low prices, poor quality images, or no customer support.

Q: How can I ensure proper fit when shopping for Indian clothing online?

A: Check the store’s sizing chart and measurements carefully, and compare them to your own measurements. Consider ordering a sample piece or swatch, or contacting the store’s customer service for guidance. Allow for some margin of error, as certain fabrics and styles may fit differently.

Q: How long does it take for online Indian clothing stores to deliver to the USA?

A: Delivery times vary depending on the store’s location, shipping options, and customs clearance. Some stores offer expedited shipping for an additional fee, while others may take several weeks to deliver. Check the store’s shipping policies and estimated delivery times before placing your order.

Q: What are some popular styles of Indian clothing for women?

A: Some popular styles of Indian clothing for women include sarees, salwar kameez, lehenga choli, anarkali suits, and kurtis. These styles vary in length, fabric, design, and occasion, and can be customized to suit your preferences.
