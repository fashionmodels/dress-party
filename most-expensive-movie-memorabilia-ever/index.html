The Maltese Falcon statue is one of the most iconic props in movie history. It was used in the 1941 film of the same name, starring Humphrey Bogart. The statue sold at auction in 2013 for a whopping $4.1 million, making it the most expensive movie prop ever sold.

As a film noir classic, The Maltese Falcon has become a staple in American cinema. The statue has become a symbol of the film and is highly sought after by collectors.
